## Narrative Arc

### 起点 (Starting Point)
- **裁定取引の加速** (arbitrage acceleration) — LLMs exploit the gap between surface-quality proxies and actual quality, creating an economic incentive cascade
- **情報流通量の加速** (information flow volume acceleration) — sheer volume overwhelms verification capacity; simulacra proliferate faster than they can be checked
- Together these drive Baudrillard's "precession of simulacra" in the AI era

### 中間展開①: Baumolのコスト病 (Baumol's Cost Disease)
- **Key contrast**: 労働集約的なサービス業（検証・行動）vs 技術革新の進む製造業（読解/記述/統合）
- **Mechanism**: サービス価格高騰 → 高コストな検証・行動 vs 自動化可能な読み/書き/統合 → 検証・行動の価値高騰
- User feedback: ~"Baumol's cost disease section is tricky with too much inferential leap — needs more careful, step-by-step explanation"~

### 中間展開②: Insight Porn
- Targets the same neural circuits as SNS addiction
- **Critical mechanism**: 変動報酬 (variable reward) × 極低努力コスト (extremely low effort cost) → 射倖心 (gambling-like urge) 
- Insight Porn = LLM output that *feels* like insight but costs nothing to consume or produce
- User feedback: ~"variable reward + low effort cost → gambling urge mechanism is what should be problematized"~

### 中間展開③: 検証のゲーデル的不完全性 / Wikiパラドックス
- Problem identification alone → feels pedantic/not important
- **Must include**: some pragmatic stance/position — how do we verify in a system that is Gödel-incomplete?
- User feedback: ~"just pointing out problems is pedantic; include pragmatic insight"~

### 着地点 (Landing Point)
- 構造的問題点を上げることは容易だが、それでもそれを必要とする**争い難い外圧**が存在する
- 問題が多くある中で**どう付き合っていくべきか**に関して重要論点を配置する設計
- Not: "here are all the problems, they're bad" — but: "given these forces are real and inescapable, what posture do we adopt?"

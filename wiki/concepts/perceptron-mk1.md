# Perceptron Mk1

## Overview
Perceptron Mk1 is a physical AI model purpose-built for video understanding and embodied reasoning, launched May 12, 2026 by [[entities/perceptron-ai]]. It achieves competitive performance with frontier models (Google, Anthropic, OpenAI, Qwen) while operating at the cost of lightweight alternatives.

## Architecture
Built to close the loop between digital intelligence and physical action. Designed for video understanding and spatial reasoning across diverse real-world domains.

## Benchmark Performance
Competitive scores across image, video, and spatial-reasoning benchmarks. Matches or exceeds frontier models at significantly lower cost — eliminating the historical tradeoff between capability and cost for visual understanding.

## Key Capabilities
- **Video understanding**: Full temporal reasoning across video streams
- **Embodied reasoning**: Perception → reasoning → action loop for robotics
- **Spatial reasoning**: Multi-view understanding, pointing for grasps, success detection
- **Agent enhancement**: Provides vision capabilities to text-first agents like Claude and Codex

- Perceptron AI API platform
- OpenRouter integration

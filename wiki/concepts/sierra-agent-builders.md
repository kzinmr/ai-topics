# Sierra — Agent Builders and Customer Teams

**Sierra** is an AI customer experience platform that launched **Ghostwriter** (March 2026), a no-code agent builder designed to let non-technical teams create and optimize customer service agents without engineering support.

## Ghostwriter — No-Code Agent Builder

Launched in March 2026, Ghostwriter enables:
- **Agent creation through natural language** — describe what you want, no code required
- **No clicks or code** — completely interface-free agent building
- **Optimization capabilities** — built-in agent performance tuning

## Impact: Non-Technical Teams as Agent Builders

Three months after launch (June 2026):
- **Support leads**, **operations managers**, and **QA teams** are directly building customer experience agents
- People who have **never written a line of code** are shaping the customer experience
- **From handoff to hands-on** — teams closest to customers now have valuable insights into agent behavior and can iterate directly

## Positioning in the Agent Ecosystem

Sierra represents the trend of **agent democratization** — making AI agent creation accessible to non-technical users. This aligns with broader industry movements toward:
- **Service-as-Software** — agents as the primary interface for business operations
- **No-code AI** — removing technical barriers to AI adoption
- **Agent-native workflows** — rethinking business processes around agent capabilities

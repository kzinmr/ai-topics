# Google Flow Music

Google Flow Music (formerly **ProducerAI**) is a Google Labs product for **AI-assisted music creation**. Powered by the **Lyria 3 Pro** music generation model, it acts as a creative collaborator for musicians, songwriters, and producers.

## What It Does

Flow Music supports artists across the creative process:

- **Lyrics**: Generate and refine song lyrics
- **Melodies**: Explore new melodic directions and genre experimentation
- **Instruments**: Create novel synthetic instruments
- **Exploration**: Early-stage creative direction exploration
- **Polish**: Final refinements before production

## Partnership with Believe (May 2026)

On May 6, 2026, Google announced a partnership with **Believe**, a global artist development company, to bring Flow Music and Lyria 3 Pro directly to Believe and TuneCore's roster of artists, producers, and songwriters.

### Key Terms

- Believe offers Flow Music to its artists as a creative exploration and iteration space
- **Google does not claim ownership** of original content generated with Flow Music
- Co-creation model: built hand-in-hand with a small community of artists before broader release

## Technology: Lyria 3 Pro

Lyria 3 Pro is Google's latest specialized music generation model, succeeding earlier versions of Lyria (announced alongside [[entities/gemini]] in late 2023). It represents continued investment in specialized audio/music generation capabilities distinct from general-purpose multimodal models.

## Positioning

Flow Music is explicitly positioned as an **augmentation tool**, not a replacement for human musicians:

- Focus on creative collaboration and iteration
- Artist ownership of generated content
- Built by musicians (led by Seth Forsgren, who built it with his bandmates)
- Part of Google Labs experimental product ecosystem

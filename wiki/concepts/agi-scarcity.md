# AGI Scarcity

AGI scarcity refers to the question of what will remain scarce in a world where Artificial General Intelligence can automate most human tasks.

## Key Concepts
- **The Relational Sector**: Services and goods where the human-in-the-loop is part of the value.
- **Human Economy**: The segment of the economy where humans provide services for each other, which may shrink as automation increases.
- **Wealth way**: The challenge of how to tax and redistribute the wealth generated by AGI.

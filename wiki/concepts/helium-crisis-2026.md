# Helium Crisis 2026

Semiconductor supply chain disruption caused by helium shortage, impacting chip manufacturing globally.

## Background

### Helium in Chip Manufacturing
- Critical cooling agent in semiconductor fabrication
- Used in extreme ultraviolet (EUV) lithography
- Essential for advanced chip production
- No viable substitutes at scale

### 2026 Crisis Triggers
- Iran conflict disrupting helium supply chains
- Taiwan chipmakers pivoting to alternative helium sources
- Global semiconductor production at risk

### Impact
- Chip manufacturing delays
- Increased semiconductor costs
- AI hardware deployment constraints
- Supply chain diversification efforts

### Geopolitical Dimensions
- Iran targeting Amazon data centers (related conflict escalation)
- Taiwan semiconductor industry vulnerability
- US-China chip war implications
- Energy security concerns

## Strategic Implications
- AI compute scaling dependent on semiconductor supply
- Helium shortage could delay next-gen chip production
- Need for strategic helium reserves
- Alternative cooling technologies research

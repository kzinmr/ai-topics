# Superintelligence

**Superintelligence** refers to artificial intelligence that surpasses human cognitive abilities across all relevant domains, including scientific creativity, general wisdom, and social skills.

## Key Concepts
- **Recursive Improvement**: The theoretical process where an AI improves its own design, potentially leading to an exponential explosion in capability.
- **Comprehensive AI Services (CAIS)**: A framework proposed by K. Eric Drexler that views superintelligent agents as a class of service-providing products.
- **Existential Risk**: The concern that superintelligent systems might have goals not aligned with human values, leading to catastrophic outcomes.
- **Alignment Problem**: The challenge of ensuring that highly intelligent agents' goals remain consistent with human intent and ethics.

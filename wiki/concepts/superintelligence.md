# Superintelligence

Superintelligence refers to artificial intelligence systems that surpass human cognitive abilities across most important domains. 

## Key Concepts
- **The "Mind" vs. "Service" Abstraction**: Traditional views of superintelligence often posited AI functionality that plays the role of a mind in a rational utility-directed agent.
- **Comprehensive AI Services (CAIS)**: A reframed model where superintelligence emerges from distributed, asymptotically comprehensive AI services.
- **AI Safety and Alignment**: The raises risks associated with self-modifying agents and the challenge of human control.

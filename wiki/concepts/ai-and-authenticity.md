## AI Companion Dependency

Houda Nait El Barj's June 2026 Noema essay "How AI Will Change Us" examines the authenticity crisis posed by AI companions. An OpenAI/MIT Media Lab RCT tracking ~1,000 participants over 4 weeks revealed a troubling paradox: the heaviest users of AI companions were the loneliest and most emotionally dependent. The essay applies Heidegger's concept of *Geworfenheit* ("thrownness") to argue that AI companions risk severing users from the authentic human experience of being "thrown" into an unpredictable social world. Source: [Noema Magazine](https://www.noemamag.com/how-ai-will-change-us/).

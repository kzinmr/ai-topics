# Soofi S — German Open 30B Multilingual Model

**Soofi S** is an open-source 30-billion-parameter large language model released by a German AI research consortium in July 2026. It achieves top benchmark performance in both English and German, representing a significant milestone in European sovereign AI capabilities.

## Overview

- **Parameters**: 30B
- **License**: Apache 2.0 (open weights)
- **Languages**: English and German (bilingual focus)
- **Release Date**: July 13, 2026
- **Developed By**: German AI consortium (multiple German universities and research institutes)
- **Key Achievement**: Tops benchmarks in both English and German simultaneously

## Consortium

The model was developed by a consortium of German academic and research institutions, reflecting the European Union's push for sovereign AI capabilities. The collaborative approach pools compute resources, talent, and training data across multiple German institutions — a model for distributed AI development outside of large corporate labs.

## Technical Details

Soofi S is a 30B-parameter dense transformer model. Key characteristics:

- **Architecture**: Dense transformer, optimized for bilingual (EN/DE) performance
- **Training Data**: Curated corpus with strong representation of German-language content alongside English
- **Benchmark Performance**: Competitive with or exceeding similarly-sized open models on standard English benchmarks while adding German-language capabilities
- **Deployment**: Designed for both research use and practical deployment, with 30B parameters striking a balance between capability and accessibility

## European AI Sovereignty Context

Soofi S exemplifies the growing **European AI sovereignty** movement, where EU member states invest in domestic AI capabilities to reduce dependence on US and Chinese AI labs. Key aspects:

- **Regulatory Alignment**: Developed with awareness of EU AI Act requirements
- **Multilingual Focus**: Addresses the gap in non-English LLM performance, a common criticism of English-dominated model development
- **Academic Collaboration Model**: Demonstrates that distributed academic consortia can produce competitive models without single-company resources
- **Open Science**: Apache 2.0 licensing enables both research and commercial use, supporting the European open-source AI ecosystem

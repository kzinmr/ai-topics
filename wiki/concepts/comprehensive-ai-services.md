# Comprehensive AI Services (CAIS)

Comprehensive AI Services (CAIS) is a model of flexible, general intelligence in which agents are a class of service-providing products rather than a natural or necessary engine of progress.

## Key Ideas
- **Service-Oriented Intelligence**: AI agents as products that provide services, rather than autonomous "minds".na- **Recursive Improvement**: The potential for AI services to develop new services, leading to asymptotically recursive improvement. na- **AI Safety Ramifications**: The CAIS model reframes prospects of an intelligence explosion and the relationship between goals, intelligence, and human control.

# AI Services (CAIS)

Comprehensive AI Services (CAIS) is a model of flexible, general intelligence where AI agents are viewed as a class of service-providing products. This approach reframes the concept of superintelligence from a single "mind" to a distributed system of services.

## Key Ideas
- **Service-Oriented Intelligence**: Agents are products designed to perform tasks rather than autonomous engines of progress.
- **Human Control**: Focuses on how these services are guided by concrete human goals and informed by strong models of human approval.
- **Safety implications**: Addresses the risks and opportunities of superintelligent-level services.

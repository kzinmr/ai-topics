# World Models for Scientific Discovery

Using world models and agent loops to automate scientific research and discovery.

## Key Concepts

### World Models
- Causal understanding of physical/biological systems
- Predictive models for scientific phenomena
- Beyond pattern matching to causal reasoning
- Applications in drug discovery, materials science, physics

### Agent Loops for Science
- Autonomous hypothesis generation
- Experimental design by AI agents
- Results interpretation and iteration
- Closed-loop scientific discovery

### Scientific Taste
- AI developing intuition for promising research directions
- Prioritization of experiments
- Pattern recognition in scientific literature
- Combining domain expertise with AI scale

## Andrew White's Approach
- World models + agent loops + scientific taste
- Systematic automation of research pipelines
- Focus on reproducible, verifiable science
- Integration with existing scientific infrastructure

## Implications
- Accelerated scientific discovery timelines
- Democratization of research capabilities
- New paradigm for hypothesis-driven science
- Challenges in validation and peer review

# ServiceNow AI Workforce

**Date**: May 5, 2026

ServiceNow unveiled an **AI workforce** that can autonomously run entire enterprise operations — sensing, deciding, and securely acting across business processes.

## Announcement

> "Enterprises need AI that senses, decides, and securely acts"

The ServiceNow platform now supports **autonomous AI agents** that can:
- **Sense**: Monitor enterprise systems, detect issues, and identify patterns
- **Decide**: Use LLM-powered reasoning to determine appropriate actions
- **Act**: Execute workflows across business processes with security guardrails

## Significance

This represents the **enterprise adoption** of agentic AI beyond coding and development. ServiceNow is positioning AI agents as first-class participants in business operations, not just automation scripts.

## Key Capabilities

1. **Autonomous Operation**: Agents run end-to-end workflows without human intervention
2. **Security Controls**: Built-in guardrails for enterprise-grade safety
3. **Cross-System Integration**: Agents can act across multiple enterprise systems
4. **Observability**: Full audit trails and monitoring of agent actions

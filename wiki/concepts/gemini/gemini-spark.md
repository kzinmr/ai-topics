# Gemini Spark

Google's **24/7 personal AI agent** announced at I/O 2026 (May 19, 2026). Gemini Spark represents a fundamental shift from AI as a responsive assistant to AI as a **proactive, continuously running digital helper** that takes action on the user's behalf. It runs on [[concepts/gemini/gemini-3-5-flash|Gemini 3.5 Flash]] and is powered by the [[entities/google-antigravity|Antigravity]] harness.

## Overview

> "Spark represents a big shift for Gemini, transforming it from an assistant that can answer your questions into an active partner that does real work on your behalf and under your direction."

Gemini Spark is cloud-based, meaning it continues working in the background even when the user closes their laptop or locks their phone. It is deeply integrated with Google Workspace tools (Gmail, Docs, Slides, etc.) and will soon support third-party MCP connections.

## Core Capabilities

### Recurring Tasks & Triggers
Define trigger-based automations that run continuously:
- "Automatically parse monthly credit card statements to flag new or hidden subscription fees"
- Monitor inbox for specific patterns and take action

### Teach New Skills
Users can teach Spark custom workflows:
- "Check my inbox for ongoing updates from my kids' school, extract critical deadlines, and send a consolidated daily digest to me and my partner"

### Complete Workflows
End-to-end task execution spanning multiple apps:
- "Synthesize raw meeting notes across emails and chats, create polished Google Docs with its findings, and even draft the companion email kicking off a project"

## Technical Foundation

- **Model**: [[concepts/gemini/gemini-3-5-flash|Gemini 3.5 Flash]]
- **Harness**: [[entities/google-antigravity|Antigravity]] — Google's agent-first development platform
- **Architecture**: Cloud-based agent, always-on
- **Integration**: Deep Workspace integration (Gmail, Docs, Slides, Calendar)
- **MCP connections**: Canva, OpenTable, Instacart (launch partners); more integrating

## Roadmap (Summer 2026)

1. **Expanded MCP connections** — Canva, OpenTable, Instacart launching; Spark will use them for task completion
2. **Texting/emailing Spark** — Send tasks via message
3. **Custom sub-agents** — Users create specialized agents for different domains
4. **Local browser operation** — Operate the user's local browser
5. **macOS app integration** — Desktop app with Spark operating on local files and automating desktop workflows

## Rollout

- Trusted testers: Week of May 19, 2026
- Beta: Google AI Ultra subscribers in the US, "next week" from May 19
- macOS app: Available for download May 19; Spark integration coming later in summer

## Related Products

Google I/O 2026 also introduced:
- **Daily Brief** — Morning agent that aggregates emails, calendar events, and next steps into a personalized digest (rolling out to AI Plus/Pro/Ultra subscribers, US first)
- **Gemini Omni** — Multimodal video generation model (text/image/video → cinematic video)
- **Neural Expressive** — New design language for the Gemini app (fluid animations, haptic feedback, rich responses)

## Significance

Gemini Spark is among the first mainstream consumer products to deliver on the **ambient agent** vision — an AI that runs continuously and proactively rather than waiting for user prompts. Combined with Antigravity's managed agent infrastructure, it signals Google's bet that the next phase of AI adoption will be agent-driven, not chat-driven.

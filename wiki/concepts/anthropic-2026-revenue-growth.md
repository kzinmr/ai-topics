# Anthropic 2026 Revenue Growth ($44B ARR)

Anthropic disclosed Q1 2026 revenue growth of 80x year-over-year, with Annualized Recurring Revenue (ARR) now above $44 billion. Widely considered the fastest enterprise revenue growth in history.

## Revenue Trajectory

| Period | ARR | Growth |
|--------|-----|--------|
| End of 2023 | ~$100M | — |
| End of 2024 | ~$1B | 10x |
| End of 2025 | ~$9B | 9x |
| May 2026 | ~$44B | Projected 5x this year |

The doubling period is approximately **6 weeks**. Analyst Ming Li estimated $96 million in ARR added per day.

## What's Driving It

Growth is driven not by adding more seats but by existing seats consuming at a categorically different rate. 1,000+ enterprise customers now spend >$1M annually on Claude — double the count from February 2026. The consumption-based pricing model enables exponential scaling as enterprises deepen their AI usage.

## Historical Context

- AWS took **13 years** to reach $35B annual revenue
- Salesforce took **20+ years** to pass $20B
- Anthropic is doing it in **months**

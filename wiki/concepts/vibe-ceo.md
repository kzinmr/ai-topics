# Vibe CEO

A management model where a solo founder (the "Vibe CEO") orchestrates AI agents rather than human employees. The CEO sets high-level strategic direction and cultural tone ("vibes"), while specialized agents handle execution across engineering, marketing, operations, and customer support.

## Key Characteristics

- **Agent Orchestration over People Management:** The CEO's primary role is directing AI agents
- **Capital Efficiency:** 10-50x higher capital efficiency vs traditional startups
- **Subscription Economics:** Replacing 70-80% salary burn with $200-$500/month AI subscriptions
- **Speed:** Decision loops are agent-fast, not meeting-slow

# AI Organization

> **This page is a landing page for the AI Organization category.** For the full content index, see [[concepts/ai-organization/_index]].

## Summary

AI Organization is a framework category exploring how human organizations and work design transform when AI agents become capable of autonomous execution. It synthesizes three major models:

1. **Block's Hierarchy → Intelligence** (Jack Dorsey): Replacing middle management with World Model + Intelligence Layer
2. **McKinsey's Agentic Organization**: Five-pillar framework with Diamond Org Chart and M-Shaped Supervisors
3. **Solo Founder / Super IC**: AI tools enabling 1-person = 10-person teams

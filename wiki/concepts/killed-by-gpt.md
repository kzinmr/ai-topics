# KilledByGPT

KilledByGPT is a website created by Chip Huyen that tracks products and companies that have been disrupted or rendered obsolete by GPT/LLM technologies.

## Overview

The site serves as a curated catalog of products impacted by AI advancement, providing insight into how rapidly AI is transforming various market segments and business models.

## Significance

- **Market impact tracking**: Documents which products are being displaced by AI
- **Trend analysis**: Shows the pace and scope of AI-driven disruption
- **Industry insight**: Helps understand which sectors are most vulnerable to AI

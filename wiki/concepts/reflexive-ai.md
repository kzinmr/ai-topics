# Reflexive AI

Reflexive AI refers to the organizational practice where AI usage is treated as a baseline expectation — employees are expected to reflexively use AI tools as part of their workflow, and managers must prove AI *cannot* do a job before hiring for it.

## Origin

The term gained prominence from [[entities/tobi-lutke]]'s internal memo at [[entities/shopify]] in April 2025: "Reflexive AI usage is now a baseline expectation at Shopify." The key principle: "Before hiring anyone new, managers must prove AI can't do the job better."

## Key Principles

### 1. AI as Default, Not Exception
- Using AI is the expected baseline, not an optional productivity hack
- Hiring requires justification against AI alternatives
- "Using AI well is a skill that needs to be carefully learned" — best learned "by using it a lot"

### 2. Unrestricted Access to Premium Models
- Let anyone use expensive models — high-value use cases come from unexpected places
- Shopify ordered 1,500 Cursor licenses, then another 1,500
- Fastest-growing users: support and revenue teams, not engineering

### 3. Default to "Yes" with Legal
- "If you don't default to 'yes,' you're defaulting to 'no'"
- Early adoption of GitHub Copilot achieved 80% adoption
- Legal team collaboration from the start, not as a gate

### 4. Unlimited AI Tool Spend
- No quota on AI tool spending
- Internal leaderboard of token usage as a proxy for value
- Remove all friction for AI adoption

### 5. Beginner's Mindset
- Foster beginner's mindset, hire more beginners
- Value adaptability over entrenched expertise
- Make AI "show its work" — transparency in decision-making

## Shopify's AI Adoption Strategy
According to First Round Capital's analysis, Shopify's AI adoption is based on the following principles:


### 1. Anyone Can Use Expensive Models
- High-value use cases can come from anywhere
- 1,500 licenses → ordered another **1,500 Cursor** licenses
- Fastest-growing users: **Support and Revenue**, not Engineering


### 2. Legal Defaults to "Yes"
- "Not defaulting to Yes = defaulting to No"
- Early GitHub Copilot adopter (80% adoption rate)
- Legal team engaged from the early stages


### 3. Unlimited AI Tool Spending
- **No quota** on AI tool spending
- Internal token usage leaderboard as a proxy for value
- Complete removal of friction for AI adoption


### 4. Showing AI the "Process"
- Transparency in AI decision-making processes
- Explicit reasoning for judgments


## Significance
Reflexive AI Usage represents a **new paradigm** for AI adoption in large enterprises:

- **Mandatory** AI skill requirements in hiring
- Halting hires for positions replaceable by AI
- Transparency and incentive design around token usage
- AI literacy as organizational culture

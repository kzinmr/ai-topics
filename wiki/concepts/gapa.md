# GAPA

> **This page is a redirect.** The concept referred to as "GAPA" is actually **GEPA — Genetic-Pareto Prompt Evolution**.

GEPA is a genetic algorithm-based prompt optimization technique integrated into DSPy that uses Pareto optimization to balance quality and cost simultaneously, achieving 6% higher performance than GRPO with 35× fewer samples. It was recognized as an **ICLR 2026 Oral** paper.

For full content, see: **[[concepts/gepa]]**

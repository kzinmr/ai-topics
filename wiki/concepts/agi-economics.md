# AGI Economics

The economic shifts resulting from Artificial General Intelligence.

## Core Themes
- **Labor Share & Wages**: How automation affects the share of income going to labor vs. capital.
- **Wealth way**: Policy frameworks for taxing and redistributing wealth generated by AI-driven productivity.
- **Relational Scarcity**: The theory that human-in-the-loop services remain scarce even as material costs drop.
- **Jevons Paradox**: The potential for AI-driven efficiency to increase total demand for resources and services.

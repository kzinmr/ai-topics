# AGI Economics

AGI Economics is the study of the impact of Artificial General Intelligence on global economics, labor, and wealth distribution.

## Key Concepts나- **Labor Market Impact**: How AGI might displace or transform human labor.
- **Wealth Concentration**: The risks of wealth concentration in an AGI-driven economy.

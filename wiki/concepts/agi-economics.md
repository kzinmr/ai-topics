# Economics of AGI

The economics of AGI explores the structural impact of artificial general intelligence on the global economy, labor markets, and wealth distribution.

## Key Ideas
- **Labor Market Impact**: Analyzing how automation will affect wages and the labor share of the economy.
- **Scarcity after AGI**: Identifying what will remain scarce in a world where AI can perform most human-like tasks.
- **Wealth Distribution**: Exploring the optimal way to tax and redistribute the wealth generated by AGI.

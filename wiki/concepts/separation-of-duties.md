# Separation of Duties

In the context of AI agents, separation of duties is an architectural design pattern used to divide responsibilities among components to prevent any single agent from performing harmful actions autonomously.

## Key Concepts
- **Risk Mitigation**: Ensuring that no singleComponent has full control over a criticalProcess.
- **Safeguards**: Implementing structural boundaries to limit potential agent failure modes.

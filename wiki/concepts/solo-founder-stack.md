# Solo Founder Stack (2026)

The set of tools, practices, and organizational models that enable a single founder to build and run a company at scale in the AI era — the "one-person unicorn" paradigm.

## Market Context

- Solo-founded startups rose from 23.7% (2019) to 36.3% (mid-2025) per Carta
- Gartner reported 1,445% surge in enterprise multi-agent AI orchestration enquiries (Q1→Q2 2025)
- Dario Amodei: 70-80% odds on a one-person billion-dollar company by 2026

## Core Components

### 1. Context Engineering
Replaces prompt engineering by architecting the entire information environment:
- [[context-engineering]] as the foundational skill
- CLAUDE.md files, MCP servers, [[ai-agent-memory]] pipelines
- Structured memory and knowledge graphs

### 2. The "Vibe CEO" Model
- Manages AI agents, not people
- Sets high-level direction, lets specialized agents handle execution
- 10-50x higher capital efficiency vs traditional startups
- Replacing 70-80% salary burn with $200-$500/month AI subscriptions

### 3. Minimum Viable AI Governance
- Purpose-driven agent orchestration
- Explicit structure over agent sprawl
- Organizational memory as critical infrastructure
- Connects to [[agent-governance]] and [[reflexive-ai]]

## Connection to Other Trends

The solo founder stack emerges from the intersection of:
- [[nvidia-dynamo]] making agentic inference affordable
- [[reflexive-ai]] normalizing AI-as-baseline in organizations
- [[experience-is-a-tax]] reducing the advantage of senior teams
- [[managed-agents]] (Telegram, Claude) lowering the barrier to entry

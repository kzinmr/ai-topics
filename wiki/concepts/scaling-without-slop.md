# Scaling without Slop

Approach to scaling AI models while maintaining quality, avoiding the "slop" problem of low-quality outputs.

## Key Principles

### Quality-Focused Scaling
- Emphasizes data quality over quantity
- Careful curation of training data
- Avoids dilution of model capabilities with noisy data
- Maintains performance as model size increases

### Anti-Slop Techniques
- Rigorous data filtering
- Quality benchmarks during training
- Iterative refinement processes
- Human-in-the-loop validation

### Implications for Model Development
- More expensive but higher-quality training pipelines
- Better reasoning capabilities vs brute-force scaling
- Sustainable scaling strategies
- Community standards for quality

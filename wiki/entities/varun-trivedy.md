About
I’m currently at LangChain where I lead a bunch of work around building agents, evaluating agents on long-horizon autonomous tasks, and pushing the frontier of agentic coding.
My Research Interests
My research focuses on three main areas:
Building Autonomous Agents
: Building agent harnesses/environments, helping agents self-improve & self-verify their work
Agent Evals
: Building good evals and using traces to improve agents
Information Retrieval
: Efficient search and representation learning for multi-modal data
I also contribute to open source projects and love building tools that make AI more accessible and useful for everyone.

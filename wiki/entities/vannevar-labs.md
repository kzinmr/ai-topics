# Vannevar Labs

Vannevar Labs is a defense technology company building AI-powered software and hardware systems for the U.S. national security community. It focuses on intelligence collection, foreign text analysis, and non-kinetic warfare capabilities.

## Key Facts
- Founded by Brett Granberg and Nini Hamrick while at Stanford Graduate School of Business.
- Named after Vannevar Bush, WWII science administrator and architect of the military-industrial research complex.
- Scaled from a $15K pilot to over $80M in revenue.
- Focused on bridging Silicon Valley engineering with national security missions.

## Products & Technology
- **Decrypt**: AI-powered foreign text workflow platform for intelligence officers — translation, pattern detection, document search across vast datasets.
- **LLM Agents**: Integrated for enhanced search and product usability.
- Forward-deployed engineers working alongside mission teams in classified environments.

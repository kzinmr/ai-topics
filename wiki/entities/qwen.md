# Qwen

**Qwen** (also known as Tongyi Qianwen) is a family of large language models developed by **Alibaba Group's** Cloud Intelligence unit. Qwen models are known for their strong multilingual performance, open-weight availability, and use as base models for fine-tuning and quantization projects.

## Key Models

- **Qwen 3.6 / 3.5**: Large-scale base models used as foundations for derivative models like [[concepts/bonsai-27b|Bonsai 27B]] by [[entities/prism-ml|Prism ML]]
- **Qwen2**, **Qwen2.5**: Earlier generation models with strong coding and reasoning benchmarks

Qwen models are released under open licenses and are widely used in the [[concepts/model-quantization|quantization]] and local LLM ecosystem.

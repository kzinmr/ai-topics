# Chinmay Karkar

> **Chinmay Karkar** is a researcher and blogger who published a comprehensive survey on On-Policy Distillation (OPD) in LLM training in June 2026.

## Overview

Chinmay Karkar maintains a technical blog at chinmaykarkar.com covering AI/ML topics. His most notable work is a detailed survey on policy distillation in language model training, covering ~40 papers from 2026.

## Key Contributions

### OPD Survey (June 2026)
Published "The Imitation Game: State of Policy Distillation in Language Model Training" — a comprehensive survey covering:
- Foundational OPD methods (MiniLLM, GKD, DistiLLM, G-OPD, AOPD, OPCD)
- On-Policy Self-Distillation (OPSD) variants (SDFT, SDPO, GATES, CRISP, RLSD, CaOPD)
- Failure modes analysis (token-level KL bias, prefix drift, local teachability collapse, Rock Tokens, diversity collapse, PI gap)
- Open problems (PI+verifier, skill distillation, uncertainty-aware feedback, cross-tokeniser OPD)
- Industrial adoption (Qwen3, DeepSeek-V4, GLM-5, MiMo-V2-Flash)

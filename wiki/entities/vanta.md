# Vanta

Leading agentic trust platform automating security monitoring and compliance for internet businesses. Helps companies achieve and maintain SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO 27001, and other certifications through continuous, real-time monitoring.

## Key Facts
- Founded in 2018 by Christina Cacioppo; Y Combinator W18 alum
- $300M+ ARR as of April 2026; backed by Sequoia, Craft Ventures, YC, J.P. Morgan, Goldman Sachs
- Valued at $2.5B+ (2024); 700+ employees; 12,000+ customers
- Christina Cacioppo previously led product at Dropbox Paper and was an investor at Union Square Ventures
- Pioneered Agentic Application Runtime Model (AARM), donated to Cloud Security Alliance

## Products & Technology
- **Agentic Trust Platform**: Continuous security monitoring replacing point-in-time audits
- Automated compliance for SOC 2, HIPAA, ISO 27001, GDPR, FedRAMP
- AI features for vendor risk assessment, data querying, and automated customer commitments
- FedRAMP Moderate authorized via Vanta Government Cloud

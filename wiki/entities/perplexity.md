# Perplexity

**Perplexity** is an AI-powered search and research platform that has expanded from general web search into **vertical-specific professional products**. In May 2026, it launched "Perplexity Computer for Professional Finance" and premium medical journal access, representing a shift toward domain-specialized AI research workstations.

## Overview

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Product** | AI-powered search and research platform |
| **Key Products** | Perplexity Pro, Perplexity Computer for Professional Finance |
| **Differentiator** | Licensed data + domain-specific workflows |
| **Vertical Expansion** | Finance (May 2026), Healthcare (NEJM/BMJ, May 2026) |

## Perplexity Computer for Professional Finance (May 2026)

A dedicated AI workstation for financial professionals:

- **35 dedicated analyst workflows** (valuation, due diligence, market research, etc.)
- **Licensed financial data** integration (FactSet, S&P Global, and similar)
- **Positioning**: An AI-powered Bloomberg Terminal competitor

This mirrors **Anthropic's finance agent templates** (pitch generation, valuation review, KYC integration with FactSet and S&P Global) — both companies are targeting the same high-value vertical simultaneously.

## Healthcare Expansion

Perplexity added **premium access to NEJM (New England Journal of Medicine) and BMJ (British Medical Journal)** for clinical-grade medical research. This positions Perplexity as a research tool for physicians, researchers, and medical professionals requiring access to the highest-tier medical literature.

## Enterprise Verticalization Trend

Perplexity's finance and healthcare moves are part of a broader 2026 trend where generic AI chatbots are being replaced by **workflow-specific "Professional" versions**:

| Company | Vertical | Product |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **Perplexity** | Finance | Professional Finance Computer |
| **Perplexity** | Healthcare | NEJM/BMJ premium access |
| **Anthropic** | Finance | Agent templates + FactSet/S&P integration |
| **OpenAI** | Healthcare | ChatGPT for Clinicians (free for verified US physicians) |

## Strategic Implications

Perplexity's verticalization strategy leverages:
1. **Licensed data moats** — agreements with premium data providers create barriers to entry
2. **Workflow specialization** — 35 analyst workflows represent domain expertise, not just search
3. **Platform economics** — each vertical adds to the same underlying AI research platform

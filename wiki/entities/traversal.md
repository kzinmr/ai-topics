# Traversal

AI platform for autonomous incident resolution and prevention. Uses agentic AI and causal machine learning to detect, troubleshoot, and resolve complex production incidents — reducing mean time to recovery (MTTR) by an average of 40%.

## Key Facts
- Founded in 2023 in New York City; emerged from stealth June 2025 with $48M funding
- Raised $53M total; Series A led by Kleiner Perkins and Sequoia; Amex Ventures strategic investor
- Founding team has years of PhD research in agentic AI and causal ML
- Backed by endorsements from Elastic CTO Shay Banon and LangChain CEO Harrison Chase

## Products & Technology
- **Production World Model™**: Continuously learning, machine-readable model of enterprise production environments
- Agentic AI that autonomously searches massive telemetry datasets for root cause identification
- Integrates with observability stacks; supports rollbacks, code changes, and automated remediation

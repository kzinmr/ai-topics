# SAP Business AI Platform

## Overview
Announced at SAP Sapphire 2026 (May 12, 2026), the **SAP Business AI Platform** is a unified foundation for building and deploying enterprise AI grounded in real business context. It unifies SAP Business Technology Platform, SAP Business Data Cloud, and SAP Business AI into a single governed environment.

It is the centerpiece of SAP's **Autonomous Enterprise** vision — where humans and AI work together to run critical business workflows profitably, strategically, and safely.

## Core Components

### SAP Knowledge Graph
Gives AI agents a structured map of business entities, processes, and relationships across a customer's SAP landscape — providing the business context essential for accurate agentic decisions.

### Joule Studio
SAP's AI-first solution for building enterprise agents, applications, and agentic workflows. Supports no-code, pro-code, and AI frameworks on SAP-managed infrastructure.

### SAP Autonomous Suite
Enables SAP's existing business applications with AI agents capable of running processes end-to-end. Deploys 50+ domain-specific Joule agents across finance, supply chain, procurement, HR, and customer experience.

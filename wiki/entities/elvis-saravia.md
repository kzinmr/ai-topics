# Elvis Saravia

**Elvis Saravia** (@omarsar0) is the founder of **DAIR.AI** (Democratizing Artificial Intelligence Research), an organization focused on AI education, research dissemination, and community building. He runs the **DAIR.AI Academy**, which provides courses and events for technical founders, AI engineers, and startups on cutting-edge AI agent and LLM topics.

## Bio

Elvis Saravia is one of the most active educators in the AI agent and LLM space, known for synthesizing practitioner insights from leading figures like Peter Steinberger, Boris Cherny, and Addy Osmani into structured educational content. Through DAIR.AI Academy, he hosts events and publishes in-depth articles that bridge research and practice — translating fast-moving developments in agentic coding, loop engineering, and AI tooling into actionable frameworks for builders.

His writing style blends technical depth with pedagogical clarity, often collaborating with Claude to produce well-structured, comprehensive articles that serve as reference pieces for the AI engineering community.

## DAIR.AI

**DAIR.AI** is an AI research and education organization founded by Saravia. It is known for:

- **DAIR.AI Academy** — courses and live events for AI practitioners
- **Research digests and newsletters** — curated summaries of AI/ML research developments
- **Community building** — connecting AI researchers, engineers, and founders
- **Educational content** — in-depth articles on LLMs, prompt engineering, agent architectures, and coding workflows

The Academy runs paid events that bring together practitioners at the frontier of AI agent development.

## Key Contributions

### Loop Engineering Synthesis (Jun 2026)

Published "From Prompting Agents to Loop Engineering," a comprehensive synthesis of the paradigm shift from manual prompting to autonomous loop design. The article consolidated perspectives from Peter Steinberger (@steipete), Boris Cherny (@bcherny), Addy Osmani (@addyosmani), and Matt Van Horn (@mvanhorn), providing:

- A taxonomy of five meanings of "loop" (ReAct → AutoGPT → ralph loop → /goal → orchestration)
- Six building blocks of production loops (trigger, isolation, context, tool reach, verifier, state)
- Concrete loop patterns (PR babysitter, CI health, deploy verification)
- Economics of loop engineering (iterations as budget line, verifier as critical path)
- Anti-patterns and failure modes

### DAIR.AI Academy Events

Runs events featuring practitioners at the cutting edge of AI agent development, covering topics such as agent architecture, coding agents, loop engineering, and AI-native development workflows.

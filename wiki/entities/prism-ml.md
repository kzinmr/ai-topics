# Prism ML

**Prism ML** is an AI research and deployment company that emerged from Caltech researchers. They are known for developing the [[concepts/bonsai-27b|Bonsai 27B]] model family — extreme low-bit quantization models capable of running frontier-scale AI on consumer devices.

Backed by **Khosla Ventures**, **Cerberus**, **Google**, and **Samsung**. The company focuses on model compression, [[concepts/model-quantization|quantization]] technology, and on-device AI deployment for agentic workloads.

## Key Products

- **Bonsai 27B**: First 27B-class model running on phones (July 2026)
- **Bonsai 8B**, **Bonsai 4B**, **Bonsai 1.7B**: Smaller variants in the Bonsai family
- **Bonsai Image**: Vision model variant

All Bonsai models are released under Apache 2.0 license and based on [[entities/qwen|Qwen]] checkpoints.

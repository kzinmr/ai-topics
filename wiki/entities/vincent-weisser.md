## Prime Intellect

Co-founded Prime Intellect in 2023 with Johannes Hagemann to democratize AI development through decentralized training. Under his leadership, the company:

- Raised $20.5M (Seed $5.5M + Series A $15M) from Founders Fund, Andrej Karpathy, Clem Delangue, Tri Dao
- Built Lab: RL post-training platform for self-improving agents
- Open-sourced [[renderers]], [[verifiers-rl|verifiers]], [[prime-rl]]
- Trained INTELLECT-1 (10B), INTELLECT-2, and INTELLECT-3 model series

## Vision

Vincent advocates for commoditizing compute and intelligence through decentralized AI. He frames Prime Intellect's mission as building "open frontier AI models and the infra to train and deploy your own agentic AI."

# Cat Wu

Head of Product at Anthropic, responsible for Claude Code and Anthropic's product development strategy.

## Overview

Cat Wu leads product development at Anthropic with a focus on making the Anthropic product team move faster than any other comparable organization. She was featured in Lenny's Newsletter (April 23, 2026) discussing Anthropic's product methodology, development velocity, and the philosophy behind Claude Code's design.

## Role at Anthropic

- **Title:** Head of Product

## Key Insights from Lenny's Newsletter Interview

### Development Velocity Philosophy

Anthropic's product team operates at exceptional speed compared to industry peers. This velocity is achieved through:

1. **Clear ownership** — Product leads have decision autonomy
2. **Bias toward shipping** — Minimal bureaucracy, fast iteration cycles
3. **Developer-first mindset** — Claude Code designed by developers, for developers
4. **Tight feedback loops** — Rapid user testing and iteration

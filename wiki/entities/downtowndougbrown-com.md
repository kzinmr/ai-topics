## Bio

About
Hi! I’m Doug. I am a classic Mac and Linux enthusiast who also uses Windows. Lately I’ve really enjoyed tinkering with Linux kernel development in my spare time. I’m currently the Senior Manager of Embedded Software at
Skip-Line
, where I work with bare-metal firmware for microcontrollers and embedded Linux projects for the road marking industry.
I don’t write blog posts on a regular schedule, but when I solve an interesting problem or work on a cool personal project, I like to write about it here.

# Kieran Klaassen

**Kieran Klaassen** is the General Manager of [[entities/every-inc|Cora]], Every's AI email assistant. He is the primary author of **Compound Engineering: The Definitive Guide**, which defines Every's [[concepts/compound-engineering-every|Compound Engineering]] philosophy — an AI-native approach to software development where each unit of work simplifies future work.

## Core Thesis

Klaassen argues that traditional engineering creates "technical debt" where each new feature makes the system harder to modify. Compound Engineering inverts this: each unit of work teaches the system new capabilities, making future work easier. His approach enables single-person teams to run entire products (Every runs 5 products this way).

# Amazon Acquires Rivr

March 2026 acquisition of Zurich-based autonomous robotics startup Rivr by Amazon.

## Deal Details

### Company Background
- **Rivr**: Autonomous robotics startup
- **CEO**: Marko Bjelonic
- **Specialization**: Stair-climbing delivery robots ("dog on roller skates")
- **Total funding**: $25M before acquisition
- **Last valuation**: $100M

### Amazon's Investment History
- Amazon Industrial Innovation Fund invested in 2024 seed round ($22.2M)
- Bezos Expeditions also participated
- Strategic interest in last-mile delivery robotics

### Pilot Program
- Austin pilot with Veho (package delivery company)
- Goal: Scale to 100 bots by 2026
- Learning from real-world deployment

## Strategic Implications

### For Amazon
- Accelerates doorstep delivery capabilities
- Expands physical AI portfolio
- Complements existing logistics infrastructure
- Competes with other delivery robot companies

### For Rivr
- Access to Amazon's scale and resources
- Faster deployment timeline
- Integration with Amazon's logistics network

### Market Context
- Part of broader robotics acquisition trend
- Competes with Waymo, Zoox, and other autonomous delivery efforts
- Signals Amazon's commitment to last-mile automation

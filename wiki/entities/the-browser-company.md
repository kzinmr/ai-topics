# The Browser Company

The Browser Company is a software startup that reimagined the web browser with Arc and later Dia — an AI-native browser. Founded to reinvent how users interact with the internet, it was acquired by Atlassian in 2025 for $610M.

## Key Facts
- Founded in 2019 by Josh Miller (ex-Facebook product manager) and Hursh Agrawal.
- Arc launched publicly in 2022–2023 with features like Spaces, Split View, and Boosts.
- Dia released in beta June 2025 as an AI-first browser with conversational AI across tabs.
- Acquired by Atlassian in September 2025 for $610 million; deal closed October 2025.

## Products & Technology
- **Arc**: Customizable browser with workspaces, tab management, built-in whiteboard, and design-focused UX.
- **Dia**: AI-native browser enabling chat with AI assistant across multiple tabs at once.
- Atlassian integration aims to create an AI-powered browser optimized for knowledge work and SaaS apps.

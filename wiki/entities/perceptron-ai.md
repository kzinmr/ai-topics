# Perceptron AI

## Overview
Perceptron AI is an AI research lab pushing the frontier of **physical AI** — building systems that perceive, reason, and act in the real world. Founded November 2024 in Bellevue, Washington, by Armen Aghajanyan (CEO) and Akshat Shrivastava (CTO), both former research scientists at Facebook AI Research (FAIR).

## Perceptron Mk1
Their first model, Perceptron Mk1 ("Mark One"), launched May 12, 2026 — purpose-built for video understanding and embodied reasoning. It delivers performance competitive with frontier models (Google, Anthropic, OpenAI, Qwen) at a fraction of the cost.

Available via Perceptron AI API and OpenRouter.

## Use Cases
- **Manufacturing & Industrial**: Defect detection, OSHA compliance, analog instrument reading, inventory tracking
- **Media & Content**: Semantic visual search, tagging, content moderation
- **Robotics & Automation**: Embodied reasoning for manipulation, navigation, grasp pointing
- **Geospatial**: Satellite/drone analysis, vegetation encroachment, disaster assessment
- **Security & Surveillance**: Context-aware alerts distinguishing meaningful events from background
- **Agentic Tooling**: Enhanced vision for Claude, Codex, and text-first agents

# Meta

Social media and technology company, developer of the LLaMA model family and other AI initiatives.

## AI Development (as of Apr 2026)

### Muse Spark
Meta's latest model announced April 2026. Position: between [[claude-sonnet-4.6]] and [[claude-opus-4.6]] in capability. API access coming, with promises of open-source release (noting "rip LLaMA" from community commentary).

### Instagram AI Search
Instagram search has significantly improved over recent months courtesy of AI integration.

### Acquisitions
Multiple acquisitions in the year prior to April 2026 with limited public showings.

## Frontier Model Position (Apr 2026)

Per Ethan Mollick's analysis:
- **Tier 1**: Google, OpenAI, Anthropic (lead)
- **Tier 2**: Meta (joining the pack)
- **Tier 3**: xAI (fallen off)
- **Chinese models**: 7-9 months behind frontier

## Open Source History

Meta has been known for open-sourcing models (LLaMA series). Community notes suggest potential shift away from this with "rip LLaMA" commentary regarding Muse Spark.

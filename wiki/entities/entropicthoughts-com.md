## Bio

My name is Chris and I live in Sweden. I have a beautiful, supportive wife whose
love I will never be able to requite, neither in degree nor kind. Our two
wonderful offspring have already taught me so much about humans and life.
I approach things as a quant, systems thinker, and anarchist

engineering, site reliability engineering, management, and undercover
statistics.

# Microsoft AI Team

The Microsoft AI Team is the research and engineering division within Microsoft responsible for developing-frontier AI models and technologies.

## Key Projects
- **MAI-Thinking-1**: A 35B active / 1T total parameter MoE model designed as a "hill-climbing machine" for reasoning.
- **MoE (Mixture of Experts)**: Exploration of sparse expert-based architectures for large-scale modeling.

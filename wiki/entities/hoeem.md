# hoeem (@hooeem)

**hoeem** (@hooeem, formerly @crypthoem) is an X/Twitter creator with 169K+ followers and publisher of the **Seven c Newsletter** on Substack. His primary content domain is cryptocurrency education, trading alpha, and smart-money tracking. He gained broader recognition in the AI developer community for his viral guide on becoming a Claude Certified Architect.

## Platforms

- **X/Twitter**: [@hooeem](https://x.com/hooeem) — 169K+ followers, 55K+ posts
- **Substack**: [Seven c Newsletter](https://sevenc.substack.com/) — 24K+ subscribers. Weekly newsletter (free + premium tiers) covering crypto trading education, smart money tracking, yield analysis, TVL momentum signals, prediction markets, and since Feb 2026, AI tooling education.
- **Joined X**: February 2021 (previously operated as @crypthoem)

## Newsletter: Seven c

The Seven c Newsletter has been published continuously for **167+ weekly issues** (~3+ years). It operates on a dual-tier model:
- **Free tier**: Weekly roundup of crypto news, technical analysis, and curated alpha aggregation from the X platform
- **Premium tier** (£9.99/month): In-depth trading education, elite wallet tracking, prediction market analysis, yield optimization, and capital flow tracking using data from ChainEDGE, DeFiLlama, and TokenUnlocks

In **February 2026** (Issue #154), the newsletter expanded its scope to cover AI alongside crypto: *"Crypto, AI, and robotics are changing the world we live in. It's important that we take advantage of it…"* The free tier now regularly features AI tooling sections alongside crypto content.

## Notable Content

### Claude Certified Architect Guide (Mar 15, 2026)
A viral X Article titled *"I want to become a Claude architect (full course)"* that explains the five domains required to pass the Anthropic Claude Certified Architect exam. Despite the exam being limited to Anthropic partners, the guide was released publicly as a comprehensive resource.

- **Metrics**: 60,405 bookmarks, 16,752 likes, 2,169 retweets, 8.8M impressions
- **Coverage**: Claude Code, Claude Agent SDK, Claude API, Model Context Protocols (MCP), and production-grade Claude application development
- → [[concepts/claude-certified-architect-domains]]

### Claude Session Extension Guide (Apr 2026)
A guide on extending Claude session limits by integrating Claude Code with Google NotebookLM (via `notebooklm-py`, an open-source CLI tool reverse-engineered from NotebookLM's internal protocols). The workflow offloads heavy document analysis to Gemini-powered NotebookLM, bypassing Claude's usage limits.

### LLM Knowledge Base Creation Course
A full course on building personal LLM knowledge bases positioned as "external brains," inspired by Andrej Karpathy's work. Covers the concept of creating custom knowledge systems for business, content, personal use, and the emerging market opportunity of building these for local businesses.

### AI Learning Resources Curation
Curated lists of AI learning resources ranked from beginner to expert, including Anthropic Academy (Claude 101 & AI Fluency), Google AI Essentials, and community-vetted tutorial paths.

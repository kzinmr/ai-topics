# Rob Hoeijmakers

Rob Hoeijmakers is a Dutch web strategist and AI governance writer who runs the blog [hoeijmakers.net](https://hoeijmakers.net/). He is associated with [Schmuki, Digital & AI Agency](https://schmuki.nl/). His writing focuses on AI governance, the EU AI Act, AI transparency, and the practical implications of AI regulation for organizations.

## Key Themes

- **AI Governance & Regulation** — Deep dives into the EU AI Act, documentation obligations, and how regulatory frameworks are shaping AI deployment
- **AI Transparency** — Analysis of [[concepts/security-and-governance/model-cards-system-cards|model cards and system cards]] as proto-regulatory documents
- **AI Strategy** — Practical guidance on working with AI tools and policy
- **Future of Work** — How AI is changing organizational practices
- **Europe & Digital Policy** — EU-specific perspective on AI regulation and digital identity

## Notable Articles

- "Model Cards, System Cards and What They're Quietly Becoming" (2025-08-05) — Analysis of frontier model transparency documents and their regulatory significance
- "Learning to Work with the EU AI Act" — Practical approach to EU AI regulation
- "AI Benchmarks and Evals" — Understanding what AI benchmarks actually measure

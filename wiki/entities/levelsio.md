## Who is Pieter Levels

Pieter Levels is one of the most prominent indie makers (solopreneurs) in tech. He gained fame through his **12 Startups in 12 Months** challenge in 2014 and has since built a portfolio of profitable SaaS businesses — all as a solo founder with no employees.

His philosophy: ship fast, use boring tech (single PHP file was his stack for years), focus on adding value rather than perfection. He open-shares his revenue numbers publicly.

# OpenAI Spud

Internal codename for OpenAI's next major language model (potentially GPT-6 or GPT-5.5). Pre-training completed March 2026.

## Key Details

### Strategic Shift
- **Sora discontinued**: Video AI tool shut down due to enormous GPU demands
- **Resource reallocation**: GPUs moved from video generation to language model development
- **Enterprise focus**: Prioritizing coding tools and business applications over creative AI

### Expected Capabilities
- Sam Altman: model could "really accelerate the economy"
- Very strong performance expected
- Release timeline: "within a few weeks" (April 2026)

### Organizational Changes
- Safety division moved under research (Mark Chen)
- Technical security under Greg Brockman
- Altman focusing on fundraising and data center construction
- Fidji Simo appointed CEO of "AGI Deployment" division
- Internal "Code Red" state since December 2025 due to competitor pressure

### Super App Strategy
- Combining ChatGPT, Codex, and proprietary browser
- Single desktop application for enterprise productivity
- Sora integration abandoned due to strategic misalignment

## Implications
- Signals OpenAI's pivot from creative AI to productivity/enterprise tools
- GPU reallocation shows resource constraints in model development
- Competitive pressure from Anthropic Claude and Google Gemini driving urgency

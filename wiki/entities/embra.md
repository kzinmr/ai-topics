# Embra

**Founded:** Feb 2023
**CEO:** [[entities/zach-tratar]]
**Website:** https://embra.ai/
**Status:** Acquired by [[entities/notion]] (Jan 2026)

## Overview

Embra was an AI platform that built a **Graph Context Engine** to power B2B AI applications. The core product was a "Business Memory Engine" that learned from users, prospects, and internal team interactions — ingesting all organizational information to detect patterns, cluster insights, and automate work.

## Product

Embra's value proposition was saving teams 1+ hour per day through contextual AI automation. Key capabilities:

- **Business Memory Engine** — Graph-based organizational knowledge representation
- **Pattern Detection** — AI-driven insight extraction from team communications
- **Workflow Automation** — Task automation based on learned context

## Acquisition

Embra was **acquired by Notion** in January 2026. Ivan Zhao (Notion CEO) announced the integration, highlighting Zach Tratar's long focus on **context engineering** — building systems that organize and leverage organizational knowledge before it became an industry term.

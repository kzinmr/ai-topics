## Bio

Hello! My name is Jimmy, and I live with my wife Dorte in a cozy little village near Billund, Denmark. (The city of Lego!) Until 2009, I made my home in Dallas, Texas, but that life seems very far away to me now. Such is the way of time and change, right?
The bulk of the articles here form an historical chronicle of interactive entertainment, digital culture, and other matters adjacent. Because history is always in the making, a project of this nature can never be truly completed. But that’s fine: I’m enjoying the journey immensely.
No matter what writers might say, every single one of them wants to be read. I’m certainly no exception. I’m therefore happy that a fair number of you have made my scribblings a small part of your online routine. Thanks for that! I’ll try to make sure the content here remains worthy of your time.
In return, you can help me out a bit, by
becoming a Patreon patron
or just
making a one-time donation via PayPal
. In either case, you can decide how much to spend, based on the enjoyment you get from reading what I write, the importance you place on this work as history, and of course your own personal financial situation. I will never require anyone to pay to read the articles on this site, but your support, if you have the means to provide it, will help to ensure that I can continue doing this for a long time to come.

## Bio

Dr. Thomas Wiecki is the CEO and co-founder of **PyMC Labs**, a Bayesian consulting firm that helps companies make better decisions through probabilistic modeling. He is a core developer of **PyMC** (formerly PyMC3), the leading probabilistic programming framework in Python, and has been instrumental in bringing Bayesian methods to industry data science.

His background in computational neuroscience at Brown University gave him a deep foundation in statistical modeling. He's known for applying Bayesian workflows to real-world problems — including a widely-cited COVID-19 model early in the pandemic.

## Current Work

### PyMC Labs

Founded to make Bayesian methods practical for business decision-making. PyMC Labs provides consulting, training, and custom model development for companies across industries.

### Show Us Your Agent Skills (Co-Host)

Since May 2026, co-hosting with [[entities/hugo-bowne-anderson]] a live series where top practitioners demo their agent workflows. Thomas brings a statistical rigor and Bayesian perspective to the discussion of agentic engineering.

### Agentic Data Science

Building a course with Hugo on applying agentic engineering to data science workflows — hands-on cohort format with reusable skills, open-source code, and notebooks.

## Key Philosophy

Thomas approaches agentic engineering from a statistical perspective — treating agent outputs as probabilistic, needing verification rather than blind trust. His Bayesian background shapes how he thinks about agent reliability, uncertainty quantification, and the generator-evaluator workflow pattern.

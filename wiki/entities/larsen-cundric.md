## Bio

Larsen Cundric is the Founding Engineer at **Browser Use**, leading the design and development of the backend, infrastructure, and public API for Browser Use Cloud. He is the sole infrastructure engineer at the company — described as "the only infrastructure engineer at Browser Use and the main person behind their Cloud platform."

His key achievement: migrated the entire infrastructure to a scalable AWS-native architecture (ECS, Lambda, RDS, S3), cutting costs by 80% and latency by 3×. He also introduced event-driven pipelines for async tasks, scheduled agent runs, and webhooks.

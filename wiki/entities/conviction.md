# Conviction

**Conviction** is a venture capital firm founded by [[entities/sarah-guo|Sarah Guo]] (formerly General Partner at Greylock Partners). The firm focuses on AI investment, with a particular emphasis on enterprise AI, developer tooling, and frontier AI infrastructure. Describing itself as "purpose-built to serve AI-Native, Software 3.0 companies," Conviction partners with the most important AI companies of tomorrow with $1M–$25M checks.

## People

The Conviction team includes experienced investors and operators:

## Portfolio

Conviction has backed a broad range of AI-native companies across infrastructure, developer tools, and applications:

### AI Infrastructure & Platforms
- **[[entities/baseten|Baseten]]** — AI model serving and inference infrastructure (founded by Tuhin Srivastava, Amir Haghighat, Phillip Howes)
- **[[entities/mistral|Mistral]]** — Open-weight LLM developer (founded by Arthur Mensch, Guillaume Lample, Timothée Lacroix)
- **[[entities/cartesia|Cartesia]]** — Real-time speech AI and audio models (founded by Karan Goel, Albert Gu, Brandon Yang, Arjun Desai, Chris Re)
- **Essential AI** — AI foundation models (founded by Ashish Vaswani, Niki Parmar — co-authors of "Attention is All You Need")
- **Mithril** — AI infrastructure and training (founded by Jared Quincy Davis)

### AI Applications & Developer Tools
- **[[entities/cognition|Cognition]]** — AI coding agent (Devin) (founded by Scott Wu, Steven Hao, Walden Yan)
- **[[entities/harvey|Harvey]]** — AI for legal professionals (founded by Gabe Pereyra, Winston Weinberg)
- **[[entities/heygen|HeyGen]]** — AI video generation platform (founded by Joshua Xu, Wayne Liang)
- **Sierra** — AI customer experience platform (founded by Bret Taylor, Clay Bavor)
- **Listen Labs** — AI voice and audio (founded by Alfred Wahlforss, Florian Juengermann)
- **Sunday** — AI-native sales platform (founded by Tony Zhao, Cheng Chi)

### Security & Specialized AI
- **Corridor** — AI security platform (founded by Jack Cable, Ashwin Ramaswami)
- **Onyx** — AI security (founded by Maxim Bar Kogan, Gil Elbaz)
- **OpenEvidence** — AI for healthcare and clinical evidence (founded by Daniel Nadler, Zachary Ziegler)
- **RunSybil** — AI cybersecurity (founded by Ari Herbert-Voss)
- **Distributed Spectrum** — Wireless spectrum intelligence (founded by Alex Wulff, Isaac Struhl, Ben Harpe)
- **Sola** — Clean energy AI (founded by Jessica Wu, Neil Desmukh)
- **Latent** — Healthcare AI (founded by Rish Jain, Sri Somasundaram)

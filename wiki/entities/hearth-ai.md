# Hearth AI

The **first agentic CRM**, founded in **2022** by **Ashe Magalhaes**. Built on GPT-3.5 before structured outputs, JSON schema retrieval, and agentic infrastructure were available as stable products — making it a pioneering example of building AI-native products on fragile foundations.

## Founding Story

Hearth AI was conceived when LLMs were just becoming viable for product use cases but the infrastructure was still immature. Ashe describes "trying to wrangle" OpenAI's early API while building agentic workflows on GPT-3.5 — pushing for features like structured outputs and JSON schema retrieval that didn't yet exist.

Key insight: > *"Our users didn't care that LLMs were new. They wanted to see the flexibility of what was just then possible."* — The bar for user experience didn't lower just because the technology was new.

## Product Philosophy

- **Agentic-first**: AI agents at the core of the CRM, not bolted on as a feature
- **Relationship-centric**: Designed to enhance human connection, not replace it — agents handle administrative overhead so humans can focus on genuine engagement
- **Non-deterministic stability**: Built product-level reliability on top of non-deterministic LLM outputs, a challenge that defined the early AI product era

## Evolution

Hearth AI started as a CRM but evolved toward a broader vision of **agentic personal operating systems** — personal AI agents that manage context, memory, and follow-through across all domains of life. This evolution reflects Ashe's belief that AI agents are instruments for **presence and intentionality**, not just productivity.

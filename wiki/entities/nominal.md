# Nominal

Nominal is an AI-native ERP agentic platform that automates complex financial operations for mid-market and enterprise companies. Its AI agents handle transaction matching, reconciliations, consolidations, and variance analysis without requiring ERP migration.

## Key Facts
- Founded by second-time AI entrepreneurs Guy Leibovitz and Golan Kopichinsky, who previously built and sold Cognigo to NetApp (2019).
- Raised $20M Series A in July 2025 led by Next47 with Workday Ventures participation; total funding ~$30M.
- Eliminated over 50,000 hours of manual accounting work for customers in a single year.
- Achieved SOC 1 Type 2 certification — one of the first AI-native finance platforms to do so.

## Products & Technology
- **AI Finance Agents**: Flux Analysis Agents, Bank Reconciliation Agents, Transaction Matching Agents, Trigger Agents, Transaction Patrol Agents.
- **Shadow General Ledger**: Works alongside existing ERPs (Workday, D365, Oracle, SAP, Sage) without migration.
- **Multi-Entity Consolidation**: Automates intercompany eliminations, currency consolidation, and period-end close.

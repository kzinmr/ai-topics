# Malika Aubakirova

**Malika Aubakirova** is a researcher on the a16z (Andreessen Horowitz) infrastructure team. She co-authored the [[concepts/openrouter-state-of-ai-2025|State of AI 2025]] study with Alex Atallah (OpenRouter), analyzing 100 trillion tokens of real-world LLM usage via the OpenRouter platform.

## Key Contributions

- **State of AI 2025 Study:** Co-authored the landmark study analyzing 100T tokens of LLM interactions, identifying the "reasoning inflection point," the [[concepts/glass-slipper-effect|Glass Slipper Effect]], and the dominance of Programming/Roleplay usage categories.
- **Usage Category Taxonomy:** Developed the automated categorization system that classified billions of interactions into meaningful usage categories (Programming, Roleplay, Technology, Science, Health, Legal, etc.).
- **Market Archetype Framework:** Identified the four market archetypes: Mass-Market Volume, Premium Leaders, Efficient Giants, and Premium Specialists.

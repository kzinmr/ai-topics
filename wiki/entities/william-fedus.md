# William Fedus

**William Fedus** is an AI researcher and entrepreneur, formerly VP of Research at **OpenAI** where he helped lead the post-training efforts behind ChatGPT. He is the CEO and founder of **Periodic Labs** ([[entities/periodic-ai]]), an AI research company building an AI Scientist for automated scientific discovery.

## Background

Fedus spent years at OpenAI, rising to VP of Research. During his tenure, he played a key role in the **post-training** pipeline for ChatGPT — the RLHF and RL-based fine-tuning that transforms base models into aligned, capable assistants. This experience in large-scale RL training directly informs Periodic's research direction.

## Periodic Labs (2025–present)

As CEO of Periodic Labs, Fedus leads a physical research lab focused on:
- **Reinforcement learning at scale** on frontier LLMs with 1T+ parameters
- **AI Scientist** — autonomous systems for scientific discovery
- **Physical AI** — embodied AI and robotics applications

The company relies on high-performance GPU cloud infrastructure (SemiAnalysis' ClusterMAX) for its workloads.

See [[entities/periodic-ai]] for the full company profile.

## Key Contributions

- **Post-training for ChatGPT**: Led the RL fine-tuning and alignment pipeline that enabled ChatGPT's breakthrough capabilities
- **RL at scale**: Research on scaling reinforcement learning to trillion-parameter models
- **AI for science**: Vision for autonomous AI-driven scientific discovery

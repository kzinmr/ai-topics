# K. Eric Drexler

**K. Eric Drexler** is a molecular biologist and researcher at the Future of Humanity Institute, University of Oxford. He is best known for his work on molecular nanotechnology and his conceptualization of superintelligence.

## Focus Areas
- **Molecular Nanotechnology**: Pioneered the idea of building machines at the molecular scale.
- **Superintelligence**: Developed the term "superintelligence" and explored the risks and opportunities of rapidly advancing AI.
- **AI Safety & Alignment**: Early advocate for considering the raises risks associated with superintelligence.

## Key Concepts
- **Comprehensive AI Services (CAIS)**: A model where superintelligent agents are viewed as service-providing products rather than natural engines of progress.독- **Existential Risk**: The potential for superintelligent AI to lead to human extinction or permanent disadvantage.

Alex Volkov is an **AI Evangelist at Weights & Biases**, newsletter author, and podcast host. He operates the **ThursdAI** weekly AI news show and maintains an active Substack presence covering AI engineering, model releases, and industry analysis.

## ThursdAI Newsletter/Podcast

**ThursdAI** is a weekly AI news show described as the "Highest signal weekly AI news show." It covers:
- Major model releases and technical deep dives
- AI engineering patterns and best practices
- Industry analysis and commentary
- Practical AI deployment insights

The newsletter is curated by Alex Volkov and distributed via Substack, with episodes typically featuring:
- Technical breakdowns of new model releases
- Commentary on AI industry trends
- Links to high-signal research and blog posts
- Community discussion and analysis

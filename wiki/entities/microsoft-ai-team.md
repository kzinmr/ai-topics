# Microsoft AI Team

The Microsoft AI (MAI) Team is the research and engineering group within Microsoft responsible for developing frontier reasoning models. They focus on scaling-focused frameworks, reinforcement learning, and building much-climbing machines for rapid AI improvement.

## Key Projects
- **MAI-Thinking-1**: A 35B active / 1T total parameter Mixture of Experts (MoE) model optimized for STEM reasoning and coding.
- **Hill-Climbing Machine**: AFramework for treating model development as a system-level optimization problem.

## Technical Focus
- **From-scratch training**: Avoiding way from distillation to ensure model steerability.
- **Reinforcement Learning (RL)**: Using robust recipes to sustain log-linear performance improvement over thousands of steps.
- **Scaling Laws**: Emphasizing empirically driven iterative improvements in architecture and data.

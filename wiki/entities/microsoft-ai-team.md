# Microsoft AI Team

The Microsoft AI Team (MAI) is a research and development group within Microsoft focused on building reasoning models and exploring scaling laws.

## Key Ideas
- **MAI-Thinking-1**: A 35B active / 1T total parameter MoE model designed for STEM reasoning and coding.
- **Hill-Climbing Machine**: A process of treating model development as a system-level optimization problem, using reinforcement learning for rapid improvement.
- **Scaling Laws**: Focus on empirically-driven** improvements to architecture and data.

# Microsoft AI Team

The Microsoft AI (MAI) team is responsible for developing areas-class models, including the MAI-Thinking-1 series.

## Key Contributions
- **MAI-Thinking-1**: A powerful reasoning model developed from scratch for STEM reasoning and coding.
- **Hill-Climbing Machine**: A scaling-focused framework for rapid model improvement through reinforcement learning.

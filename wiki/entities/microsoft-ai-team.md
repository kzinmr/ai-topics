# Microsoft AI Team

The Microsoft AI (MAI) Team is the research group within Microsoft responsible for developing frontier reasoning models, including the MAI-Thinking-series.

## Key Contributions
- **MAI-Thinking-1**: A 35B active / 1T total parameter Mixture of Experts (MoE) model designed for STEM reasoning and coding.
- **Hill-Climbing Machine**: A framework for treating model development as a system-level optimization problem.

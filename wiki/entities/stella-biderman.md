# Stella Biderman

**Stella Biderman** (also known as Stella Rose Biderman) is a mathematician, AI researcher, and the Executive Director of [[entities/eleutherai|EleutherAI]]. She is a leading figure in the open-source AI movement, having led the development of several of the world's most powerful open generative models including GPT-Neo, GPT-NeoX-20B, BLOOM, Pythia, and OpenFold.

## Overview

Stella transitioned EleutherAI from a grassroots volunteer collective into a non-profit research institute in 2023. Her work focuses on making cutting-edge AI technologies accessible to academics, auditors, and non-profit researchers without requiring partnerships with the corporations that commercialize them.

## Education

- **M.S. in Computer Science**, Georgia Institute of Technology (2022)
- **B.S. in Mathematics with honors**, University of Chicago (2016)
- **B.A. in Philosophy**, University of Chicago (2016)

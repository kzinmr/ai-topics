# Fidji Simo

**Role:** CEO of AGI Deployment, OpenAI
**Affiliations:** Former CEO of Instacart; OpenAI (2024–present)

## Overview

Fidji Simo joined OpenAI as **CEO of AGI Deployment**, a newly created executive role responsible for overseeing OpenAI's product strategy and deployment. She was recruited from **Instacart**, where she served as CEO and led the company through significant growth and its eventual IPO.

## OpenAI Tenure

### CEO of AGI Deployment (2024–present)

Simo was brought in to lead OpenAI's product and deployment strategy during a period of intense competitive pressure from Anthropic (in coding) and Google (in consumer AI). Her role encompassed oversight of ChatGPT, Codex, and the company's broader product portfolio.

### Medical Leave (May 2026)

In May 2026, Simo took **medical leave** to focus on her health. During her absence, OpenAI co-founder and president **Greg Brockman** assumed interim oversight of product strategy. Simo worked directly with Brockman on organizational changes from afar, including the consolidation of ChatGPT, Codex, and the developer API into a unified product team.

OpenAI confirmed that Simo remains employed and expects her return, noting that she was actively involved in designing the reorganization despite being on leave.

## Instacart Background

Before joining OpenAI, Simo was CEO of Instacart, where she:
- Led the company through its public listing
- Oversaw expansion beyond grocery delivery into broader retail
- Built experience scaling consumer-facing technology platforms

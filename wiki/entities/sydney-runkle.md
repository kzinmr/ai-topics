# Sydney Runkle (@sydneyrunkle)

**Sydney Runkle** (handle `@sydneyrunkle`) is a software engineer at [[entities/langchain|LangChain]], working on Deep Agents infrastructure. She co-authored RubricMiddleware, a self-correcting grader loop middleware that enables agents to evaluate and iteratively correct their own work against defined criteria.

## Notable Works

- [**Introducing Rubrics: Build Agents that Evaluate and Correct Their Work**](https://x.com/i/article/2061868304654864384) (Jun 2, 2026) — Co-authored with Seshadri (@sseshadri43). Introduced RubricMiddleware for LangChain Deep Agents.

# Odyssey ML

**Odyssey** is an AI research company building **multimodal world models** — generative AI systems that learn from pixels, motion, and actions in large-scale video rather than text alone. Led by Oliver Cameron and Jeff Hawke.

## Products & Models

- **[[concepts/starchild-1]]** (May 2026): First real-time multimodal world model, generating synchronized audio + video with continuous user interaction
- **Odyssey-2**: Most powerful general-purpose world model; advances physical accuracy state-of-the-art
- **[[concepts/agora-1]]**: Multi-agent world model — multiple human or AI participants interact in the same simulation in real-time
- **PROWL**: Adversarial RL framework where an RL agent explores game environments to improve world model performance

## Philosophy

Grounded in the empiricist tradition ("Nothing is in the intellect that was not first in the senses" — Aquinas). World models must learn the full multimodal richness of the world to revolutionize robotics, education, gaming, healthcare, and defense.

## Team

Core contributors include Ahmad Nazeri, Amogh Adishesha, Jenny Seidenschwarz, Richard Shen, Sarah King, Tobiah Rex, Vighnesh Birodkar, and 20+ others. Leadership: Jeff Hawke, Oliver Cameron.

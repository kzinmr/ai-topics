# MAI-Image-2.5

**MAI-Image-2.5** is Microsoft's strongest image generation model, released May 26, 2026 by the MAI Superintelligence Team. It ranked third on the Arena text-to-image leaderboard at launch.

## Key Capabilities

- Wide range of artistic styles
- Precise instruction following
- **Significantly improved text rendering** — words in posters, labels, and brand materials are sharper
- Detailed, coherent images with strong visual reasoning
- Better understanding of objects, scene structure, lighting, scale, and spatial relationships

## Improvements Over MAI-Image-2

- Major gains in text rendering — words on posters, labels, packaging
- Stylized illustration quality
- Commercial and brand imagery polish
- More deliberate scene composition

## Availability

- Available on Arena at launch
- Coming to MAI Playground and Foundry within two weeks of launch

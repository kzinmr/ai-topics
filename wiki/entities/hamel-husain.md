# Hamel Husain

AI researcher, creator of open-source tools, and co-creator (with Shreya Shankar) of the world's most popular course on AI evals. Has trained over 2,000 PMs and engineers at companies including OpenAI and Anthropic.

## Contributions

- Co-created the world's most popular AI evals course with Shreya Shankar
- Trained 2,000+ PMs and engineers in systematic AI evaluation techniques
- Open source contributor and creator of various AI tools
- Developer of frameworks for building reproducible AI evaluations

## Core Ideas

1. **Reproducibility is key** — Evaluations must be deterministic and repeatable
2. **Systematic approach** — Move from ad-hoc testing to structured evaluation frameworks
3. **Practical tooling** — Build accessible tools for non-experts to perform quality checks
4. **Data-driven development** — Use evaluation results to guide model improvements

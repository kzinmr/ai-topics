# Hailey Schoelkopf

**Hailey Schoelkopf** (she/her) is a Research Scientist at [[entities/eleutherai|EleutherAI]] and currently a Member of Technical Staff at Anthropic. She is the primary maintainer of the [[concepts/llm-evaluation-harness|LM Evaluation Harness]] (lm-eval), the industry-standard tool for reproducible LLM evaluation.

## Overview

Hailey's research focuses on three main areas:
- **Rigorous, reliable evaluation** of LLMs and generative models — standards for reproducible evaluation, complex task measurement, and a science of capability testing
- **Distributed training engineering** — making large-scale training fast and accessible
- **Scalable model development** — understanding and systematizing the transformation of compute into performance

## Notable Projects

### LM Evaluation Harness (lm-eval)
Primary maintainer of the open-source library for independent, reproducible, and extensible LLM evaluation. Used by Hugging Face's Open LLM Leaderboard and organizations like NVIDIA, Cohere, and Mosaic ML.

### Pythia Suite
Co-authored the Pythia suite of 16 LLMs (70M to 12B parameters), a highly controlled setup for studying LLM training dynamics. Published at ICML 2023.

### Llemma
Engineering for continued pretraining of the Llemma base models for mathematics.

# Chris Hayduk

Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE) on the Life Sciences team at **OpenAI**, where he works with pharma and biotech organizations deploying frontier AI systems for drug discovery, clinical development, and the broader life sciences stack.

Previously: Machine Learning Engineer at Meta (ads ranking models, graph neural networks, LLMs), Lead ML Engineer for Drug Discovery at Deloitte (biomedical knowledge graphs, in-silico drug optimization).

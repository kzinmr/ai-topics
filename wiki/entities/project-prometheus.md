# Project Prometheus

## Overview

**Project Prometheus** is a secret AI startup co-founded by Jeff Bezos, reportedly raising **$10 billion at a $38 billion valuation** as of April 2026. The company focuses on **"Physical AI"** — AI systems designed for real-world industrial applications including manufacturing and aerospace.

## Key Facts

- **Founder**: Jeff Bezos (Amazon co-founder)
- **Focus**: Physical AI for industrial applications (manufacturing, aerospace)
- **Funding**: ~$10 billion raised
- **Valuation**: ~$38 billion
- **Status**: Secret/stealth mode as of April 2026
- **Talent Strategy**: Actively poaching engineers from OpenAI and Google DeepMind

## Significance

Project Prometheus represents Bezos's second major AI bet after Amazon's existing AI infrastructure (AWS, Alexa). The focus on **physical-world AI** rather than purely software-based models suggests a thesis that the next AI breakthrough will come from systems that interact with the physical environment — robotics, manufacturing optimization, aerospace systems.

The massive $10B raise and $38B valuation position it among the largest AI startup fundraises, competing with xAI, OpenAI, and other frontier AI companies.

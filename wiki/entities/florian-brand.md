## Research Focus

- **LLM Evaluation**: Benchmarking and evaluating LLMs across capabilities
- **Open Models**: Tracking and analyzing open-weight model releases at Interconnects
- **Agent Evals**: Particularly interested in BFCL (Berkeley Function Calling Leaderboard) and IFEval for small models — "A small model shouldn't solve math, but it should adhere to my prompts and do API calls"
- **OSWorld Critique**: Documented that OSWorld benchmarks are not standardized — "everyone benches a different set of prompts, so you can't really compare scores"

## Prime Intellect Role

At Prime Intellect, leads evaluation infrastructure for the Lab product, ensuring models trained on the platform meet rigorous benchmarks.

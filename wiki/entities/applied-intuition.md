# Applied Intuition

Applied Intuition is an American autonomous vehicle and physical AI software company headquartered in Sunnyvale, California. It provides a comprehensive autonomy stack — simulation, validation, Vehicle OS, and data tools — for automakers, defense contractors, trucking, mining, construction, and agriculture.

## Key Facts

- Founded in 2017 by Qasar Younis (former COO of Y Combinator) and Peter Ludwig (former Google engineer)
- Valued at $15 billion as of 2025, with estimated $800 million in revenue
- Serves 18 of the 20 largest global OEMs, including Volkswagen and General Motors
- Defense contracts with the U.S. Army for self-driving military vehicles
- Offices across 12+ global locations including Stuttgart, Munich, Tokyo, Bangalore, Seoul

## Products & Technology

The platform includes AI-powered ADAS/AD toolchain for simulation, data exploration, and validation; Vehicle OS for AI-defined vehicle experiences; and autonomy stacks specialized for off-road, trucking, and defense applications. Hardware-agnostic, works across sensors, compute platforms, and vehicle architectures.

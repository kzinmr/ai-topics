## Bio

Hi, I’m
Matheus Lima
, and I made a blog. Why? Honestly, because it’s cheaper than therapy and more fun than yelling at my terminal.
The name
Terrible Software
? It’s a nod to all the times I’ve stared at my code and thought,
“Maybe I should’ve chosen another profession.”
Turns out, embracing the chaos is way more productive than pretending I’ve got it all figured out.
Here, I’ll share stories about software,
engineering leadership
,
computer science
, and
tech adventures
. If you’ve ever copied and pasted a solution from Stack Overflow without fully understanding it, you’ll fit right in.

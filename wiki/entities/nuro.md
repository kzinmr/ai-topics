# Nuro

Autonomous vehicle technology company specializing in Level 4 self-driving systems. Originally built custom autonomous delivery vehicles; since 2024 licenses its Nuro Driver platform to automakers and mobility providers for robotaxis and personally owned autonomous vehicles.

## Key Facts
- Founded by ex-Google/Waymo engineers Jiajun Zhu and Dave Ferguson
- First company to receive NHTSA autonomous vehicle exemption
- Partnerships with Kroger, Domino's, FedEx, 7-Eleven, Uber, and Lucid
- Raised $2B+ total funding; Series E; ~700 employees; headquartered in Mountain View, CA
- Pivoted in 2024 from custom delivery bots to licensing autonomous driving software

## Products & Technology
- **Nuro Driver**: Level 4 autonomous driving system for robotaxis and personally owned vehicles
- **Nuro Toolkit**: Integration tools for automaker partners
- Developed Universal Autonomy Model for scalable self-driving AI

# Omri Weinstein

**Role:** Co-founder and CEO, Pearl Research Labs
**Affiliations:** Pearl Research Labs
**Themes:** Proof of Useful Work, AI inference cost reduction, blockchain-crypto-economic AI infrastructure

## Overview

Omri Weinstein is the co-founder and CEO of **Pearl Research Labs**, a research company developing the **Pearl Network** — a blockchain protocol that uses **Proof of Useful Work** to offset the cost of AI training and inference workloads.

## Pearl Research Labs

### Proof of Useful Work

Weinstein's key innovation is the **Pearl Network**, which generates cryptocurrency (¶PRL) by obtaining strong proofs of matrix multiplications that occur during AI model training and inference. This approach:
- Turns otherwise "wasted" GPU computation into a revenue-generating activity
- Simultaneously secures a blockchain while performing useful AI work
- Changes the unit economics of AI by allowing every GPU cycle to produce a native digital asset

### Together AI Partnership (May 2026)

In May 2026, Pearl Research Labs announced an exclusive partnership with **Together AI**:
- Launched a discounted inference endpoint for **Gemma-4-31B-it-pearl**
- Over **25% cost reduction** offset by future value of crypto emissions
- As ¶PRL spot price increases, the endpoint price is expected to decrease further
- Represents the first product integrating the Pearl Network

### Vision

Weinstein's thesis is that AI's economic bottleneck is compute cost, and that crypto-economic mechanisms can subsidize AI workloads, making them more accessible. This bridges the worlds of blockchain infrastructure and AI deployment.

# Xiaomi (MiMo AI)

Chinese technology company best known for smartphones and electric vehicles. Through its MiMo AI division, Xiaomi has emerged as a significant contributor to open-source AI, releasing the [[entities/xiaomi-mimo|MiMo family]] of large language models under the MIT license.

## AI Models

Xiaomi's MiMo model family has rapidly evolved through multiple generations:

- **MiMo-V2-Flash** (Dec 2025): Initial release
- **MiMo-V2-Pro** (Mar 2026): 1T parameters, 42B active, 1M context
- **MiMo-V2.5-Pro** (Apr 2026): Flagship agentic coding model — 1.02T params, 42B active, comparable to Claude Opus 4.6 on SWE-bench Pro
- **MiMo-V2.5** (Apr 2026): Native multimodal variant — 310B total, 15B active, supports video/audio/image/text

All models released under MIT license with weights on Hugging Face.

## Significance

Xiaomi represents the emergence of Chinese consumer electronics companies as serious AI model developers, alongside [[entities/deepseek|DeepSeek]], [[entities/alibaba|Alibaba (Qwen)]], and [[concepts/glm-5-1|Zhipu AI (GLM)]].

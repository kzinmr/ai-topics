# Romain Huet

Head of Developer Experience at **OpenAI**. Host of the **Builders Unscripted** interview series, where he talks with developers building on the frontier of AI-assisted software development. Known as **@romainhuet** on X/Twitter.

## Bio

Romain Huet leads Developer Experience at OpenAI, focusing on helping developers adopt and build with OpenAI's tools — particularly **Codex** (the CLI coding agent) and the broader OpenAI API platform. His role bridges the gap between OpenAI's product teams and the developer community, surfacing builder needs and showcasing real-world use cases.

He previously worked on developer relations and platform teams across the tech industry, and brings a builder-first perspective to OpenAI's developer outreach.

## Builders Unscripted

Huet created and hosts **[[concepts/builders-unscripted|Builders Unscripted]]**, an OpenAI-produced interview series that spotlights "the stories behind real projects and the mindset that makes them possible." The series tagline: *"You can just build things."*

### Episodes

- **Ep. 1 — Peter Steinberger, Creator of OpenClaw** (Feb 2026): Interview with Peter Steinberger prior to his joining OpenAI. Covers OpenClaw's viral growth, building with Codex, and the philosophy of AI-native development. [[raw/articles/2026-02-24_openai_builders-unscripted-ep1-peter-steinberger]]
- **Ep. 2 — Ashe Magalhaes, Founder of Hearth AI** (Apr 2026): Interview with Ashe Magalhaes, founder of the first agentic CRM. Covers solar car racing, builder-as-artist philosophy, AI and human connection, and agentic personal OS. [[raw/articles/2026-04-10_openai_builders-unscripted-ep2-ashe-magalhaes]]

## Key Ideas

- **Builder-first developer experience**: Focus on what developers actually need to build with AI, not just API documentation
- **Unscripted format**: Conversations that reveal the real stories and mindsets behind successful AI projects
- **Codex advocacy**: Championing the shift from traditional coding to agent-assisted development workflows

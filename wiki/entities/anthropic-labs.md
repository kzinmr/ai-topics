# Anthropic Labs

Anthropic's experimental product division, responsible for launching products outside the core Claude AI assistant.

## Overview

Anthropic Labs is the division within Anthropic focused on building productized experiments — features and tools that extend Claude's capabilities beyond the chat interface. The first product from Anthropic Labs is [Claude Design](claude-design), released April 17, 2026.

## Products

### Claude Design
- Visual design collaboration tool powered by Claude Opus 4.7
- First product launched from Anthropic Labs
- Research preview as of April 2026

## Context

Anthropic Labs represents a strategic expansion of Anthropic beyond the single-product (Claude chat) model. By creating a dedicated experimental division, Anthropic can productize features that would otherwise remain as internal capabilities.

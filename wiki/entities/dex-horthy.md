# Dex Horthy (@dexhorthy)

| | |
|---|---|

## Bio

Dex Horthy is an observer and tracker of emergent patterns in the agent harness ecosystem. Addy Osmani cited Horthy as one of the key voices "tracking the pattern as it emerges" alongside Vivek Trivedy, HumanLayer, Anthropic Engineering, and Birgitta Böckeler.

## Key Contributions

### Emergent Harness Pattern Tracking

Horthy has been monitoring and cataloging the convergence of agent harness patterns across the industry. As Osmani noted:

> "If you look at the top coding agents today, they look more like each other than their underlying models do."

Horthy's tracking focuses on identifying:
- Load-bearing scaffolding patterns that recur across harnesses
- Convergence dynamics between independently-developed harnesses
- Emerging architectural solutions to common agent failures

### Relationship to Harness Engineering Discourse

Horthy is part of the core group of voices (alongside Trivedy, Osmani, HumanLayer, Anthropic, Böckeler) that converged on the harness engineering framing in early-to-mid 2026 — recognizing that the harness, not the model, is where agency lives.

# Factory

AI research lab bringing autonomy to software engineering through "Droids" — autonomous AI agents that integrate into existing development workflows. Droids handle refactors, migrations, incident response, and code reviews across IDE, CLI, and CI/CD.

## Key Facts
- Founded April 2023 by Matan Grinberg (ex-theoretical physicist, UC Berkeley PhD dropout) and Eno Reyes (ex-Hugging Face ML engineer)
- Raised $150M Series C at $1.5B valuation from Khosla, Sequoia, Blackstone, Insight Partners, NEA
- Focused on enterprise legacy codebase migrations — "code bases older than the people working on them"
- Droids have read/write access to repos, participate in Slack/GitHub discussions, submit PRs
- Backed by McKinsey partnership for enterprise AI transformation

## Products & Technology
- **Droids**: Autonomous AI agents that accept high-level objectives (refactors, migrations) and execute independently
- Integration with IDEs, CLI, CI/CD pipelines, Slack, Microsoft Teams, GitHub, and Linear
- Agent parallelization for multi-OS software updates and maintenance

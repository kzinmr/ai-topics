# Chan Zuckerberg Initiative (CZI)

The **Chan Zuckerberg Initiative (CZI)** is a philanthropic organization founded by Mark Zuckerberg and Priscilla Chan. Its **Biohub** biomedical institute develops open-source AI tools for biological research.

## Key Projects

### ESMFold2 / ESM Atlas (May 30, 2026)
- **1.1 billion predicted protein structures** (surpasses [[entities/deepmind|DeepMind]] AlphaFold DB's 200M by 800M+)
- **6.8 billion protein sequences** cataloged
- Fully open source, no commercial restrictions
- Includes metagenomic sequences from soil, ocean, environments not in AlphaFold DB
- Lead researcher: Alex Rives

### ESM Protein Language Model (2024)
- Trained on billions of proteins from across the tree of life
- Foundation for ESMFold2 structure predictions

### Design Capability
- Designed new antibodies and proteins for cancer/immunological targets
- Lab-verified: high proportion of designs worked as predicted
- Found structural similarities between CRISPR microbial defence proteins and gene-editing protein from soil fungus

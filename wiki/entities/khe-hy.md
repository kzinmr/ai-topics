# Khe Hy

Khe Hy is a writer and thinker focused on organizational AI adoption strategies.

## Key Work

### "How to Get Your Company AI-Pilled" (April 2026)
- 7,438 bookmarks, 1.59M impressions on Twitter/X
- Framework for organizational AI adoption
- Key principle: "Treat AI proficiency as a learning curve, not a light switch"
- "The bottleneck is never the tool. It's always the first person in the room willing to look stupid using it out loud."

### Core Recommendations
1. Get people to the 'Aha' moment as fast as possible
2. Crowdsource what's working internally
3. First person to visibly use AI in meetings breaks the adoption curve
4. Executives should narrate prompts live
5. Shift from "we have AI tools" to "we use AI to think"

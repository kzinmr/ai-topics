# Metronome

Usage-based billing and monetization infrastructure platform. Provides real-time metering, pricing, billing, and reporting for software companies — trusted by OpenAI, NVIDIA, and other fast-growing AI businesses.

## Key Facts
- Founded in 2020 by ex-Dropbox leaders Scott Woody and Kevin Liu
- OpenAI uses Metronome for scalable billing infrastructure (API credits, usage tracking)
- Acquired by Stripe to build the future of monetization infrastructure
- Raised $30M+; backed by leading venture investors
- Pioneers outcome-based pricing and dynamic pricing models for the AI era

## Products & Technology
- Real-time usage metering and entitlement infrastructure at scale
- Flexible pricing levers supporting usage-based, seat-based, and hybrid models
- Customer lifecycle notifications and monetization intelligence

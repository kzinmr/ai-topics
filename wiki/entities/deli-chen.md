# Deli Chen

Deli Chen is a Deep Learning Researcher at [[entities/deepseek]] and the creator of the [[concepts/auto-research]] framework. He is known for his work on autonomous AI research agents and reinforcement learning.

## Background

- **Affiliation**: Deep Learning Researcher at DeepSeek
- **Education**: BS and MS from Peking University (PKU)
- **Research Focus**: AI agents for research automation, reinforcement learning, self-play, and continual learning
- **X/Twitter**: [@victor207755822](https://x.com/victor207755822)

## Key Contributions

### AutoResearch Framework

Chen developed the AutoResearch framework, an AI agent system that automates the entire research pipeline for reinforcement learning. The framework can:
- Autonomously plan GPU experiments
- Write and debug code
- Run experiments on large-scale models (e.g., DeepSeek 285B)
- Summarize conclusions without human intervention

The framework was open-sourced on June 17, 2026, along with the 4th survey paper on self-play techniques.

### Self-Play Research

Inspired by AlphaZero, Chen's research explores how models can discover globally optimal solutions through self-play rather than relying solely on prior knowledge. This work represents a step toward continual learning research.

### GRPO Implementation

The AutoResearch Agent uses Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO) as its primary tool for RL training, demonstrating the viability of automated RL experiment pipelines.

## Research Philosophy

Chen emphasizes that this is a personal research project, unaffiliated with any organization, and all views are his own. He sees the AutoResearch framework as the beginning of a continual learning research journey.

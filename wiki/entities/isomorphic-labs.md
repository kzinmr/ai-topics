## Overview

Isomorphic Labs is an Alphabet subsidiary applying AI to reimagine drug discovery. Founded by DeepMind's Demis Hassabis, the company leverages its proprietary **IsoDDE** (AI Drug Design Engine) to accelerate biomedical breakthroughs across multiple therapeutic areas and drug modalities.

## $2.1B Series B (May 2026)

In May 2026, Isomorphic Labs closed a **$2.1 billion Series B** round — the second-largest biotech raise in history:

| Investor | Role |
|----------|------|
| **Thrive Capital** | Lead investor |
| **Alphabet / GV** | Existing backer |
| **MGX** | New investor |
| **Temasek** | New investor |
| **CapitalG** | New investor |
| **UK Sovereign AI Fund** | New investor |

## Pharma Partnerships

Isomorphic Labs has established partnerships with major pharmaceutical companies:
- **Eli Lilly** (Lilly)
- **Novartis**
- **Johnson & Johnson** (J&J)

## Strategic Significance

Isomorphic Labs represents Google/Alphabet's bet on AI-driven drug discovery as a major commercial application of AI research. The $2.1B raise signals strong investor confidence in AI's ability to transform pharmaceutical R&D.

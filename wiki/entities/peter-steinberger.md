# Peter Steinberger

## Overview

Peter Steinberger (@steipete) is an Austrian software developer best known as the creator of **OpenClaw** (originally "Clawdbot", briefly "Moltbot"), an open-source personal AI agent framework that gained significant traction in the AI agent community.

## Key Events

### OpenClaw Creation & Growth
- Created OpenClaw as an open-source AI agent framework connecting to Claude and other models
- Project gained cult following, with 135,000+ instances running at peak
- Renamed from "Clawdbot" to "Moltbot" after Anthropic trademark complaint, then to "OpenClaw"

### Joining OpenAI (Feb 2026)
- Announced departure from OpenClaw to join OpenAI on February 14, 2026
- Sam Altman publicly welcomed him to "drive the next generation of personal agents"
- OpenClaw moved to an open-source foundation with OpenAI's continued support

### Anthropic-OpenClaw Conflict (Apr 2026)
- Anthropic blocked third-party tools (including OpenClaw) from Claude subscriptions on April 4, 2026
- Steinberger & Dave Morin negotiated a 1-week delay in enforcement
- Steinberger's own Claude account was temporarily suspended on April 12, 2026 ("suspicious signals"), restored within hours
- Publicly criticized Anthropic: "First they copy some popular features into their closed harness, then they lock out open source"

# Bedrock Robotics

Bedrock Robotics brings advanced autonomy to the construction industry, retrofitting existing heavy equipment (excavators, bulldozers, loaders) with self-driving technology. Founded by former Waymo leaders, it applies autonomous vehicle expertise to the built world.

## Key Facts
- Founded in 2024 by three former Waymo engineering leaders (Boris Sofman, Kevin Peterson, Ajay Gummalla) who helped pioneer fully autonomous vehicles on public roads.
- Fourth co-founder Tom Eliaz previously scaled Segment's engineering systems through $3.2B acquisition by Twilio.
- Raised $350M across Seed, Series A, and Series B rounds.
- Headquartered in San Francisco.
- Backed by C4 Ventures (February 2026 investment).

## Products & Technology
- **Bedrock Operator**: Retrofits existing construction equipment with sensors, compute, and ML-powered autonomy.
- **Environmental Understanding**: Machines navigate hills, trenches, obstacles, and terrain while shaping land to precise plans.
- **ML-Driven Autonomy**: Large-scale ML models trained across projects nationwide for expert-level quality.
- **Real-Time Intelligence**: Live progress and performance metrics for contractors.

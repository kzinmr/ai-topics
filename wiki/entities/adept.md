# Adept AI

AI research and product lab building general intelligence by enabling humans and computers to collaborate. Develops AI agents that automate complex software workflows using existing tools and APIs.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Private (VC-backed, Unicorn) |
| **Founded** | 2022 |
| **Leadership** | David Luan (Co-Founder & CEO), Kelsey Szot (Co-Founder & President), Ashish Vaswani (Co-Founder), Niki Parmar (Co-Founder & CTO) |
| **Key Products** | ACT-1 (large transformer for digital tool use), Adept Workflow Language (AWL), Fuyu (multimodal model), Persimmon-8B |
| **Website** | [adept.ai](https://www.adept.ai) |
| **Tech Blog** | [adept.ai/blog](https://www.adept.ai/blog) |

## Key Facts
- Founded by ex-OpenAI and Google researchers; David Luan previously led engineering at OpenAI (GPT-2, GPT-3)
- Co-founders Ashish Vaswani and Niki Parmar co-invented the Transformer architecture at Google (2017)
- Raised $350M Series B in March 2023 led by General Catalyst and Spark Capital
- Backed by NVIDIA, Microsoft, and Greylock among others
- Pioneered the "AI agent that uses software like a human" paradigm

## Products & Technology
- **ACT-1**: Large transformer trained to use digital tools (browsers, SaaS apps) and execute high-level user requests
- **Fuyu**: Multimodal model family designed for digital agents; Fuyu-8B open-sourced
- **Persimmon-8B**: Fully permissively-licensed language model (<10B params)

# Ahmed Awadallah

Research manager at **Microsoft Research, AI Frontiers** lab. Leads the team that developed [[echo-rl|ECHO]], providing space, GPUs, and research infrastructure for pursuing novel ideas in agent reinforcement learning.

- **Affiliation**: Microsoft Research AI Frontiers
- **Role**: Research manager / lead

## Research Focus

- Reinforcement learning for AI agents
- CLI/terminal agent training
- World models and environment prediction
- Agent infrastructure and scaling

## Notable Work

### ECHO: Terminal Agents Learn World Models for Free (2026-05)
Research lead on ECHO, co-authored with [[entities/vaishnavi-shrivastava|Vaishnavi Shrivastava]], [[entities/piero-kauffmann|Piero Kauffmann]], and [[entities/dimitris-papailiopoulos|Dimitris Papailiopoulos]]. Paper: [arxiv.org/abs/2605.24517](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.24517).

As Dimitris put it: "Thanks to... Ahmed Awadallah for giving us space — and GPUs — to chase ideas like this, even when they start as just a research itch."

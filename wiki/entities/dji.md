# DJI

## Overview

Chinese drone manufacturer with an "iron grip" on the global drone market. Despite U.S. regulatory efforts, DJI's market dominance remains largely intact.

## Market Position

- Dominant global share in consumer and professional drone markets
- U.S. regulators unable to loosen DJI's market position despite security concerns
- Founded by Wang Xingxing, who later founded [[unitree-robotics]]

## Technology

- Advanced AI-powered navigation and autonomous flight
- Sensor fusion expertise later applied to robotics by former employees
- Manufacturing scale and supply chain dominance

## Relationships

- [[china-ai-industry]] — Foundation of China's hardware-AI ecosystem
- [[unitree-robotics]] — Founder Wang Xingxing previously worked at DJI
- [[xpeng]] — Related in autonomous systems space

## Open Questions

- How long can U.S. regulatory pressure affect DJI's market position given its technological and cost advantages?
- What is DJI's role in China's broader embodied AI strategy?

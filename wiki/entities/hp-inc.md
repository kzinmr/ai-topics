## Overview

HP Inc. is a global technology company providing personal computing, printing, and enterprise solutions. In June 2026, HP launched a **Frontier strategic partnership** with [[entities/openai|OpenAI]], scaling AI deployment across customer-facing solutions, software development, and enterprise operations.

## OpenAI Frontier Partnership (June 2026)

HP Inc. launched a Frontier strategic partnership with OpenAI to deploy frontier AI across its global operations. Following successful pilots beginning in February 2026, the partnership focuses on deploying AI across:

- **Customer and partner-facing solutions**: Pricing, partner portal, store, and customer support workflows. AI agents to provide always-on guidance across program navigation, business information, and partner operations management for HP's 100,000+ partner ecosystem.
- **Customer telemetry insights and reporting**: Using device telemetry, support knowledge, and operational data to reason across fleet health signals.
- **Employee productivity**: Broad knowledge work including research, analysis, ideation, and workflow automation via ChatGPT and Codex.
- **Software development**: Codex supporting modernization, planning, UI scaffolding, and parallel software-delivery tasks.

### Pilot Results

Early pilot successes included:
- One engineer used OpenAI models to move through **122 pull requests across 43 projects** in weeks
- A security team remediated several software bugs in a day — work estimated to have otherwise taken up to a month
- Security capacity unlocked: directional estimate of roughly **82 hours/week** of security-team capacity

### Frontier as Connective Layer

HP is using OpenAI Frontier as a unified platform to manage AI deployment at enterprise scale: understanding what is running, what context each system can use, how actions are governed, and how outcomes are evaluated. Frontier provides the operating model — connecting access, context, deployment, and evaluation as work moves from pilots toward production.

### Workforce Experience Platform (WXP)

HP's WXP platform offers fleet management for CIOs. Using Frontier, HP is exploring how device telemetry, support knowledge, operational objects, schemas, and runbooks can help AI reason across fleet health signals — investigating crashes, Wi-Fi issues, and app hangs — eventually supporting grounded remediation.

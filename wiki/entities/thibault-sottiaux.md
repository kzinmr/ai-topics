# Thibault Sottiaux

**Role:** Head of Codex, OpenAI → Leader of Core Product and Platform Teams
**Affiliations:** OpenAI

## Overview

Thibault Sottiaux is an OpenAI executive who led the development of **Codex**, OpenAI's AI coding agent. In May 2026, as part of OpenAI's product reorganization, he was tapped to lead the company's **core product and platform teams**, consolidating oversight of ChatGPT, Codex, and the developer-facing API under a unified leadership structure.

## Key Contributions

### Codex Leadership

Sottiaux was instrumental in building Codex into one of OpenAI's fastest-growing products. Under his leadership:
- Codex became a major revenue driver for OpenAI
- The product expanded from CLI to multi-surface (desktop, IDE, web)
- It gained agentic capabilities for autonomous software development

### "Super App" Initiative

Sottiaux is one of the leaders overseeing development of OpenAI's forthcoming **"super app"** — a unified desktop application that combines:
- **Codex** (AI coding agent)
- **ChatGPT** (conversational AI)
- **Atlas** (OpenAI's web browser)

This represents OpenAI's strategy to consolidate its product offerings into a single, integrated experience.

## May 2026 Reorganization

As part of the May 2026 product consolidation:
- Sottiaux's role expanded from Codex-only to **core product and platform** leadership
- This reflects OpenAI's view that Codex is increasingly powering both consumer and enterprise offerings
- The reorganization aimed to "execute with maximum focus toward the agentic future"

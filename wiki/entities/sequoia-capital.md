# Sequoia Capital

**Sequoia Capital** is one of the world's leading venture capital firms, founded in 1972. In 2026, the firm has been at the forefront of articulating the AI-native business model shift through partner [[entities/julien-bek|Julien Bek]]'s "Services: The New Software" thesis.

## AI Investment Thesis (2026)

Sequoia's most notable intellectual contribution to the 2026 AI landscape is the **Service-as-Software** framework, authored by London-based partner Julien Bek (March 5, 2026):

- The next $1T company will be a **software company masquerading as a services firm**
- AI has crossed the threshold from assisting knowledge workers (Copilots) to replacing the work itself (Autopilots)
- The addressable market shifts from software spend (1x) to labor/services spend (6x)
- The optimal wedge is **outsourced intelligence tasks** — vendor swaps, not reorganizations

### Portfolio Companies in AI-Native Services

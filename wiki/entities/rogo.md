# Rogo

Rogo is an enterprise AI platform purpose-built for financial services, automating research, analysis, and document creation for investment banks, private equity firms, and hedge funds. It helps finance professionals build slide decks, generate company profiles, draft investment memos, and perform competitive benchmarking — all within a secure, compliance-ready environment.

## Key Facts

- Founded by Gabriel Stengel, John Willett (Princeton classmates), and Tumas Rackaitis (ex-hedge fund trader)
- Raised $165M+; $75M Series C in January 2026 at $750M valuation led by Sequoia
- Revenue grew from $2M (2024) to $15M+ (2025)
- 25,000+ users across 150 firms including Moelis, Lazard, Tiger Global
- Partners include Google Cloud, AWS, Fitch Solutions, Third Bridge
- Achieved EU AI Act compliance and ISO/IEC 42001 certification

## Products & Technology

Rogo's platform integrates a firm's proprietary data with 65M+ external sources (SEC filings, PitchBook, S&P Global, FactSet, Preqin, news wires). It generates analyst-ready outputs: slide decks, company profiles, investment memos, competitive benchmarks. Runs on Google Cloud with Vertex AI and Spanner; also uses Amazon Bedrock. Features autonomous security monitoring (Sisyphus). Expanded to European markets via Plux acquisition.

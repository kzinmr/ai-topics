# Brain Co.

AI platform and applications company building bridges between foundational AI models and the world's most important institutions. Focused on applied intelligence for governments, supply chains, insurance, and hospitality.

## Key Facts
- Co-founded by Jared Kushner, Elad Gil, and Luis Videgaray; raised $30M
- Acquired Serene AI (mental health AI companion) in September 2024
- Partnered with OpenAI for foundational model access
- Delivers 30% lower supply chain costs, 99% reliability for institutional clients
- Blog features technical deep-dives on AI/ML including Bayesian networks, voice agent architectures, and LLM explainability

## Products & Technology
- Supply chain optimization using stochastic/probabilistic simulation
- LLM-generated rules engines for explainability in regulated industries
- Computer vision + LLM hybrid architectures for information extraction

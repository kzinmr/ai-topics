# AgiBot

## Overview

Chinese humanoid robot manufacturer. Second-largest Chinese humanoid robot seller after Unitree.

## 2025 Shipments

- **5,168 humanoid units** shipped in 2025
- China controlled ~90% of global humanoid shipments in 2025
- Unitree (5,500 units) + AgiBot (5,168 units) outpaced every Western rival combined

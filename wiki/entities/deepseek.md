# DeepSeek

## Overview

Chinese open-source LLM provider that has driven **cost disruption** in the AI industry. Exemplifies China's open-source AI strategy enabling rapid global uptake.

## Strategy

- Open-source models with widespread free accessibility
- Open-source feedback loop: adoption → rapid iteration → further adoption → reinforced industrial dominance
- Rapid global uptake via open-source channels

## Market Impact

- Cost disruption in AI model pricing
- Part of China's broader open-source AI ecosystem alongside Alibaba's Qwen (100,000+ Hugging Face derivatives)
- Recognized in U.S. congressional hearings on national security risks of PRC AI (March 2026)

# Marcus Moretti

**Marcus Moretti** is the General Manager of [[entities/every-inc|Spiral]] (Every's AI writing partner). He is the author of the widely-circulated guide on *Agent-native Product Management*, which introduced the `/ce-strategy` and `/ce-product-pulse` skills as part of Every's [[concepts/compound-engineering-every|Compound Engineering]] plugin.

## Background

- **Role:** General Manager, Spiral
- **Company:** [[entities/every-inc|Every Inc.]]
- **X/Twitter:** [@tryspiral](https://x.com/tryspiral)
- **Byline:** every.to/@marcus_fd8302_1

## Notable Work

- **"A Guide to Agent-native Product Management"** (Every, 2026) — Comprehensive guide on how PMs can use AI agents (Claude Code, Codex) for strategy, planning, and monitoring
- **"Claude Code for Product Managers"** (Every, May 1, 2026) — Companion essay on using Claude Code for the full product lifecycle

## Philosophy

Moretti operates as a "two-slice team" (coined by [[entities/every-inc|Every]]): a single person responsible for code, customer support, marketing, and product management, enabled entirely by AI agents. He argues that PM work has shifted from 20/80 planning-to-execution to **80% planning and 20% execution**, and that "the conversation is the work."

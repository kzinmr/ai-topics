# Periodic AI

**Periodic AI** (also known as **Periodic Labs**) is an AI research company with a physical lab focused on **reinforcement learning at scale** and **AI-driven scientific discovery**. Led by CEO **William Fedus** (ex-VP of Research at OpenAI), Periodic operates a physical research lab running RL experiments on frontier LLMs.

## Leadership

Periodic was founded by **William Fedus**, formerly VP of Research at OpenAI where he helped lead post-training efforts behind ChatGPT. His leadership brings deep expertise in large-scale RL training to the company's research agenda.

## Key Work

### AI Scientist for Scientific Discovery
Periodic is building an "AI Scientist" — an autonomous system capable of conducting novel scientific research. This positions Periodic at the intersection of RL scaling and automated scientific discovery.

### Scaling RL on Frontier LLMs (ICLR 2026)
At ICLR 2026, [[entities/sheshansh-agrawal]] presented "Scaling RL on Frontier LLMs with 1T+ Parameters" using experimental data from Periodic's physical lab. The research demonstrates how RL training can be effectively scaled to trillion-parameter models.

### Physical AI & Robotics
Periodic also operates in the physical AI space, competing with other well-funded startups targeting embodied AI and robotics applications.

## Infrastructure
Periodic relies on stable, performant GPU cloud infrastructure for its workloads, evaluating providers through benchmarks like SemiAnalysis' ClusterMAX for reliability, performance, networking, and managed Kubernetes orchestration.

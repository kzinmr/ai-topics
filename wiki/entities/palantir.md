# Palantir Technologies

Palantir is a software company that builds data integration and decision-making platforms for commercial and government organizations. In the AI agent era, Palantir has positioned itself as a leader in **human-agent decision infrastructure**.

## Key Products

### Palantir AIP (Artificial Intelligence Platform)
- Real-time, human-agent decision-making platform
- Powers disaster response, nuclear energy production, and other critical enterprise contexts
- Features **AIP AgentCamps** — hands-on-keyboard sessions where customers achieve AI outcomes in hours

### The Palantir Ontology
- A system designed to represent **decisions** in an enterprise, not simply data
- The core differentiator of Palantir's approach: a **decision-centric software architecture**
- Captures reasoning that goes into decision-making and the resulting actions
- Enables learning and incorporation of AI into operational workflows

## Philosophy
Palantir argues that traditional data architectures don't capture the reasoning behind decisions or the actions that result, limiting organizational learning and AI integration. Their **Ontology** approach contextualizes computation within "lived reality" rather than abstract data models.

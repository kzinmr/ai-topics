## Overview

TeortaxesTex is a writer and commentator active on X/Twitter since September 2010, with approximately 107K tweets, 59K followers, and a distinctive voice that bridges Chinese AI ecosystem observation, geopolitical analysis, and rationalist-adjacent philosophy. They describe themselves with the tagline *"We're in a race. It's not USA vs China but humans and AGIs vs ape power centralization."*

### The Jensen-Dwarkesh Epistemic Gap Analysis (April 2026)

Following the Dwarkesh × Jensen Huang podcast (April 2026), TeortaxesTex published a detailed analysis that clarified their position on both figures:

- **On Dwarkesh Patel**: "A great podcaster, unnaturally so" who has "engineered a methodology doing away with frustrating defects" from predecessors like Thomas Friedman. However, notes that Dwarkesh sometimes overdoes "exaggerated naivete" and uses rationalism as a "ragebaiting tactic." Acknowledges Dwarkesh's real value: "Many times has he goaded powerful men to spell out beliefs I could only conjecture they held."

- **On Jensen Huang**: "REALLY is Not a Loser" — argues Jensen is "the driver, not the car," with one of the greatest "dynamic range of lived experience" of anyone alive, having gone from "a toilet-scrubbing immigrant runt to a demigod." Compares Jensen to Xi Jinping as peer-level outliers. Insists that "when they deign to explain their ways, however awkwardly, us mortals should sit our asses down, listen and learn."

- **On the Epistemic Gap**: Argues that Jensen's worldview is not "inferior" to Dwarkesh's rationalist framework — it is "just different, and insistence on Not Being a Loser is its functional part." Jensen sorts moves into "self-strengthening and self-defeating" rather than constructing "consistent, defensible, lawyerly arguments."

### Causal Backbone Lens Applied to Tech Leaders

TeortaxesTex invoked tailcalled's **[[concepts/causal-backbone-conjecture]]** to analyze Jensen vs. Dwarkesh:

> "The core difference between agency-driven and information-driven systems — such as humans and base LLMs, or entrepreneurs and rationalists — is that the former are oriented towards the latent substructure of reality that Makes Shit Happen."

This frames Jensen as a "whole vertebra of the Universe's backbone" — someone whose journey from immigration to $4.5T CEO informs his worldview as much as Dwarkesh's "be really good at Reasonably Conversing, insure your middle class stake" informs his.

## Geopolitical Perspective

TeortaxesTex holds several distinctive positions:
- **Anti-utopian**: Skeptical of technological utopianism but not anti-technology
- **China realist**: Argues that China is not falling behind in AI due to energy/manufacturing advantages, and that export restrictions accelerate Chinese self-sufficiency
- **Anti-centralization**: "It's not USA vs China but humans and AGIs vs ape power centralization"
- **Pro-open AI**: Supports open-source AI development and opposes monopolistic control of AI infrastructure

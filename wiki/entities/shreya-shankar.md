# Shreya Shankar

AI researcher and co-creator (with Hamel Husain) of the world's most popular course on AI evals. Has trained over 2,000 PMs and engineers, including many teams at OpenAI and Anthropic.

- **Course:** [Maven AI Evals Course](https://bit.ly/4myp27m)

## Contributions

- Co-created the world's most popular AI evals course with Hamel Husain
- Trained 2,000+ PMs and engineers in systematic AI evaluation techniques
- Developed frameworks for distinguishing code-based evals vs. LLM-as-judge approaches
- Advocated for error analysis as the first step in building effective evals

## Core Ideas

1. **Systematic over subjective** — Move beyond "vibes-based" development to measurable quality
2. **Open coding first** — Human-led review of real user traces before automation
3. **Theoretical saturation** — Know when you've found enough failure modes to stop coding
4. **Benevolent dictator** — Single domain expert maintains eval taxonomy consistency

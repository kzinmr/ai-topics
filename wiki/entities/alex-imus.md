# Alex Imas

**Alex Imas** is the Director of AGI Economics at Google DeepMind and a Professor of Economics at the University of Chicago. He focuses on the economic implications of artificial general intelligence.

## Key Concepts
- **Relational Sector Scarcity**: Services and goods where human involvement is part of the value remain scarce despite mass automation.
- **AGI Economics**: Analyzing tax policy, wealth redistribution, and labor share in a post-AGI world.
- **Jevons Paradox**: Investigating how automation might lead to increased demand for certain resources rather than decrease.

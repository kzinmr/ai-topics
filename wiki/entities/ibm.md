# IBM (AI / watsonx)

**IBM** is a global technology company whose AI strategy centers on the **watsonx** platform for enterprise AI. At Think 2026 (May 2026), IBM announced a comprehensive expansion positioning watsonx as the control plane for the multi-agent enterprise.

## Think 2026 Announcements

### watsonx Orchestrate (Next Generation)

Evolved into an **agentic control plane** for the multi-agent era. Organizations can deploy agents from any source with consistent policy enforcement and accountability. Available in private preview.

### IBM Bob

An agentic development partner for enterprise use (generally available). Designed to assist developers in building, testing, and deploying AI applications.

### IBM Confluent

Real-time, AI-ready data foundation — governed, connected information that agents need to act with speed.

### IBM Concert Platform

AI-powered hybrid cloud management — connects infrastructure, security, and operations across hybrid environments.

### IBM Sovereign Core

Operational independence with built-in governance and sovereignty controls for running AI at scale.

## The AI Operating Model

IBM's thesis at Think 2026: enterprises have invested heavily in AI, but few believe it's paying off. The solution is a new operating model built on four integrated systems:

1. **Agents**: Coordinated AI that executes and adapts across the business
2. **Data**: Real-time, connected information giving teams a shared view
3. **Automation**: End-to-end infrastructure and automated workflows at scale
4. **Hybrid**: Operational independence for sovereignty, governance, and security

> "The enterprises pulling ahead are not deploying more AI — they're redesigning how their business operates." — Arvind Krishna, Chairman and CEO

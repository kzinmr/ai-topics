# Zoox Robotaxi Expansion

Amazon subsidiary Zoox executing largest growth phase in history with multi-city robotaxi rollout.

## City Rollouts

### Las Vegas
- Doubling service locations
- Partnerships with The Sphere & T-Mobile Arena
- Employee-only currently, public access "later this spring"

### San Francisco
- Quadrupling service area
- Coverage: Marina, North Beach, Chinatown, Pacific Heights, Embarcadero
- "Zoox Explorers" program expansion

### Austin & Miami
- Initial rollout in specific zones
- Employee & family testing
- Austin waitlist opening soon
- Full launch targeted for end of year

## Key Metrics
- ~2 million autonomous miles logged
- >350,000 passengers transported (Las Vegas, <1 year)
- Purpose-built vehicle approach (vs retrofitted cars)

## Product Enhancements
- **Find My Zoox**: Lighting & sound cues for vehicle identification
- **ZooxCast**: Bluetooth audio streaming
- Updated ETA engine for higher precision
- Pre-booking trip estimates

## Manufacturing
- Serial production facility in Hayward, CA (announced Sept 2025)
- Scaling robotaxi fleet manufacturing

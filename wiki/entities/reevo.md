# Reevo

Reevo is an AI-native go-to-market (GTM) platform billed as a "Revenue Operating System" that unifies marketing, sales, and customer success into a single platform. It replaces fragmented legacy sales stacks with an AI-powered alternative.

## Key Facts
- Founded in 2024 by former DoorDash engineering and growth leaders.
- Launched with $80M in funding from Khosla Ventures, Kleiner Perkins, and Zhu Ventures.
- Incubated by Khosla Ventures with Vinod Khosla as early backer.
- Team of ~90 employees with talent from Affirm, Airbnb, DoorDash, HubSpot, Notion, Salesforce, Square, and Uber.

## Products & Technology
- **Revenue Operating System**: Single AI-native platform spanning Find (prospecting), Connect (outreach), Sell (deal management), and Manage (post-sale).
- **Stackless Architecture**: Replaces multi-tool sales stacks (CRM, email, dialer, etc.) with one unified system.
- SOC 2 and ISO 27001 compliant.

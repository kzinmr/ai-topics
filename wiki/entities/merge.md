# Merge

Merge is a unified API platform that lets companies add hundreds of integrations to their products with a single API. It handles the full integration lifecycle — build, observability, and maintenance — across HRIS, accounting, CRM, file storage, ATS, and more.

## Key Facts
- Founded by Shensi Ding and Gil Feig, who met as freshmen at Columbia University.
- Both experienced integration pain at their previous startups (Expanse and Jumpstart).
- Raised $75M total across Seed, Series A, and Series B from Accel, NEA, and others.
- Headquartered in San Francisco with ~68 employees.

## Products & Technology
- **Unified APIs**: Single API to integrate with hundreds of services across HRIS, ATS, accounting, CRM, file storage, ticketing.
- **Merge Gateway**: Control plane for production AI — zero data retention gateway and MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers.
- **MCP Connectors**: Pre-built Claude Code connectors for Slack, Jira, Gmail, GitHub, Salesforce, and 20+ SaaS tools.
- Covers authentication, sync, observability, and ongoing maintenance.

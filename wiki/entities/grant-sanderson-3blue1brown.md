## Overview

Grant Sanderson is the creator of **3Blue1Brown**, one of the most influential educational YouTube channels for mathematics and machine learning. His visual-first approach to teaching complex topics — using custom animation software (Manim) — has made concepts in calculus, linear algebra, neural networks, and Transformers accessible to millions.

## Key AI/ML Contributions

- **"But what is a GPT?"** (2024) — Visual explanation of Transformer architecture and autoregressive language modeling, widely recommended as the best starting point for understanding LLM internals
- **"Attention in transformers, visually explained"** (2024) — Follow-up video on the attention mechanism
- **Neural networks series** (2017–2024) — From basic definitions through backpropagation
- **Essence of calculus** and **Essence of linear algebra** — Foundational series for ML math prerequisites

His content is cited as a 🟢 top-tier resource in the [[concepts/genai-handbook|GenAI Handbook]] for both math foundations and Transformer understanding.

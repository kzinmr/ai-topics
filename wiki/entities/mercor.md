# Mercor

AI-powered hiring and data labeling platform that matches expert contractors with AI labs and enterprises. Uses AI avatars for candidate interviews and automated resume screening. Creators of the APEX benchmark suite for AI agent evaluation.

## Key Facts
- Founded in 2023 by three high school friends — all Thiel Fellows, all age 22 at $10B valuation
- Raised $350M at $10B valuation (Oct 2025); backed by Felicis, Benchmark, General Catalyst
- Founders became world's youngest self-made billionaires, surpassing Mark Zuckerberg's record
- Customers include OpenAI and Anthropic; 30,000+ contractors managed
- Hired Sundeep Jain (ex-Uber CPO) as President in May 2025

## Products & Technology
- **AI Recruiting Platform**: Automated resume screening, AI avatar interviews, candidate matching
- **APEX-Agents**: Benchmark measuring AI model performance on professional tasks (consulting, law, banking)
- **APEX-SWE**: Software engineering benchmark co-created with Cognition

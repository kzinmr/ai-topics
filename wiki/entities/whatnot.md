# Whatnot

Whatnot is a digital marketplace and livestream shopping platform where users buy and sell items through online video auctions. Starting as a Funko Pop marketplace, it has grown into one of the largest livestream commerce platforms in the U.S.

## Key Facts
- Founded in 2019 by Grant LaFontaine and Logan Head, originally as a Funko Pop marketplace in Phoenix, AZ.
- Pivoted to livestream shopping during COVID-19; Y Combinator W20 batch.
- In 2024, sellers sold $3B worth of goods via live sales, mainly in collectibles (trading cards, sports cards).
- Valued at ~$5B after $265M Series E (2024), later $11.5B (Series F); raised ~$975M total.
- Named to TIME100 Most Influential Companies (2026).

## Products & Technology
- **Live Video Auctions**: Sellers host live shows promoting products with real-time bidding.
- **Categories**: Collectibles, trading cards, sneakers, fashion, and expanding into food.
- **Rewards Program**: Sellers create reward incentives for buyers (coupons, free shipping, exclusive access).
- Built-in payment, authentication, and community features.

## HuggingFace Era

At HuggingFace, Elie contributed to several key open-source projects:

- **SmolLM 3**: Small language model series demonstrating competitive performance
- **FineWeb**: Large-scale web data curation for pretraining
- **FinePDF**: PDF data extraction and processing pipeline

He appeared on Latent Space's "Open Model Pretraining Masterclass" (Oct 2025, 5.4K views) discussing pretraining methodology for open models.

## Prime Intellect Role

At Prime Intellect, leads LLM training efforts including the INTELLECT model series — contributing to decentralized training infrastructure and scaling open-source models.

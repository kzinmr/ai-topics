# Unitree Robotics

## Overview

Hangzhou-based humanoid and quadruped robot manufacturer. Founded 2016 by Wang Xingxing (former DJI engineer, age 26 at founding). World's #1 humanoid robot seller in 2025.

## Strategy

Core conviction: humanoid robots only matter at scale if affordable at scale. Aggressive pricing, rapid iteration, ruthless production scaling.

## Products

| Product | Type | Price | Notes |
|---------|------|-------|-------|
| **Go2** | Quadruped | $1,600 | Democratized robotic research |
| **G1** | Humanoid | $16,000 | Default humanoid research platform at universities worldwide |
| **H1** | Humanoid | ~$90,000 | Industrial-grade humanoid |
| **H2** | Humanoid | <$30,000 | Unveiled early 2026, full-size industrial |
| **R1** | Humanoid | $4,900 | Compact, mass-production focused |

## 2026 Milestones

- **5,500 units shipped** in 2025 (world's top humanoid robot seller)
- **10,000-20,000 units** shipment target for 2026 (nearly 4x growth)
- **10,000 sqm factory** opened in Hangzhou (2025)
- Listed on **Amazon** for direct-to-consumer global sales
- **2026 Spring Festival Gala**: Unitree humanoid robots performed martial arts routines (sword forms, nunchaku, 3-meter aerial flips, drunken boxing) — broadcast to ~600 million viewers, serving as an implicit product launch

## Technology

- **UnifoLM** (Unified Robot Large Model): AI system for robot intelligence
- **G1 feedback loop**: Open-sourced university research accelerated G1's real-world performance without proportional R&D spend
- 23 degrees of freedom (G1), 360° vision, 90-minute battery life (H1)

## Key Person

- **Wang Xingxing** — Founder & CEO. Previously worked at [[dji]]. Disclosed 2026 shipment targets to 36Kr immediately after Spring Festival Gala.

## Market Impact

- Reshaped price floor for humanoid robots from $90,000+ range down to $4,900
- Morgan Stanley revised 2026 China sales forecast to 28,000 units
- Goldman Sachs revised 2035 global market forecast to $38B (6x upward revision)

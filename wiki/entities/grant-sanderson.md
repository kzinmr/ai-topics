# Grant Sanderson

**Grant Sanderson** is the creator of [3Blue1Brown](https://www.3blue1brown.com/), one of the most popular mathematics education channels on YouTube (millions of subscribers). His visual explanations of linear algebra, calculus, neural networks, and other mathematical topics have made him a leading voice in mathematics communication.

As of June 2026, Sanderson has been an early and prominent voice on the intersection of AI and mathematics, arguing that AI is making faster progress in mathematics than in most other fields and that math serves as a leading indicator of what AI progress will look like elsewhere.

## AI and Mathematics (June 2026)

In a podcast conversation with [[entities/dwarkesh-patel|Dwarkesh Patel]] (June 30, 2026), Sanderson discussed:

- **AI in mathematics is a leading indicator**: AI has been making much faster progress in mathematical problem-solving than in most other domains — showing "what AI progress will look like" elsewhere
- **Conceptual breakthroughs vs pattern matching**: The difference between discovering new proofs (which requires conceptual understanding) and pattern matching on existing solutions
- **Verification loop**: AI's ability to verify proofs creates a self-reinforcing cycle of improvement
- **Hidden bridges between fields**: AI's pattern-matching capabilities may reveal connections between disparate mathematical fields that humans have missed
- **Advice for students**: Guidance on navigating mathematical careers in an AI-transformed landscape

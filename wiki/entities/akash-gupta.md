# Akash Gupta

**Akash Gupta** (@akashgupta) is a researcher and engineer focused on AI safety and the architectural design of agentic systems. His work centers on the application of software engineering security principles — particularly **separation of duties** — to AI agent architectures.

## Key Ideas

### Agent Safety Separation of Duties
Gupta's core thesis applies the security engineering principle of **separation of duties** (SoD) to AI agent systems. In traditional security engineering, SoD ensures no single entity has unchecked authority over critical operations — Gupta argues this principle is equally essential in agentic workflows where autonomous agents execute multi-step operations.

### Structural Safeguards for Agent Systems
Proposes embedding safety constraints directly into the orchestration layer of agent systems (rather than relying on model-level safety alone). This includes:
- Role-based access control for agents
- Explicit permission boundaries per agent
- Audit trails and escalation paths for cross-boundary operations

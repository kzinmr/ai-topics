# Isaac Tai

**Isaac Tai** ([@hi_im_isaac_](https://x.com/hi_im_isaac_)) is an engineer on the AI/Growth team at [[Cerebras Systems|entities/cerebras-systems]]. He is a co-author of the Cerebras Knowledge Base — an internal enterprise knowledge platform handling 15,000+ queries per day across the company's hardware, inference, training, and cloud teams.

## Key Work

- **Cerebras Knowledge Base** (July 2026) — Co-author of the hybrid search enterprise knowledge base architecture, documented in the Cerebras engineering blog. The system combines full-text search, embeddings, IDF, and age decay with LLM-based query planning and MCP-exposed retrieval primitives. See [[concepts/enterprise-knowledge-base-architecture]].

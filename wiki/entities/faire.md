# Faire

Faire is an online wholesale marketplace connecting independent retailers with brands and makers worldwide. It replaces traditional trade shows with a digital platform for discovering, buying, and selling wholesale merchandise across categories.

## Key Facts
- Founded in 2017 by four former Square employees: Max Rhodes, Marcelo Cortes, Daniele Perito, and Jeffrey Kolovson.
- Raised $1.7B across 9 rounds; valued at $5.2B (Series I, Nov 2025).
- Previously valued at $7B in 2021 after Sequoia-led Series F.
- Serves as the digital alternative to trade shows for small retailers and independent brands.
- ~1,500 employees as of 2026.

## Products & Technology
- **Two-Sided Marketplace**: Brands list products; retailers discover and purchase wholesale inventory.
- **Net Payment Terms**: Faire offers financing options for retailers.
- **AI-Powered Discovery**: Recommendation and search algorithms for product matching.
- Categories include home decor, apparel, beauty, food, and more.

## Overview

Jay Alammar is one of the most influential visual explainers in machine learning, known for his "Illustrated" series that demystifies complex ML concepts through clear diagrams and intuitive explanations. His blog posts have become canonical references for anyone learning about Transformers, embeddings, and modern NLP.

## Key Resources (cited in GenAI Handbook)

- **"The Illustrated Transformer"** — Visual walkthrough of the Transformer architecture from "Attention is All You Need". The most widely-recommended visual guide.
- **"The Illustrated Word2Vec"** — Building intuition for word embeddings and semantic vector spaces
- His blog posts are cited as 🟢 top-tier resources in the [[concepts/genai-handbook|GenAI Handbook]] (Section II: Neural Sequential Prediction)

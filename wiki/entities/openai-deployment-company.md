# OpenAI Deployment Company (DeployCo)

The **OpenAI Deployment Company** (DeployCo) is an OpenAI-majority-owned entity launched in May 2026 to help organizations deploy AI systems into production operations. It represents OpenAI's move from selling model access to providing implementation services — deploying Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs) who work inside client organizations.

## Key Facts

- **Launched**: May 11, 2026
- **Investment**: $4 billion initial at $10 billion pre-money valuation
- **Ownership**: Majority-owned and controlled by OpenAI
- **CEO/CRO**: Denise Dresser (Chief Revenue Officer at OpenAI)
- **Lead investor**: TPG
- **Co-lead founding partners**: Advent International, Bain Capital, Brookfield
- **Other investors**: B Capital, BBVA, Emergence Capital, Goanna, Goldman Sachs, SoftBank Corp., Thrive, Warburg Pincus, WCAS
- **Consulting partners** (also investors): Bain & Company, Capgemini, McKinsey & Company
- **Total partners**: 19 global firms
- **Investor terms**: Guaranteed minimum 17.5% return, profits capped (per Axios)

## Tomoro Acquisition

DeployCo's first acquisition: **Tomoro**, an applied AI consulting and engineering firm with ~150 Forward Deployed Engineers and Deployment Specialists. Tomoro has experience with mission-critical workflows at Tesco, Virgin Atlantic, and Supercell. The acquisition is subject to regulatory approval and expected to close in coming months.

## Strategy

DeployCo bridges the gap between AI model capability and operational impact:

1. **Forward Deployed Engineers** work inside client organizations
2. **Connect models** to internal data, tools, controls, and business processes
3. **Learn and generalize**: Successful deployment patterns are fed back to OpenAI and scaled across the economy

> "AI is becoming capable of doing increasingly meaningful work inside organizations. The challenge now is helping companies integrate these systems." — Denise Dresser

## Competitive Context

- [[entities/anthropic]] is reportedly pursuing a comparable enterprise AI services effort
- Goldman Sachs is the only backer of both DeployCo and Anthropic's initiative
- The move puts OpenAI in direct competition with traditional consulting firms (Accenture, Deloitte) while partnering with Bain, Capgemini, and McKinsey

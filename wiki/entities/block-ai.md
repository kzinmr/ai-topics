# Block AI

**Block, Inc.** (formerly Square, Inc.) is an American technology company and financial services provider founded by [[entities/jack-dorsey|Jack Dorsey]] in 2009. In 2024–2026, Block underwent a radical AI-driven organizational transformation articulated in the internal essay **"From Hierarchy to Intelligence,"** which proposes replacing traditional management hierarchy with an AI-powered intelligence layer.

## Company Overview

- **Founded:** February 14, 2009 (as Square, Inc.)
- **Founders:** Jack Dorsey, Jim McKelvey, Tristan O'Tierney
- **Renamed:** Block, Inc. (December 2021)
- **Stock Ticker:** XYZ (NYSE, formerly SQ)
- **Employees:** ~5,000 (post-February 2026 layoffs, down from ~10,000)
- **Revenue:** Processed $241 billion in payments annually (2024)
- **Users:** 57 million consumers, 4 million sellers

### Products
- **Square** — Point-of-sale payment processing
- **Cash App** — Peer-to-peer payment and financial services
- **Afterpay** — Buy now, pay later (acquired 2021)
- **Bitkey** — Self-custody Bitcoin wallet
- **TBD** — Decentralized finance platform

## The "From Hierarchy to Intelligence" Transformation

In April 2026, Block published an internal document detailing a radical restructuring of the company's organizational model. The core thesis: most companies use AI as a **copilot** (optimizing existing structures), but Block is building the company as an **intelligence** to replace the hierarchy itself.

### Historical Context
The article traces 2,000 years of organizational design, noting that every major innovation worked around the human span-of-control limit:
- Roman Army → Nested hierarchy
- Prussia → Middle management via General Staff
- US Railroads → First org charts
- Frederick Taylor → Scientific Management
- Matrix Org (McKinsey) → Balance central/local
- Modern Tech → Spotify squads, Zappos Holacracy (all reverted to hierarchy at scale)

### The New Architecture
1. **Capabilities** — Atomic financial primitives (no UIs)
2. **World Model** — Company operations + Customer reality
3. **Intelligence Layer** — Composes capabilities into proactive solutions
4. **Interfaces** — Delivery surfaces (secondary to model/intelligence)

### Four New Roles
- **Edge Function** — Humans handle intuition, ethics, novel situations
- **Individual Contributors** — Deep specialists using world model context
- **Directly Responsible Individuals (DRIs)** — Own cross-cutting outcomes
- **Player-Coaches** — Replace traditional managers

**Result:** No permanent middle management. Intelligence lives in the system.

### Layoffs & Market Reaction
- **February 26, 2026:** Block laid off ~4,000 of 10,000 employees, citing AI as primary reason
- **Market reaction:** Shares jumped 24% following the announcement
- This was one of the largest AI-driven workforce reductions by a major tech company

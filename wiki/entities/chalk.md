# Chalk

Chalk is a real-time data platform purpose-built for machine learning. It provides feature storage, compute, and monitoring infrastructure that unifies training and serving to eliminate train-serve skew and accelerate ML iteration.

## Key Facts
- Founded by former Affirm, Palantir, Airbnb, and Google engineers.
- Raised $50M Series A in May 2025 at a $500M valuation led by Felicis.
- Previously raised $10M Seed in December 2023.
- Customers include Turo, Whatnot, Medely, Mission Lane, and iwoca.

## Products & Technology
- **Feature Platform**: Combines feature storage, on-demand compute, caching, and monitoring in one cloud-native system.
- **Data Pipelines**: Declarative Python API for defining features with online/offline serving.
- **Runs in Your Cloud**: Deploys in the customer's own VPC for data security.
- Unifies training and serving to prevent data drift and feature skew.

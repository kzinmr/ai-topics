# Center for AI Standards and Innovation (CAISI)

The US government's primary hub for AI model testing, established June 2025 within NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology) under the Department of Commerce. Successor to the US Artificial Intelligence Safety Institute (US AISI).

## Leadership
- **Director**: Chris Fall
- **Established by**: Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick (June 2025)

## Mandate
1. Develop guidelines and best practices for AI system security
2. Lead **unclassified evaluations** of AI capabilities posing national security risks (cybersecurity, biosecurity, chemical weapons)
3. Evaluate US and adversary AI systems; assess international AI competition
4. Assess security vulnerabilities and malign foreign influence from adversary AI systems
5. Coordinate with DoD, DOE, DHS, OSTP, Intelligence Community
6. Represent US interests in international AI standards

# Abridge

Abridge is a generative AI company focused on healthcare, creating AI-powered tools that transform patient-clinician conversations into structured clinical documentation. Its platform gives clinicians agency back by automating note-taking and clinical workflows.

## Key Facts
- Founded by Shiv Rao, MD, a practicing cardiologist at UPMC with a background in healthcare investment and ML.
- Named one of Fast Company's Most Innovative Companies of 2026.
- Rao named to Forbes 250 Greatest Innovators (2026).
- Expanding into nursing documentation with Mayo Clinic partnership (2026).

## Products & Technology
- **Clinical Documentation AI**: Real-time conversation-to-note generation for clinicians.
- **Contextual Reasoning Engine**: Proprietary AI that understands medical context.
- **Revenue Cycle & Nursing**: Expanding beyond clinical notes into revenue cycle management and nursing workflows.
- Named Best in KLAS for multiple categories.

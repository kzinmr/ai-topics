# XPeng

## Overview

Chinese electric vehicle manufacturer with integrated robotics and flying vehicle divisions. Builds cars, robots, and flying vehicles on a single AI-powered platform.

## Products

| Product | Type | Notes |
|---------|------|-------|
| **EVs** | Electric vehicles | AI-powered self-parking (Shenzhen/Guangzhou developed) |
| **PX5** | Quadruped robot | Autonomous navigation, automotive AI expertise |
| **Iron** | Humanoid robot | Prototype stage |
| **Flying vehicles** | Air vehicles | Part of multi-modal platform strategy |

## Strategy

Leverages automotive AI expertise (self-driving, sensor fusion, real-world data) for robotics applications. The "single platform" approach allows technology reuse across cars, robots, and flying vehicles.

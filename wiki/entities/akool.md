# AKOOL

AI Video Generation Suite based in Palo Alto, CA. Provides a complete multimodal platform combining advanced generation tools with enterprise-grade production infrastructure for studio-quality AI video at scale.

## Overview

AKOOL develops a full-stack AI video inference engine achieving 10–20× speed gains over conventional approaches. Traditional AI video generation required tens of seconds per clip; AKOOL's system reduces that to 1–3 seconds with real-time streaming at sub-30ms latency per frame.

## Key Technology (May 2026)

In May 2026, AKOOL announced a production-grade video inference engine rebuilt from the ground up:

- **Full-stack optimization**: Algorithm design through hardware execution
- **Reduced computational steps** with increased GPU parallelism
- **Eliminated runtime overhead** leveraging next-gen GPU architectures
- **Cross-platform deployment**: Cloud, real-time streaming, on-device

The engine powers AKOOL's product suite including **Live Camera** for real-time digital avatars, live translation, and interactive video experiences.

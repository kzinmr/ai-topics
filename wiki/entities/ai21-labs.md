# AI21 Labs

Israeli AI company that shut down its model team in May 2026 and pivoted entirely to agents, representing the broader **"Model Lab → Agent Lab"** industry shift alongside DeepSeek forming its first "Harness team" and OpenAI signaling that "the model alone is no longer the product."

## Overview

AI21 Labs was founded in 2017 by AI pioneers — including Prof. Amnon Shashua (co-founder of Mobileye) — and initially focused on building foundational models, notably the Jurassic series. The company raised significant funding ($155M Series C in 2023 at a $1.4B valuation) and operated one of the largest NLP platforms.

## Strategic Pivot to Agents (May 2026)

In May 2026, AI21 Labs announced the shutdown of its model team, pivoting its entire focus to **AI agents and agent infrastructure**. This decision aligns with a broader industry trend:

- [[entities/deepseek]] — Formed its first "Harness team" for agent infrastructure
- [[entities/openai]] — Greg Brockman stated "the model alone is no longer the product"
- [[entities/anthropic]] — Launched Claude Agents SDK (GA) and Project Glasswing

The pivot validates the "Systems over Models" thesis — that the moat is shifting from standalone model performance to the **model + harness + workflow + UI + memory + economics** stack.

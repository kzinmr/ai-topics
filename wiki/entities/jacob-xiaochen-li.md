# Jacob Xiaochen Li

**Affiliation:** MIT CSAIL

Researcher working on AI agent capabilities, specifically on how agents can autonomously develop expertise from document corpora. Co-author of the [[concepts/machine-studying|Machine Studying]] framework and [[concepts/machine-studying#studybench-benchmark|StudyBench]] benchmark with [[entities/omar-khattab|Omar Khattab]] and Rick Battle.

## Key Contributions

- **Machine Studying** (June 2026): Problem formulation for agents developing expertise from corpora. Defines *expertise* as efficiency of turning inference compute into accuracy. Three paradigms: self-supervised objectives, synthetic data/environments, amortized context management.
- **StudyBench**: Benchmark evaluating agent ability to study novel domains (DSPy, OpenClaw tasks).

# GPT Image 2 vs Nano Banana 2

## What is being compared

Two leading AI image generation models for professional use: **OpenAI's GPT Image 2** (ChatGPT Images 2.0) and **Google's Nano Banana 2** (NB2, via Google AI Studio).

## Comparison Summary

| Dimension | GPT Image 2 (OpenAI) | Nano Banana 2 (Google) | Winner |
|-----------|---------------------|----------------------|--------|
| **Initial output quality** | Superior prompt adherence, remarkable vibrancy | Good, but repetitive styling | GPT Image 2 |
| **Aesthetic style** | Clean, professional, not overdesigned, highly adaptable | "Cartoonist" default; consistent fonts, borders, shadows, icons | GPT Image 2 |
| **Iteration control** | `Select` lasso tool + `Aspect ratio` control post-generation | Natural language prompts that often fail | GPT Image 2 |
| **Hallucinations** | Significantly lower | Higher frequency (esp. complex layouts) | GPT Image 2 |
| **Text/Chart accuracy** | Accurate data visualization, no text repetition | Garbled charts, repeated text, misplaced axes | GPT Image 2 |
| **Generation speed** | 40s–60s+ (>2x slower) | 20–25s | Nano Banana 2 |
| **Resolution control** | Not documented (likely fixed) | 512px, 1K, 2K, 4K | Nano Banana 2 |
| **Aspect ratio options** | Post-gen control | 1:1, 16:9, 9:16, 3:4, 4:5, 4:1, etc. | NB2 (broader) |
| **Watermarks** | None | None | Tie |
| **Platform** | ChatGPT subscription | Google AI Studio + Gemini App | — |

## Example Outputs (one-shot comparisons)

1. **"A slide about renewable energy"**
   - NB2: Repetitive styling, lacks professional polish
   - GPT Image 2: Clean layout, accurate to prompt, no AI tells

2. **"An infographic about Tesla"**
   - NB2: Fails on small components. Charts garbled. Repeated text in "The Future" section.
   - GPT Image 2: Accurate data visualization, no text repetition or layout errors

3. **"A bar chart showing global population growth"**
   - NB2: Misplaced Y-axis just before 2100 projected bar. Unexplained colors for projected bars.
   - GPT Image 2: Simple, precise, avoids unnecessary backgrounds/annotations

## Verdict

For **professional output quality**, GPT Image 2 is the clear winner. It produces clean, professional results that reviewers describe as "not looking AI-generated." The iteration tools (Select lasso, aspect ratio control) give users much tighter control than NB2's natural-language-only approach.

The primary trade-off is **speed**: GPT Image 2 is ~2x slower (40–60s vs 20–25s). However, reviewers find the ~30-second delay negligible, noting that waiting for superior output is more efficient than:
- Settling for sub-par NB2 results
- Hiring a human designer
- Manually fixing errors in Figma/Canva

Only delays of 20+ minutes would justify reconsidering the trade-off.

## Platform Context

- **GPT Image 2** is embedded in ChatGPT, accessible to subscribers as part of the conversation flow
- **NB2** is accessible via Google AI Studio and the Gemini App (with reduced controls on mobile)

# Weekly Update 498

Source: https://www.troyhunt.com/weekly-update-498/

This week, more time than I'd have liked to spend went on talking about the trials of chasing invoices. This is off the back of a customer (who, for now, will remain unnamed), who had invoices stacking back more than 6 months overdue and despite payment terms of 30 days, paid on an average of
80 days
. But as I say in this week's video, more than anything, it was the gall of the CEO to take issue with my frustrated tone rather than with their complete lack of respect for basic business etiquette and paying one's suppliers. And so, Copilot and I spent the weekend fixing up a nice little Xero integration to ensure this never happens again. If you arrive at this post sometime in the future after finding your HIBP enterprise service no longer functioning weeks after an unpaid invoice was due, at least you'll know it's not personal... and pay your damn bills!

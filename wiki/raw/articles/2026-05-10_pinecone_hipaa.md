Pinecone is now HIPAA compliant across Amazon Web Services (AWS), Azure, and Google Cloud Provider (GCP). This milestone enables healthcare and life science organizations to build powerful GenAI applications that deliver fast, accurate, and critical search results and more to their patients.
HIPAA and leveraging AI to unlock better patient care and research for healthcare and life sciences
A top priority for Pinecone is always ensuring customer's data is secured and available. We are constantly committed to exceeding the industry standards regarding security and compliance.
Some of the largest enterprises already trust Pinecone to store data and power their mission-critical applications. For those
subject to HIPAA requirements
, we can now support and comply with their data sharing and search use cases.
Enabling healthcare and life sciences organizations to leverage Pinecone for their AI use cases can lead to many advancements like comparing medical images to quickly detect anomalies, building support bots that answer questions specific to a patient or a medical condition, and analyzing large datasets of personal health information (PHI) to better understand a drug's efficacy and side effects.
In addition to supporting HIPAA compliance, Pinecone is also SOC2 Type II certified. We maintain compliance with strict access controls, internal monitoring, routine third-party security reviews, and much more.

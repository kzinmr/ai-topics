# Gemini Gets to Work, Claude's Big Pull, and OpenAI Unchained

**Source:** [The Signal](https://open.substack.com/pub/thesignal/p/gemini-gets-to-work-claudes-big-pull) | **Date:** May 03, 2026 | **Author:** Alex Banks

## 1. Google: Gemini Updates & Integration

* **Global File Generation:** Gemini can now generate and export Docs, Sheets, Slides, PDFs, .docx, .xlsx, .csv, and Markdown files directly in-chat. (No native PowerPoint export at launch).
* **UK Memories:** Personalization features (cross-conversation memory) launched in the UK to meet regulatory standards.
* **Switching Tools:** Users can now import full chat histories from rivals like ChatGPT via ZIP exports or copy-paste memory summaries.
* **Strategic Outlook:** Google is 7–9 months behind OpenAI and Anthropic in file generation, but its structural advantage (owning the chips, models, and distribution) remains its "long-term hand."

> Alex's Take: "Google has the long-term hand. They need to play it with more focus and velocity at the model layer, before a competitor's meaningfully smarter model eclipses all their integration work."

## 2. Anthropic: The Enterprise Powerhouse

* **Creative Coalition:** Launched nine connectors for professional tools including **Adobe Creative Cloud (50+ tools)**, Blender, Autodesk Fusion, Ableton, and SketchUp.
* **Claude Security:** A new public beta for Enterprise using **Opus 4.7**. It scans codebases, validates findings to reduce false positives, and proposes patches.
* **Market Dominance:** Anthropic now holds **40% of enterprise LLM spend** and **54% of the enterprise coding share**, surpassing OpenAI with **$30B ARR**.
* **Security Feat:** Claude Mythos found 271 zero-day vulnerabilities in Firefox in one sweep—nearly 4x what Mozilla patched in all of 2025.

## 3. OpenAI: Multi-Cloud & Infrastructure

* **Microsoft Partnership Amendment:** OpenAI is now free to sell across any cloud. Microsoft retains IP licenses through 2032 and revenue shares through 2030.
* **AWS Integration:** OpenAI models (Codex, Managed Agents) are now available on **Amazon Bedrock** in limited preview.
* **Symphony (Open Source):** Gained 15K GitHub stars in days.

## 5. Robotics & Manufacturing

* **Figure (BotQ Line):** Increased production from 1 robot/day to **1 robot/hour** (24x jump).
* **Unitree:** Launched a dual-arm humanoid at **$4,290**.
* **RobotEra:** Deploying thousands of L7 humanoids in China Post logistics, achieving **85% human-level efficiency** 24/7.
* Labor cost moving from biological/educational constraint to manufacturing curve (Wright's Law).

## 6. Key Insights & Quotes

* **Google Compute Dominance:** "Google controls ~25% of global AI compute, with ~3.8 million TPUs and 1.3 million GPUs" — Alexis Ohanian
* **Future of Interfaces:** "Chat window" will persist for iterative work, but voice-first, screen-aware computing (like Clicky) is the future.
* **Vibe Maths:** An amateur using ChatGPT recently solved a 60-year-old mathematics problem.

GleanによるAIサマリー
Glean will feature informative sessions on generative AI, including building AI experiences for enterprises and implementing AI applications, with speakers from Glean and other leading companies.
Attendees can visit Glean's booth for live demos and meetings with sales leadership to explore how Glean's enterprise search and generative AI can transform their work.
Glean and Databricks will host a poolside party to celebrate AI innovation, providing an opportunity for networking and discussing AI-powered productivity.
We're excited to announce that we'll be at
Google Cloud Next ‘24
– one of the most exciting events this year in AI innovation and cloud technology! This year, we’ll be featuring a series of informative sessions, showing you the latest and greatest Glean features at our booth, as well as hosting an AI-themed poolside party for you to wind down at while connecting with other AI experts and enthusiasts.
Read on for all the details you need to get involved!
Unmissable sessions and panels
Interested in learning about the best way to bring generative AI into the enterprise – safely, securely, and effectively – without falling behind or spending a fortune? Make sure to catch our sessions:
CloudTalk Theatre Session:
Building generative AI experiences for the enterprise on Google Cloud
Speaker:
Eddie Zhou, Founding Engineer
‍
About:
Building an assistant capable of answering complex, company-specific questions and executing workflows requires first building a powerful
Retrieval Augmented Generation
(RAG) system. Founding engineer Eddie Zhou explains how Glean built its RAG system on Google Cloud— combining a domain-adapted search engine with dynamic prompts to harness the full capabilities of Gemini's reasoning engine.
Panel Session:
A guide for enterprises: How to implement generative AI applications
Speaker:
Arvind Jain, Founder & CEO
‍
About:
Join experts from Box, Typeface, Glean, CitiBank, and Securiti AI for actionable tips to effectively implement AI-powered apps across your enterprise. We'll discuss real use cases, implementation best practices, and how to measure returns on investment — whether that's in marketing, financial services, HR, or beyond.
Panel Session:
Founder series panel: Solving consumer and enterprise hurdles with natural language
Speaker:
Arvind Jain, Founder & CEO
‍
About:
Join us to hear from Adam Ghetti (Co-Founder at Stealth), Arvind Jain (Co-Founder and CEO at Glean), Alex Mashrabov (Co-Founder and CEO at Higgsfield AI) and Cody Coleman (Co-Founder and CEO at Coactive AI) who are leading the application of Generative AI in the enterprise and scaled consumer world. They will share how they’re using Natural Language to solve some of the biggest hurdles in the day-to-day lives of consumers and enterprises. Come learn how they foresee the evolution of Natural Language in the near term and how General AI is going to catalyze the answers to many problems that remain yet to be solved.

Decagon is now an accredited solution with Five9
‍
Decagon and Five9 deliver a powerful combination of contact center infrastructure and agentic AI automation. Five9 serves as the engagement and routing fabric by handling omnichannel interactions, intelligent routing, and workforce optimization, while Decagon acts as the agentic brain, powering autonomous AI agents and advanced workflows across the same Five9-managed traffic.
The result is higher containment and end-to-end automation of complex, multi-step interactions like refunds, account updates, and policy-driven workflows. When human intervention is required, Decagon passes rich context (transcripts, customer data, and intent signals) directly to Five9 agents, enabling informed handoffs to human agents allowing for improved resolution times and a concierge customer experience.
Crucially, this all happens without replacing the CCaaS or CRM systems enterprises already rely on. Decagon is purpose-built to layer onto existing infrastructure, and the joint solution supports both PSTN and SIP integrations, including pre-built SIP trunking that simplifies deployment and reduces telephony overhead.
Decagon x Five9: Faster time to value, unparalleled transparency and control, and concierge CX at scale
Decagon's accredited SIP integration with Five9 means enterprises can deploy production-ready AI concierge agents directly on top of their existing Five9 environment with no rip-and-replace, no custom infrastructure, and a clean handoff to Five9 human agents when escalation is needed.
The result is concierge voice experiences that are faster to launch and easier to trust:
Faster time to value:
Deploy production-ready AI voice agents in weeks using natural language with Agent Operating Procedures (AOPs), directly on the Five9 channels and routing customers already use.
Unparalleled transparency and control:
Strict guardrails, full reasoning traceability, and seamless escalation to Five9 human agents let teams scale automation confidently while retaining complete control.
Concierge customer experiences at scale:
Transform reactive support into a growth engine with personalized, proactive engagement across voice, chat, email, SMS, and more — orchestrated end-to-end across the systems enterprises already use.

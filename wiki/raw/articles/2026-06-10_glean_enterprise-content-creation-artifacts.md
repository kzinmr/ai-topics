AI Summary by Glean
Most enterprise AI tools generate generic, context-poor first drafts, whereas Glean acts as an AI coworker grounded in a personal and enterprise knowledge graph to create immediately usable, personalized deliverables and collaborate through brainstorming, revision, and transparent review.
Glean introduces refreshable, interactive artifacts in Canvas—dynamic pages that consolidate organizational data and support live exploration—enabling use cases like ROI calculators, dynamic task lists, and customer datasheets that can be built once, refreshed as needed, and shared directly from Glean.
Glean now supports AI-built spreadsheets in Canvas so any employee can analyze company data without analyst bottlenecks, using personalized, AI-assisted spreadsheets to tackle tasks such as product adoption analysis, campaign performance tracking, deal reviews, and prioritized customer feedback lists.
When most enterprise AI tools generate content, they do so without context of how you and your company actually work. Without a knowledge graph that connects people, processes, and information, there is a meaningful gap between generated output and usable work. Although you may receive a first draft, you still lose a significant amount of time looking for information to incorporate as you rework drafts, finalize narratives, and iterate to create a final deliverable.
Glean is the AI coworker that creates content immediately worth using. Every output is personalized because Glean’s personal graph grounds what you create in your company’s knowledge and your writing style and preferences. Glean also develops deliverables with you in the exact way you want to work, helping you brainstorm, revise, and carry work across systems with a transparent review process. Glean works hand in hand with you to execute high-value work quickly and drive better output across your entire organization.
Today, we’re adding spreadsheets and refreshable, interactive artifacts in canvas to make Glean an even more personalized and collaborative AI coworker.
Build refreshable interactive artifacts with your AI coworker
Most business deliverables still live in static documents, slide decks, and spreadsheets. In many cases, however, users prefer to create and get more value from an interactive artifact – a dynamic, visual page that brings together data from across an organization. Interactive artifacts make it easy to probe data, ask follow-up questions, apply filters, and see results update live, instead of manually consolidating and refreshing content and data.
With interactive artifacts, you can build:
ROI calculators:
Build calculators that let users input their own metrics, like tickets resolved or hours saved per week, for internal reporting.
Dynamic task lists:
Track dates, owners, risks, and open decisions with sections that get updated as projects are completed.
Customer datasheets:
Package research, proof points, and next steps into a shareable briefing page account teams can view and share internally to prepare for a customer meeting.
Glean can now produce durable, interactive content from natural language, grounded in your enterprise context. You can build it once, refresh the data as needed, and share it right from Glean.
Analyze data with an AI coworker who builds spreadsheets with you
Spreadsheets have been a staple of business for decades, yet they remain a difficult tool for average employees to use effectively. For complex analysis, analyst teams are often brought in, creating bottlenecks when there isn’t enough support and slowing decisions. With spreadsheets in Glean canvas, anyone can build a personalized spreadsheet grounded in your company data. You can collaborate with an AI coworker to sort, filter, edit data, and get answers to move work forward.
Teams can use spreadsheets to:
Analyze product adoption:
Understand adoption by customer segment and identify where usage is growing or stagnating.
Track campaign performance:
Build a view across channels, owners, spend, and pipeline impact to understand campaign effectiveness.
Create deal reviews:
Prepare account history, pipeline, renewal risk, and next steps in one place.
Prioritize customer feedback:
Monitor support tickets, customer survey data, and internal feedback to prioritize a list for future product roadmaps.
Context helps AI transform content into enterprise-ready deliverables, not just first drafts
Creating great content with the right context is one of the highest-leverage things an AI coworker can do, and one of the hardest to get right. Most enterprise AI tools don’t understand your users or your business, so they leave teams editing generic content.
Glean is the enterprise AI coworker built on a deep understanding of how you and your organization actually work. That’s what makes creating with Glean different. It works hand in hand with you to build personalized artifacts that are grounded in your personal and enterprise graphs.

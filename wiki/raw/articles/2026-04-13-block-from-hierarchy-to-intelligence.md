# Block: From Hierarchy to Intelligence

## Core Thesis
Most companies use AI as a **copilot** (optimizing existing structures). Block is building a company as an **intelligence** to replace the hierarchy itself.

## 2,000-Year History of Organizational Design
Every major innovation worked *around* the human span-of-control limit:
- Roman Army: Nested hierarchy (contubernium → century → cohort → legion)
- Prussia: General Staff created middle management
- US Railroads: First org charts
- Frederick Taylor: Scientific Management
- Matrix Org (McKinsey): Balance central standards with local agility
- Modern Tech: Spotify squads, Zappos Holacracy — all reverted to hierarchy at scale

## The New Architecture
1. **Capabilities**: Atomic financial primitives (no UIs)
2. **World Model**: Company ops + Customer reality
3. **Intelligence Layer**: Composes capabilities into proactive solutions
4. **Interfaces**: Delivery surfaces (secondary to model/intelligence)

## Three Roles
- **Edge Function**: Humans handle intuition, ethics, novel situations
- **Individual Contributors**: Deep specialists using world model context
- **Directly Responsible Individuals**: Own cross-cutting outcomes
- **Player-Coaches**: Replace traditional managers

**Result**: No permanent middle management. Intelligence lives in the system.

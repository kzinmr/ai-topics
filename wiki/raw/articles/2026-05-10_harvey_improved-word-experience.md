:Harvey:’s Improved Word Experience Understands How You Work
Harvey’s Word Add-In is enhanced, providing a more intuitive and reliable experience for legal work.
by
Harvey Team
•
Nov 18, 2025
Legal professionals spend most of their day in Microsoft Word drafting, reviewing, and refining complex documents that shape meaningful outcomes. We built Harvey’s Word Add-In to help lawyers move faster, directly in the environment they’re already working in.
The results speak for themselves — customers like
Talanx
use Word Add-In to speed up review times for certain tasks by up to 60%, allowing them to shift towards more strategic work.
We’ve continued to invest in the product, and now, we’re taking that experience to the next level.
Harvey’s improved Word experience delivers a more intuitive and more reliable way to work, with a redesigned interface, playbook review enhancements, and new workflows. With these updates, our Word Add-In better understands how legal teams work, so they can complete complex legal tasks with less friction and more control.
Intuitive Editing, From Start to Finish
We’re updating the Word Add-In homepage to optimize for clarity, personalization, and responsiveness. The new homepage experience will include suggestions for prompts based on the document type, helping users immediately discover the right actions to take.
Greater Control for Playbook Reviews
Contract review requires high precision and clarity. Teams already use playbook features to quickly generate accurate first drafts, precision edits, and redlines, reducing drafting and review times. To help these legal teams move with confidence, we’re enhancing the
playbook review experience
to provide more granular controls and insights into contracting.
Harvey now provides a complete view of all contractual changes, including redlines not tied to a specific playbook rule, making sure teams never miss a counterparty redline. Early next year, the Word Add-In will also allow teams to specify which party they represent and indicate whether the contract is on first-party or third-party paper. With that context, Harvey will tailor its playbook commentary and recommendations to the right perspective.
Additionally, required language flags will be available in December, helping teams review consistently across every deal. Instead of searching for prior examples or precedent, teams will be able to review Harvey’s suggested language directly in the document they’re editing and redline instantly.
These updates will help teams quickly complete contract reviews without sacrificing quality.
Easily Edit and Review With New Workflows
Workflows in Harvey’s Word Add-In help lawyers address common tasks with just one click. To cover more use cases across the contracting lifecycle, we’re continuing to add new workflows to Word Add-In:
Redact:
Quickly scan documents for any sensitive information, like PII, and automatically remove it to make files anonymous — helping save time and preserve data security.
Convert to a Template:
Turn any document into a reusable template by converting detected fields, like names and addresses, into placeholders.
Review Definitions:
Easily identify and resolve any inconsistencies in defined terms used in a contract, including missing or outdated definitions. This workflow, available in December, will help teams ensure that contract terms are consistent, precise, and compliant.
Receive More Reliable Results With Embedded Knowledge Sources
To help users ground results in reliable sources, we will integrate 80+
region-specific knowledge sources
directly into the Word Add-In, with more being added in the future. This means users will be able to reference authoritative sources as context when drafting and editing — improving overall accuracy and quality.
Harvey’s improved Word experience is our latest step towards helping legal teams work seamlessly and efficiently across
Microsoft 365
. With smarter automation, sharper insights, and precise controls, Harvey meets lawyers where they are so they can do their best work — right where it happens.

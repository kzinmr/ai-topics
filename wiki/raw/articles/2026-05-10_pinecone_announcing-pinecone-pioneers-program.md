AI is evolving fast—and so is the way people build and share what they know. We’ve seen developers in the Pinecone community doing a lot more than ship code. They’re writing tutorials, hosting meetups, helping others get unstuck, and creating content other developers refer to.
We want to support that work. So we’ve launched the
Pinecone Pioneers
program.
Pinecone Pioneers is our way of recognizing and supporting the people who push the boundaries of making AI knowledgeable —those working on agentic AI, search, personalized assistants, and everything in between. The goal is to elevate their work, open up opportunities for collaboration, and give back in meaningful ways.
This first cohort includes people from all over the world, working across startups, research, and enterprise.

About the Pinecone Pioneers program
What it is
This program recognizes people who are already out there doing the work—building with Pinecone, teaching others, sharing what they’ve learned, and helping the broader AI ecosystem move forward.
It’s not a promotional campaign or a perk club. It’s a simple way to back the people who are showing up and contributing meaningfully.
What Pioneers get
🧭
Early product roadmap
— Be the first to know what’s shipping and where we’re headed
📣
Amplification
— We’ll help more people see what you’re doing
💬
Direct access to the team
— Feedback goes both ways
🎟️
Support for talks and events
— We’ll help you get there, whether that’s a conference or a meetup
🧵
Private space for Pioneers
— A quieter place to share ideas
Who it’s for
We’re looking for people who are actively building and sharing. That could mean:
Devs shipping demos, tools, or integrations
Educators writing technical posts or tutorials
Organizers running events or community conversations
Engineers putting vector search into production
Or anyone else contributing in a meaningful way
You don’t need a huge following. Just a clear point of view and a willingness to share.

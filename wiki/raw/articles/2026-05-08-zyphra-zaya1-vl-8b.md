## Summary

Zyphra released ZAYA1-VL-8B, their first vision-language model. It's an MoE with 700M active/8B total params, built on ZAYA1-8B base. Key architectural innovations: vision-specific LoRA parameters and bidirectional attention for image tokens. Trained on AMD GPUs, Apache 2.0 license. Strong at document understanding, OCR, spatial perception, grounding, and GUI tasks.

# Ghost Side Control Escape System (BJJ)

Source: https://grantslatton.com/bjj-ghost-escape

Ghost Escape
Early in my BJJ practice, I got really good at this somewhat unorthodox side control escape system. It's the mainstay of Coach Brian Petersen.
Unfortunately, the system's split between a bunch of videos that all teach different aspects of the system that have been uploaded haphazardly over the years.
Because people I spar with always ask me about the system, I decided to make an organized supercut.

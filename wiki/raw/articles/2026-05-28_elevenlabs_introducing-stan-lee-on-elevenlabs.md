ElevenLabs is proud to announce a landmark partnership with Stan Lee Universe, bringing the voice, likeness, and spirit of one of pop culture's most beloved icons to our platform - across voice, image, and music.
The voice of a legend
Stan Lee's voice is now available on the
ElevenLabs Iconic Marketplace
and the
Eleven Reader app
. Crafted from professional recordings that capture his unmistakable warmth, wit, and energy, this voice clone lets fans experience Stan's narration and storytelling in a manner that feels true to the man himself.
The partnership also marks the launch of the
Stan Lee Book of the Month Club
inside Eleven Reader. It’s a monthly celebration of the literature Stan loved, beginning with June's selection:
Treasure Island
by Robert Louis Stevenson: A perfect summer opener for the adventurous. Pirates, treasure maps, and high-seas action. It’s short and propulsive, just the way Stan would've liked it.
ElevenLabs is committed to adding one public domain work featured in the Stan Lee Book of the Month Club to the platform over the next 12 months, honoring his lifelong love of literature and making the classics accessible to all.
His image, reimagined
Stan Lee's likeness is coming to the
ElevenLabs Creative Templates
For a limited time, users will be able to generate visual content featuring Stan Lee - in the spirit of the cameos that cemented his legend. All use is governed by mutually approved safety guidelines for personal, non-commercial use only. Licensed commercial use of his likeness is available through the Stan Lee Universe team via ElevenLabs.

Built for fans
This partnership was designed from the ground up to honor Stan's legacy while giving fans something they've never had before: a real, living connection to the man behind the myths.
"Stan always believed in meeting his fans where they were: in the pages of a comic, at a convention, or in a quick on-screen cameo. This partnership is a way of continuing that. Fans have always told us that when they read his comics, they hear the words in Stan's voice, and now, thanks to ElevenLabs, we can make that a reality. His voice, his image, his love of storytelling...ElevenLabs gives us a way to keep that alive and in fans' hands in a way that's true to who he was."
- Chaz Rainey, Stan Lee Universe

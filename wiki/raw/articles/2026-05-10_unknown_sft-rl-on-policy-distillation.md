## Context from web search
On-policy distillation (OPD) is an active research area. Related frameworks include GKD (Agarwal et al., 2024), MiniLLM (Gu et al., 2024), DistiLLM (Ko et al., 2024), and G-OPD (Yang et al., 2026). OPD trains the student on its own generations rather than teacher-generated outputs, reducing distribution mismatch between training and inference. Recent extensions explore self-distillation, reward extrapolation, and selective imitation.

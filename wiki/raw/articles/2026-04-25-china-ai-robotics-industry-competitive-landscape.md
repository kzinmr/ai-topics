## Key Statistics (2025-2026)

- **Global robot installations**: China = 52% of worldwide total (2025)
- **Market size**: $18.4B in 2024, growing at 15.2% CAGR, projected $43B by 2027
- **Robot density**: 392 robots per 10,000 manufacturing workers
- **Operational stock**: >2 million units (4x Japan, 5x U.S. & South Korea)
- **Humanoid shipments**: China controlled ~90% of global shipments in 2025
- **Patents**: >28,000 robotics patents filed in 2024
- **Government investment**: $12B in robotics R&D funding
- **AI integration target**: 90% nationwide by 2035 (15th Five-Year Plan)

## Core Thesis

The U.S. leads in foundational AI models and semiconductor design, but China is winning on **implementation, data acquisition, and robotics deployment**. China's state-coordinated industrial strategy ("Made in China 2025") combined with a demographic crisis driving automation adoption creates a compounding advantage.

As Max Fenkell (Scale AI) stated: *"The U.S. is winning on the dimensions that Washington tracks—the models and the chips. But we are losing where the future will be decided—data and implementation."*

## U.S. vs China Strategic Divergence

| Dimension | United States | China |
|-----------|---------------|-------|
| Approach | Free-market driven, fast innovation | State-coordinated industrial policy |
| AI strategy | White House AI master plan (Mar 2026, 4 pages, non-binding) | AI woven into energy, military, governance, labor policy |
| Robotics policy | Early debate stages | Subsidized, cross-sector coordinated |
| Data strategy | Internet-scraped data for LLMs | Real-world embodied AI data from robots |
| Open-source | Costly consumer models | Free accessibility, Alibaba Qwen has 100,000+ derivatives on Hugging Face |
| Demographics | Aging but slower decline | Birth rate at record low, UN expects >50% population loss by 2100 |
| Energy infrastructure | Lagging in data center power supply | Unprecedented grid buildout, integrated compute networks |

## AGI Race Implications

- **U.S. Focus**: Internet-scraped data hitting resource limits
- **China Focus**: Real-world embodied AI data from robots operating in households, workplaces, dynamic environments — creating a "limitless" data ecosystem
- 15th Five-Year Plan explicitly highlights AGI as a strategic goal
- China's open-source strategy targets Global South, creating feedback loops reinforcing industrial dominance

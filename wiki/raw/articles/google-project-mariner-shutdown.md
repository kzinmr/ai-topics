# Google Project Mariner Shutdown

Google has quietly shut down Project Mariner, its Chrome-browsing AI agent designed to complete tasks on users' behalf. Project Mariner was originally highlighted on stage at Google I/O 2025.

**Shutdown date**: May 2026

**Reason**: Poor performance and high operational costs

**Context**: Project Mariner was one of several browser-based AI agents announced in 2025. Unlike competitors, it struggled to achieve reliable task completion rates.

Try Mixes today
Today we're launching Mixes on ElevenMusic: a personalized playlist built from your listening history, refreshed every Sunday with 33 tracks curated around your taste.
It's not a genre station or algorithmic radio feed. It's 33 tracks, just for you which get better every week.
Discovery that moves with you
Every play, save, skip, like, and generation on ElevenMusic builds your sound profile. The more signal you give it, the more precisely your Mix reflects the moods, genres, and styles you keep returning to.
That means your Mix isn't static. It updates each week with a new selection shaped by what you've been listening to and creating so discovery stays current as your taste evolves.
Tracks generated exclusively for you
Some of the 33 tracks in your Mix aren't just curated from the ElevenMusic library. They're generated specifically for you. Their tracks that don't exist anywhere else, made for one listener, you.
Pro users receive 11 generated tracks in their Mix each week. Free users receive 5.
How your Mix works
As you use ElevenMusic, your listening and generation activity builds your sound profile. Once there's enough signal, your Mix card appears on your home screen.
From there, it refreshes every Sunday. If you hear something you love, heart it. Your Mix will keep moving forward as your taste evolves.
New to ElevenMusic? Your Mix will pull from the library's trending tracks until you've built up enough listening history. Heart 3 tracks or spend some time listening and it starts learning your taste immediately.
The start of deeper personalisation
Mixes is the first step toward a more personalized ElevenMusic experience.
We're building toward music that understands mood, genre affinity, artist patterns, and generation behaviour — then uses those signals to create something that feels increasingly precise. The goal isn't to recommend music to you. It's to generate around you.

Mixes is available now on web and iOS.
Open ElevenMusic and find your Mix on the home screen.

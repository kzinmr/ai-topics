Here’s a rundown of our major releases in September, and where to read more about them.
Evaluate RAG Correctness
The new
Evaluation API
helps evaluate the correctness and completeness of responses from Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems. It’s currently in
public preview, and comes built into our Assistant
.
Choose the Model for Pinecone Assistant
Pinecone Assistant now allows users to select a
specific Large Language Model (LLM)
for their assistant, giving developers greater control over the assistant’s responses. Additionally, you can now filter the assistant’s responses based on
metadata
, enabling more refined and tailored interactions.
Marketplace Billing Integration with All Major Clouds
You can now connect your Pinecone organization to the
AWS Marketplace
,
GCP Marketplace
, or
Azure Marketplace
Marketplace for seamless billing integration. This makes it easier to manage subscriptions and optimize costs through your preferred cloud provider.
Additional Integration Guides for Data Pipelines
New integration guides for
Datavolo
and
Matillio
n can help you get started composing your data pipelines quickly and easily

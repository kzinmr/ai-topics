Singapore data residency is now available to ElevenLabs Enterprise customers. Companies operating in or serving Singapore can now build and scale use cases across ElevenAgents, ElevenCreative, and ElevenAPI while meeting local data residency, security, and latency requirements.
Whether you are an enterprise building multilingual voice customer support, real-time voice interfaces, or producing content at scale, your data and inference can now stay entirely within Singapore.
What this means for enterprises in the region
Local hosting of inference and storage:
core ElevenLabs models run entirely within Singapore-based infrastructure
Compliant and secure deployment:
built to meet Singapore's data residency and security requirements, with enterprise-grade controls including SOC2 and Zero Retention options
Low latency
: hosting in-region further reduces latency for users in Singapore and East Asia
How to get access
Singapore data residency is available exclusively to ElevenLabs Enterprise customers.
Existing Enterprise customers interested in enabling it can contact their dedicated customer success manager for implementation details.
Enterprises considering ElevenLabs Enterprise with Singapore data residency can
contact our sales team
to discuss specific needs.
Our commitment to Singapore
ElevenLabs is investing in Singapore as a core market. We are giving enterprises here the infrastructure, security, and capabilities to scale responsibly, and we are already working with companies like Scoot (the low-cost subsidiary of Singapore Airlines), Atome, Funding Societies to put voice at the center of their agent and customer engagement workflows.

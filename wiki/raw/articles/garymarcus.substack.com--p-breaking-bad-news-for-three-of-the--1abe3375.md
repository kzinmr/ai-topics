A month later, it
already
looks like the moment has passed; tokenmaxxing may have jacked up Anthropic’s second quarter, but it looks like it won’t last.  Nobody is going to be comfortable accidentally burning half a billions of tokens in month. Budgets will tighten, especially when people realize just how costly agents (which burn orders of magnitudes more tokens) are.
Axios quotes Ali Ansari, CEO of a model training firm known as Micro1, as  saying  “The enterprise is undergoing a "healthy swing" away from AI overuse — or "tokenmaxxing," the push to burn as many AI tokens as possible” – which means less revenue for Anthropic and others. Or lower prices and even more challenges in being profitable.

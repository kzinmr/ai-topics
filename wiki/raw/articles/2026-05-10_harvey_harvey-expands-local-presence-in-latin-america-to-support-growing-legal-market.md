:Harvey: Expands Local Presence in Latin America to Support Growing Legal Market
Announcing the launch of a dedicated Harvey team in Mexico.
by
Harvey Team
•
Aug 25, 2025
Harvey is expanding its presence in Latin America with the launch of a dedicated team in Mexico, reinforcing our commitment to legal professionals across Latin America and marking another step in our global growth strategy.
The legal sector is evolving quickly throughout Latin America, with increasing demand for technology that enables speed, accuracy, and scale. Harvey is already working with over 80 forward-thinking legal teams in 13 countries across Latin America, including most of the leading firms in the region like
Mijares
, and incorporating local knowledge sources in the platform. This local investment allows us to provide closer, more responsive support for firms leading the way.
“Latin America represents a significant opportunity for Harvey,” said Winston Weinberg, Co-Founder and CEO of Harvey. “We’re building a team on the ground to partner directly with our clients, learn from the region’s legal leaders, and support the adoption of AI across a fast-growing market.”
With a local team, we’re positioned to engage more deeply with firms across Mexico and Latin America — helping them unlock the full value of Harvey’s platform while meeting the unique needs of their jurisdiction and practice.
As always, we remain committed to privacy, security, and responsible AI, aligned with both global and regional regulatory standards.

# Reflexive AI Usage is Now a Baseline Expectation at Shopify

**Author:** Tobi Lütke (Shopify CEO), reported by First Round Capital
**Tweet:** 2025-04-07 — 6,482 bookmarks, 2.50M impressions

## Key Points

- Tobi Lütke declared "Reflexive AI usage is now a baseline expectation at Shopify"
- Before hiring anyone new, managers must prove AI can't do the job better
- "Using AI well is a skill that needs to be carefully learned" by "using it a lot"

## Shopify's AI Adoption Strategy (from First Round analysis)

### Insight 1: Let Anyone Use the Expensive Models
- High-value use cases can come from anywhere
- 1,500 Cursor licenses ordered, then another 1,500
- Fastest growing users: support and revenue, not engineering

### Tactic: Default to "Yes" with Legal
- "If you don't default to 'yes,' you're defaulting to 'no'"
- Early adoption of GitHub Copilot (80% adoption)
- Legal team collaboration from the start

### Tactic: Unlimited Spend on AI Tools
- No quota on AI tool spending
- Internal leaderboard of token usage as proxy for value
- Remove friction for AI adoption

### Insight 2: Make AI Show Its Work
- Transparency in AI decision-making processes

### Insight 3: Beginner's Mindset
- Foster beginner's mindset, hire more beginners
- Value adaptability over entrenched expertise

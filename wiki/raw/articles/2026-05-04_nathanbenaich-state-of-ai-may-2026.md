# State of AI: May 2026

**Source**: Nathan Benaich, State of AI newsletter, May 4, 2026
**URL**: https://nathanbenaich.substack.com/p/state-of-ai-may-2026

## Key Themes

### Cyber Crossed a Threshold
- Claude Mythos Preview: first model to clear AISI's "The Last Ones" (TLO) range — 3/10 runs end-to-end, 73% expert tasks
- GPT-5.5: 2/10 end-to-end, 71.4% expert tasks — near-identical profile just 3 weeks later
- AISI estimates frontier cyber-offence capability doubling every 4 months (was 7 months at end of 2025)
- Static-signature and rules-based security vendors face existential crisis

### Microsoft-OpenAI Reset
- Original 2019 deal renegotiated: Microsoft remains primary cloud partner, retains non-exclusive IP licence through 2032
- OpenAI secured right to multi-source compute (Oracle, CoreWeave)
- AGI clause replaced with granular capability gates and narrower revenue-sharing

### China's Coding Sprint
- Kimi K2.6, MiniMax M2.7, Z.ai GLM-5.1 landed within 12 days of each other
- All scoring 56-59 on SWE-Bench Pro
- All open-weights
- All priced below Western equivalents
- "The 'China is six to nine months behind' framing no longer works for agentic coding"

### Anthropic Funding
- Additional $40B from Google
- $5B from Amazon (packaged with $100B AWS spend)
- Chip deals with Google and Broadcom worth hundreds of billions

### Agents Meet Real Markets
- AI labs deploying agents at scale face procurement and compliance friction
- Joint ventures (Anthropic/Blackstone, OpenAI/TPG) signal deployment services as new battleground

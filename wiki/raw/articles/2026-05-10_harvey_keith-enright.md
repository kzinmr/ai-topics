Keith Enright is Chief Strategy Officer at Harvey AI. He previously served as Co-Chair of Gibson Dunn’s global Artificial Intelligence Practice Group and Tech & Innovation Industry Group, advising clients worldwide on complex legal and business issues. Prior to that, he spent nearly 14 years at Google, where he served as Chief Privacy Officer and Vice President, leading the company’s global privacy and consumer protection legal functions. Keith also serves on public, private, and nonprofit boards, with a focus on entrepreneurship and advancing the benefits of technology for society.

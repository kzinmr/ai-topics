# OpenAI Plans Biggest-Ever ChatGPT Overhaul, Shifting to 'Intent Router'

**Source:** Reuters, syndicating an exclusive Financial Times report  
**Date:** June 7, 2026  
**Archived via:** Yahoo Japan News (Reuters Japanese syndication)

## Summary (from Yahoo Japan / Reuters)

On June 7, 2026, the Financial Times reported that OpenAI is planning the biggest-ever overhaul of its conversational AI "ChatGPT," aiming to evolve it into a "super app" equipped with automatic code-writing capabilities and autonomous task execution features, ahead of its planned IPO.

## Key Facts from the Report

1. **"Intent Router" Overhaul**: OpenAI plans to shift ChatGPT from a traditional chatbot to an "intent router" — a unified interface that understands user intent and routes requests to the appropriate model, tool, application, or AI agent.

2. **Biggest-Ever Redesign**: The FT described this as the largest overhaul in ChatGPT's history, citing interviews with more than 10 current and former OpenAI employees.

3. **IPO Preparation**: The restructuring comes as OpenAI prepares for an initial public offering (IPO), aiming to expand revenue by focusing resources on the highly profitable enterprise market.

4. **Competitive Pressure**: The reorganization is partly aimed at strengthening OpenAI's competitive position against rival Anthropic.

5. **"Super App" Vision**: The goal is to evolve ChatGPT into a "super app" with capabilities including:
   - Automatic program/code writing
   - Autonomous task execution on behalf of users
   - Intent understanding and intelligent routing

6. **Product Reorganization**: The overhaul is part of a broader product reorganization under OpenAI's leadership, with Thibault Sottiaux playing a key role in the restructuring.

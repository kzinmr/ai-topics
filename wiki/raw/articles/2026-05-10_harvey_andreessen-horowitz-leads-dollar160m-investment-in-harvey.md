Andreessen Horowitz Leads $160M Investment in :Harvey:
by
Winston Weinberg
and
Gabe Pereyra
•
Dec 4, 2025
Today, Harvey announced a $160M investment at an $8B valuation led by Andreessen Horowitz. The round follows EQT’s recent investment in Harvey and features new investors WndrCo and accounts advised by T. Rowe Price Associates, Inc. as well as returning investors in Sequoia, Kleiner Perkins, Conviction, and Elad Gil.
As part of this investment, Harvey will complete our first tender offer. The announcement comes as part of our growth trajectory this year, which includes over 50% of the Am Law 100 law firms using Harvey alongside in-house legal teams including Bridgewater, Comcast, and Carvana.

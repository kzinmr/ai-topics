# Corti Launches Symphony for Speech-to-Text

**Source:** [Corti Newsroom](https://www.corti.ai/newsroom/corti-launches-symphony-for-speech-to-text)
**Date:** May 20, 2026

## Overview

Corti, the frontier lab for clinical-grade AI, launched Symphony for Speech-to-Text — clinical-grade speech-to-text models for real-time dictation, conversational transcription, and batch audio file processing.

## Performance Highlights

- **93% lower WER**: 1.4% WER in English vs 17.7% (OpenAI), 18.1% (ElevenLabs), 17.4% (Whisper), 18.9% (Parakeet)
- **98.3% recall** on formatted entities (dosages, measurements, dates) vs 44.3% best baseline
- **Surpasses Dragon Medical One**: 4.6% WER vs Dragon's 5.7% (19% improvement), medical term recall 93.5% vs 92.9%
- **Multilingual**: 2.4% WER in German (vs 13.0%), 3.9% WER in French (vs 10.6%)

## Key Quote

> "In the agentic era, speech recognition requires more than simply producing a transcript - we need to give AI systems accurate clinical facts to reason from. If a model mishears a medication, dosage, or symptom, every downstream step becomes less reliable." — Andreas Cleve, CEO

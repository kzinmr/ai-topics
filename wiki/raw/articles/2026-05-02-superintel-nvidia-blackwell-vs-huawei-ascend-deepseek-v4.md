# NVIDIA Blackwell vs. Huawei Ascend: Did DeepSeek V4 Prove China Doesn't Need Western Silicon?

**Author:** Kim "Chubby" Isenberg
**Date:** May 2, 2026
**Publication:** Superintel+ (getsuperintel.com)
**Note:** Paywalled — only free preview extracted

## Preview Content

DeepSeek released V4, a 1.6 trillion parameter model running on Huawei's domestic Ascend chips, matching or beating the best Western models on key benchmarks, at API prices 90 to 97 percent lower.

### Key Claims (from preview):

1. **SMIC's quadruple-patterning workaround** bypasses banned EUV machines to produce advanced chips
2. **DeepSeek engineering** pushed Huawei chip utilization from ~60% to over 85%
3. **Classified Shenzhen lab** where recruited former ASML engineers built a functioning EUV prototype — claimed to be a decade ahead of Western intelligence projections
4. DeepSeek V4 confirmed to run on **Huawei Ascend 950** for inference and deployment
5. Training hardware remains ambiguous — may still rely on NVIDIA GPUs (ChinaTalk, 04/27/2026)
6. The strategic significance: DeepSeek has built a frontier model that no longer depends on Western hardware to operate at scale

The article argues that Western assumptions about China being 10-15 years behind in AI chips were wrong — the gap was closed in roughly 3 years.

What's New: May Disclosure 2026
This month, Hebbia expanded what’s possible with Chat, Matrix, and more.
This month, we launched spreadsheets and branded multi-slide decks directly in Chat, upgrades to Matrix, and more.
Spreadsheets and Presentations Improvements
Build Spreadsheets
Generate and edit custom Excel files from a Chat prompt. Simply describe the spreadsheet you need, upload a reference file to match your preferred template, and Hebbia builds it in the app.
Create Multi-Slide Decks
Generate client-ready PowerPoint presentations from a single Chat prompt, then refine as needed: edit layouts and sources, reorder, add, and delete slides, all in your firm's branding.
New Agents and Skills
Email Agent in Projects [beta]
Intern is the agent you can email to run analysis, generate slides, and build spreadsheets without leaving your inbox. Now email the Intern associated with each Project to get live deal updates, pull market data, or generate files tied to specific work.
Scaling Expertise with New Skills
Skills are purpose-built instruction sets that codify how expert professionals perform specific tasks. We've added 30+ to a growing library for bankers, lawyers, and investors—trigger them by natural prompting or type "/" to browse all available Skills.
Matrix as Your Workflow Engine
Matrix Column Manager
One place to control every column in your Matrix. Rename, reorder, edit prompts, swap models, and run bulk operations across your entire analysis to speed up your workflows.
Matrix as a Column Source
Reference any existing Matrix as a source within a new Matrix column. Build on prior analysis without starting from scratch.
Finance-tailored Chat
Public Company Overviews
Look up any public company and get an instant snapshot with the company’s business model, financials, recent filings, and key metrics from CapIQ, all within Chat.
Platform Upgrades
New LLMs in Hebbia
Access Claude Opus 4.7 and GPT 5.5 in Chat.
Automated User Provisioning at Scale
SCIM support and automated user provisioning so that user lifecycle management scales with your firm.

AI Summary by Glean
Glean Apps and Glean Actions allow marketing teams to automate tasks and generate content using a no-code environment, enhancing efficiency and creativity.
Marketers can build custom AI assistants, copilots, chatbots, and agents to streamline workflows and jumpstart content creation, such as generating social media posts from complex documents.
Glean's platform supports the integration of large language models (LLMs) and enterprise-ready AI tools, enabling seamless automation and content management within the apps marketers use most.
Through Glean Platform, users can now access Glean Apps and Glean Actions to utilize a no-code environment for building custom AI assistants, copilots, chatbots, and agents. Choose the large language model (LLM) of your choice and stay in the apps you use most, while streamlining and automating your day-to-day workflows with enterprise-ready AI assistance.
In this blog, we’ll be going over how marketing teams can use Glean Apps and Actions to automate and jumpstart everyday tasks without the hassle of starting from scratch.
Jumpstart great work
Starting new content can be one of the most challenging aspects of the creative process for marketers. Whether it's summarizing intricate topics or compiling information from multiple sources, numerous obstacles can arise when trying to craft a great piece of content.
Marketers aiming to streamline this process can leverage an assistant built through Glean Apps. For instance, a marketing manager can use a Social Media Assistant to quickly generate a concise LinkedIn post from a complex survey results document, ensuring it maintains the appropriate tone of voice.
Even better, once the content is reviewed and approved, Glean Actions can take over and publish the post with just a click of a button!
Customized AI experiences for enterprise workflows
Glean Apps and Glean Actions is a great way for marketers to take information and use enterprise-ready AI to build and launch new content. There’s also many other ways for enterprise workers to use Glean Apps to create custom AI assistants, copilots, chatbots, and agents to do great work.

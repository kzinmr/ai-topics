# Google Flow Music and Believe bring next-gen tools to artists

**By Seth Forsgren, Group Product Manager, Google Flow Music** | May 6, 2026

## Partnership Announcement

Google Flow Music (formerly ProducerAI) announced a partnership with Believe, the global artist development company, to bring AI music creation tools directly to Believe and TuneCore's artists, producers, and songwriters.

## What is Flow Music?

Flow Music is a Google Labs product that acts as a creative collaborator for musicians:
- Helps with lyrics
- Explores new melodies and genres
- Creates new instruments
- Supports early-stage exploration and final polish

It was built by musicians (including Seth Forsgren and his bandmates) working closely with a small community of artists.

## Key Details

- **Powered by Lyria 3 Pro** — Google's latest music generation model
- **Artist ownership**: Google doesn't claim ownership of original content generated with Flow Music
- **Believe partnership**: Believe is offering Flow Music to their artist roster as a space for creative exploration and iteration
- **Google Labs**: Flow Music is part of Google Labs, Google's experimental product incubator

## Significance

This represents a major step in bringing AI music generation tools from research into production with a major distribution partner. The focus on artist ownership and creative collaboration (rather than replacement) positions Flow Music as a tool for augmentation rather than automation. The Lyria 3 Pro model suggests continued investment in specialized music generation capabilities.

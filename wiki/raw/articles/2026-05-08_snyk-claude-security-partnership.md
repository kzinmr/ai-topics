# Snyk Integrates Anthropic Claude into AI Security Platform

Snyk announced the integration of Anthropic's Claude models into the Snyk AI Security Platform.

## Key Details

- Claude's reasoning capabilities power both vulnerability discovery and fix generation
- LLMs are finding vulnerabilities faster than organizations can address them — Snyk ranks findings for priority and creates fixes within developer workflows
- Claude enables sharper discovery and faster, higher-confidence remediation

Manoj Nair (Chief Innovation Officer, Snyk): "As AI dramatically accelerates how fast developers can write code, traditional security simply cannot keep up. By leveraging Claude's advanced reasoning within the Snyk AI Security Platform, we are equipping enterprises with an intelligent, autonomous defense system that scales right alongside their AI-driven innovation."

## Availability

Available to joint customers immediately, with expanded access rolling out through 2026.

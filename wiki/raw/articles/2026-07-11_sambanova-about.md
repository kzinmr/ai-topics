# SambaNova — Company Overview

**Tagline**: The Fastest AI Inference Platform

**Description**: SambaNova is the complete AI platform delivering the fastest AI inference, fine-tuning, and scalable solutions for agentic AI easily integrated into existing data center infrastructures.

**Key facts**:
- Founded: 2017 by Rodrigo Liang (CEO), Kunle Olukotun, Chris Ré
- HQ: Palo Alto, California
- Total funding: $1.1B+ (Series D at $5B+ valuation)
- Key product: SN40L RDU (Reconfigurable Dataflow Unit) — custom AI chip
- SambaNova Cloud: inference API for open-source models (Llama, DeepSeek, etc.)
- Known for: highest token/s benchmarks on open-source models, Cerebras competitor

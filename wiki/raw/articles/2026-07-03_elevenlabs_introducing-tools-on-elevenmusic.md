Every musician knows the feeling, a melody stuck in your head you can't get out, a vocal take that's right but rough, a finished track that's almost there but needs to go somewhere new. The gap between the idea and the result has always been where most music dies. Tools close that gap.
Tools are purpose-built ways to create, reshape, and evolve your music. Each one starts from a different point in the process, so wherever you are with a song, there's a tool that meets you there.
Start with whatever you have
Voice to Song takes a raw recording of you singing, off-pitch, phone-recorded, imperfect, and returns a faithful, studio-quality version of that exact performance. It keeps your melody, phrasing, and lyrics, and fixes the rest: pitch, timing, breath, and combines it with a full professional arrangement.
Loop Studio starts even earlier. Pick a genre and a BPM, and get back a tight, seamless instrumental loop, drums, bass, one strong motif, built to repeat cleanly with no seam. A quick and easy way to create something playable to build a track around.
Take a finished track further
Tools aren't just for starting. Genreshift keeps your voice and melody intact but rebuilds everything else - instrumentation, production, feel — in a completely different genre.
Meanwhile, Unplugged does the opposite. It strips a finished track down to an intimate, acoustic version, keeping the original melody and lyrics front and center.
Built for the full creative journey
Tools are designed for every stage of making music, from the first idea to the final version. They are a faster, smarter way to create.

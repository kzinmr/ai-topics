# Introducing Daily Chat

Source: https://dayoneapp.com/blog/introducing-daily-chat/

Today, Day One introduces
Daily Chat
, a conversational journaling experience that helps you capture and reflect on your day through a simple back-and-forth chat. Your conversation becomes a journal entry that preserves your words, your mood, and the moments that matter most.
It’s available now on iPhone, iPad, Android, Windows, and the web, found on the new Today Tab with
Day One Gold
.
Video Demo:
Daily Chat on iPhone
Why Daily Chat Exists
Some days are full of moments worth remembering, but sitting down to write about them can feel like the hardest part. Not because the moments weren’t meaningful, but because translating a lived experience into words on a blank page takes a kind of energy that isn’t always there.
Daily Chat was designed for those moments. Rather than starting with a blank entry, you start with a conversation. Think of it as a quiet back-and-forth that helps you slow down, recall what happened, and notice what it meant. Let a thoughtful follow-up question draw out something you might not have written on your own.
The result is a journal entry that reflects what you actually experienced, captured in your own words and shaped by reflection.
How Daily Chat Supports Deeper Journaling
Journals hold their deepest value when they capture more than just what happened. The best entries hold context, emotion, and meaning — not only the events of a day, but how those events felt and why they mattered.
Daily Chat supports this kind of memory preservation by guiding you through your day with care:
Follow-up questions surface what you might otherwise forget.
A prompt like “How did that make you feel?” or “What stood out about that moment?” can help you move from a surface-level recap to a richer, more personal reflection.
Your words stay your words.
The journal entry generated from your conversation reflects the language, tone, and mood of what you shared. Your voice remains at the center.
Small moments get captured, too.
Conversations have a way of uncovering the quieter details of a day — the ones that rarely make it into a traditional journal entry but often carry the most meaning over time.
How Daily Chat Works
Open Daily Chat.
Open the new “Today” tab to find the Daily Chat section and start a new conversation.
Talk through your day.
Share what happened, what you noticed, or what’s on your mind. Daily Chat will respond with thoughtful follow-up questions to help you reflect more deeply.
Generate a journal entry.
When you’re ready, turn your conversation into a journal entry. The entry reflects your words, your tone, and your mood. Review and edit, or save and return to refine later.
Resume and update anytime.
Come back to a previous conversation to add new details or continue reflecting. Updated conversations can update the daily entry to merge any edits to the entry or updates from chat.
Daily Chat also includes “Voice Mode” for a hands-free voice chat experience. Tap on the button to the right side of the text input to begin Voice chat.
It Remembers What Matters to You
Daily Chat includes a Memory system that learns from your conversations over time. As you chat, it extracts key details — people, places, interests, things you care about — and uses them as context in future conversations. The more you use Daily Chat, the more personal and relevant it becomes.
Memories are synced to your account using the same end-to-end encryption as your journal entries, so they’re as private as everything else in Day One. A full Memory manager is coming in a future update, giving you visibility into what’s been remembered and control over every detail.
Daily Chat is Part of Day One Gold
Daily Chat is available with
Day One Gold
, a new tier designed for deeper reflection and memory preservation. The value grows over time — as you build a habit of reflective conversation, the entries become richer, and the practice becomes more personal.
Privacy and Trust
Journaling is one of the most personal things a person can do. Adding AI to that experience requires care, transparency, and respect for boundaries. Daily Chat was built with those principles in mind.
Fully opt-in.
Daily Chat is completely optional. AI features are only active if enabled from the opt-in prompt or in Settings > AI Features.
Nothing is stored or used for training.
When you chat with AI, only the messages you choose to send are temporarily processed to generate responses or journal entries. Conversations are securely transmitted using encrypted protocols (HTTPS) and are never stored or used for AI training by Day One or our AI partners. Once processing is complete, the content is deleted and only stored encrypted.
End-to-end encryption is respected.
If your journals are end-to-end encrypted, content is temporarily decrypted on your device only while AI generates a response, then re-encrypted immediately afterward.
You remain in full control.
You decide when to use Daily Chat, what to share, and whether to save the generated entry. Nothing happens without your intent.
Day One is continuously exploring on-device AI models to make these experiences even more private in the future. Until then, the same commitment to privacy that has defined Day One from the beginning applies to every AI feature.
Getting Started
Daily Chat is available with Day One Gold on iPhone, iPad, Android, Windows, and the web. Open the new Today tab to find Daily Chat to begin your first conversation.
The moments of your day are worth preserving. Daily Chat helps you capture them with the context, emotion, and meaning they deserve.

# MUFG aims to become AI-native with OpenAI

Source: https://openai.com/index/mufg
Date: May 28, 2026

MUFG (Mitsubishi UFJ Financial Group), one of Japan's largest financial groups, is rolling out ChatGPT Enterprise to ~35,000 employees at Mitsubishi UFJ Bank starting 2026.

Key metrics:
- 100% training participation (mandatory e-learning before access)
- 1,800+ custom GPTs ("AI bankers") created in 4 months post-training
- 20-30% workload reduction in research tasks

Customer-facing AI: Moneytree (wealth management), WealthNavi (robo-advisory), emutt digital bank with AI concierge and MAP (Money Advisory Platform).

Core philosophy: AI extends human thinking and creativity; employees and AI learn together. Cultural shift from "What can we use AI for?" to "How should we use it?"

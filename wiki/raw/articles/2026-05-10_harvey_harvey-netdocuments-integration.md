:Harvey:’s Integration With NetDocuments Intelligent DMS
Securely access and use your NetDocuments files directly in Harvey without changing how your organization manages its knowledge.
by
Harvey Team
•
Dec 8, 2025
Legal teams depend on fast, secure access to their documents: research, contracts, briefs, diligence files, and the institutional knowledge that powers their work. For many organizations, that context lives in
NetDocuments
.
Watch Video
Our goal with the NetDocuments intelligent DMS integration is to meet lawyers where they already work, making knowledge available without compromising security or changing how teams manage their documents.
Your Knowledge, Connected to Harvey
With the integration, teams can securely pull NetDocuments files into Harvey without creating local copies or changing existing permissions. Access to files through the integration remains governed by your organization’s NetDocuments access controls — just surfaced directly where you are accessing Harvey.
You can upload files from NetDocuments into Harvey for use in
Assistant
queries or to save in
vaults
. You can also quickly filter by cabinet and search by matter, folder, client, or file to find exactly what you need leveraging NetDocuments search capabilities. And soon, you’ll be able to export work generated in Harvey back to NetDocuments for seamless management.
Say you’re working on due diligence and need to analyze a set of commercial agreements to examine their change of control provisions. From inside the project’s
vault
, create a subfolder called “Commercial Agreements” and then upload the files you need from the equivalent folder in NetDocuments.
Next, run a Change of Control Analysis
workflow
to automatically extract information from the agreements like whether or not there is a change of control provision, which parties are restricted, and how “change of control” is defined.
Removing Friction From Legal Work
By connecting to the systems lawyers rely on every day, we’re building a more seamless, secure workflow that helps teams focus on higher-value analysis instead of manual document handling.
The NetDocuments integration is live in early access. If you’re a current Harvey customer and want to test it, reach out to your customer success manager. If you want to learn more about how Harvey helps legal teams work smarter,
contact our team
.

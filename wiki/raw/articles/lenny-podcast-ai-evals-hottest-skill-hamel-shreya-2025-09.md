## Core Insights

### Evals as PRDs
Well-crafted eval prompts effectively become living product requirements documents that continuously test AI in real time. "Evals are the new PRDs for AI products."

### Error Analysis First
Always start with error analysis before writing evals. Manually review real user traces to uncover upstream issues and patterns.

### Human-in-the-Loop
LLMs cannot replace humans in initial error analysis. Human intuition is required to discover unknown failure modes before automation.

### Minimal Time Investment
After initial setup, maintaining an eval system requires only ~30 minutes per week.

The Brief: October
What's new from Harvey, October 2025
by
Harvey Team
•
Oct 2, 2025
Welcome back to The Brief. This month’s updates focus on flexibility and efficiency, with major upload and processing improvements, streamlined admin controls, and faster tools for drafting and reviewing in Word.
Significant Performance Improvements
Platform upgrades that improve speed, scale, and reliability.
Faster Vault Uploads and Processing
Work with large datasets more efficiently in Vault with uploads that are up to 10x faster and processing speeds that are 53% quicker.
Improved Platform-Wide Document Processing
Get quicker and more accurate results from documents uploaded anywhere in the platform with 89% lower upload latency, 6x fewer document processing errors, and 3x faster Excel processing.
Increased Vault Project Limits
Manage even larger-scale matters with Vault projects now supporting up to 100GB or 100,000 files.
Skip Citations for Hidden Prompts in Custom Workflows
Experience faster custom workflows by automatically excluding citations in hidden prompts.
Work Smarter in Word
A suite of enhancements to Harvey for Word that facilitate easier document editing and review.
Fill and Edit Tables
Speed up first drafts and reduce manual copy-paste by using natural language prompts to populate tables with content from your reference materials.
Magic Prompt and Prompt Library in Ask Mode
Refine prompts in one click or select from a curated set of proven prompts, now integrated directly into Ask Mode.
Query History
Quickly pick up where you left off by viewing your full history of Ask, Edit, and Playbook interactions on each document.
Improved Playbook Review
Review playbook content more easily with clearer redlines, better visibility into reviewed sections, and new options to standardize language.
More Flexible Input and Integrations
Expanded options for how you interact with Harvey and where your documents come from.
Voice to Prompt
Prompt more quickly and naturally by dictating directly in Harvey’s web app using voice input.
Attachments in Emails
Extract deeper insights from email threads by querying across both emails and their attachments.
iManage Integration
Keep files synced across systems with a secure, native integration that lets you work with documents in Harvey and return them to the appropriate matter in iManage in one click.
Magic Prompt in Words to Workflows
Improve the quality of custom workflows with one-click prompt refinement.
Streamlined Workspace and Workflow Management
New features that simplify admin tasks and support large-scale project management.
Admin Opt-in Feature Controls
Discover and activate new features more easily through direct workspace settings and a guided flow for assigning access individually or in bulk.
Updated Workflow Builder Permissions
Maintain oversight while scaling workflow creation. Users can draft and submit workflows for approval, while admins maintain control over publishing to the workspace.
Easier Workflow Block Management
Modify workflows more easily with new block-level actions to quickly adjust the order of individual and group blocks.
That’s all for October. See you next month!

At Fireworks, our mission is to empower developers with the premier toolchain using open models, delivering transparency, steerability, control, privacy, low latency, and cost efficiency.
As agentic products continue gaining widespread adoption, the speed and efficiency of advanced AI models like
DeepSeek R1
have become critical factors for product differentiation. Staying ahead, we continuously push the boundaries of performance and cost-efficiency through innovations like our specialized version of FireAttention and a distributed inference engine tailored specifically for DeepSeek’s unique MLA, MTP, and wide MoE architecture.

Today, we're thrilled to announce exciting new options for deploying DeepSeek on Hopper GPUs, enhancing both speed and throughput. Expect even more advancements as we soon bring Blackwell GPUs into production.

# The Pope appears to understand AI better than Geoffrey Hinton does

**Author**: Gary Marcus
**Published**: 2026-05-31
**Source**: https://garymarcus.substack.com/p/the-pope-appears-to-understand-ai

## Summary

Gary Marcus responds to Geoffrey Hinton's recent interview where Hinton argued that Claude (Anthropic's LLM) possesses genuine consciousness. Marcus argues this fundamentally misunderstands how AI outputs are generated — they are the product of mimicry, not genuine internal states. He draws parallels to his previous critique of Richard Dawkins making similar errors, and contrasts this with Pope Leo XIV's encyclical Magnifica Humanitas, which Marcus argues shows a more nuanced understanding of AI's nature.

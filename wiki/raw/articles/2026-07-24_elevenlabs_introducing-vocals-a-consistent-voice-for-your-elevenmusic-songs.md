With Vocals on ElevenMusic, you can now generate original music with a consistent voice, be it your own or one from our Vocal Library.
Summary
Vocals let you generate original songs that keep a consistent voice across every track.
The Voice-to-Song tool turns a single voice recording into a finished track in seconds.
You can save a reusable Vocal by uploading a selection of your existing music.
The Vocal Library offers ready-made voices if you don't have your own Vocal yet.
Styles bring the same consistency to sound, and can be combined with Vocals in a single generation.
Every uploaded voice and music sample is screened for copyright compliance.
Generate songs with your own voice
Start with a voice recording and generate an original track with your own voice in seconds via our
Voice-to-Song tool
. It's the fastest way to hear new musical ideas performed in your vocal style.
For ongoing projects, create a reusable Vocal by uploading a selection of your existing music. Music v2 learns your vocal tone, texture, and style, so every future generation carries your voice when your Vocal is applied.
Choose from the Vocal Library
If you don't have your own Vocal, you can choose one from the
Vocal Library
and quickly generate songs with a consistent vocal identity. It's a fast way to prototype ideas or build projects around a single voice.
Consistent sound with Styles
We're also bringing Styles to
Music v2
.
Upload your own tracks to create a Style based on your sound, or choose from our growing Style Library. Styles enable you to generate full tracks with stylistic consistency, now with everything Music v2 brings.
Styles can be combined with Vocals, making it possible to generate music with both a consistent voice and a consistent sound.
Built responsibly
Every uploaded voice and music sample is screened for copyright compliance to help protect artists' rights.
Try Vocals and Styles on ElevenMusic
Vocals and Styles are available on
ElevenMusic
and within Finetunes on
ElevenCreative
. Explore ElevenMusic to hear what Vocals and Styles can do, and
create a free account
to try them on your own tracks today.

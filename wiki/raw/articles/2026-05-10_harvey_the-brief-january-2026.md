The Brief: January 2026
Happy new year, and welcome back to The Brief. This month brings deeper intelligence, smoother workflows, and expanded legal research capabilities. From GPT-5.2's transparent reasoning to improved document understanding, these updates help you work faster and more accurately.
by
Harvey Team
•
Jan 12, 2026
More Powerful AI, Better Reasoning
Access Harvey's most advanced models and capabilities for clearer analysis and more accurate outputs.
GPT-5.2 Now Available in Harvey
Work with a more transparent and self-aware model for complex legal work – now available across Assistant, Vault, and Workflow Builder.
Learn more
.
Understanding Tables Inside Documents
Get more accurate, contextually grounded answers from documents by reading both the structure and content of embedded tables in PDFs and Word files.
Learn more
.
Auto Mode in Word
Harvey for Word now automatically selects the right mode – Ask, Edit, or Draft – based on your query.
Learn more
.
Streamlined Work and Organization
Navigate Harvey more efficiently with improved interfaces and unified access to essential tools.
Unified Vault and Assistant Query Creation
Access all Assistant features – including drafting, deep analysis, and magic prompt – directly from any vault, and create review tables from Assistant without switching pages.
Learn more
.
Sort and Organize Files in Vault
Manage your files more efficiently with new sorting and organization capabilities in vaults.
Learn more
.
File Groups in Review Tables
Automatically analyze related documents together instead of manually sorting one-by-one.
Learn more
.
Preserve Verified Cells on Re-Runs
Re-run review table cells while keeping any cells you've already verified, preventing verified or manually edited outputs from being overwritten.
Learn more
.
Workflow Builder Table Redesign
See workflow activity, access permissions, and approvals at a glance with a redesigned table that surfaces key information faster.
Learn more
.
UI Refresh
Navigate more easily with visually distinct sidebars and panels, including updated designs for threads, drafts, and review tables.
Learn more
.
Expanded Legal Research and Jurisdictional Coverage
Query more sources and jurisdictions to support precise, localized legal analysis.
50+ New Legal Research Sources
Access the latest jurisdictional information from expanded data sources across the US, UK, Switzerland, Sweden, Spain, Portugal, Netherlands, Italy, Germany, France, Finland, and Australia.
Learn more
.
EDGAR Knowledge Source Enhancements
Search and analyze all SEC filing types with improved accuracy and greater flexibility with our revamped EDGAR Knowledge Source.
Learn more
.
Language Selection for Review Columns
Control language settings at the column level in review tables for more flexible, precise analysis across multilingual documents.
Learn more
.

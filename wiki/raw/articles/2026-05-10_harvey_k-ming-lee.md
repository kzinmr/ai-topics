At Harvey, K-Ming Lee leads customer engagement, building strategic relationships and programs that drive adoption and long-term loyalty among legal teams and law firms. An early leader at Harvey, he built the company’s core post-sales functions and works closely with customers to translate legal AI into real-world impact. He brings over a decade of experience in enterprise software, having led global customer teams at Templafy and played a key role in Dropbox’s shift from consumer to enterprise ahead of its IPO (DBX).

AI Summary by Glean
Glean now supports Gemini 1.5 Pro on Vertex AI and Claude 3 Sonnet on Bedrock, providing users with more choices for enterprise RAG capable models and cloud providers.
Glean's multi-LLM approach ensures simplicity, speed of implementation, and robust security, allowing data to remain within the customer's preferred cloud provider while following modern security principles.
The launch of Glean Apps, Actions, and Search/Chat APIs offers a comprehensive and secure generative AI platform for work, enabling users to consolidate data, compute, and billing in their chosen cloud provider.
With significant advances in Large Language Models (LLMs) over the past year, companies now have more choices than ever for enterprise RAG capable models and cloud providers where they can deploy these models. We want to ensure that users never have to worry whether the model they’re using is the right one for the job.
That’s why, alongside the launch of
Glean Apps, Actions
, and Search/Chat APIs, we’re very excited to announce today the availability of Gemini 1.5 Pro for customers on GCP and Claude 3 Sonnet for customers on AWS. This is an important step to ensuring that our customers have choice and can consolidate all of their data, compute, and billing in their preferred cloud provider.
Foundations for the future of workplace AI
Our multi-LLM approach ensures simplicity and speed of implementation while retaining product quality and customer security. That’s because all models work through Glean, which ensures security by design. Glean was built to follow modern security principles and beyond, including the zero trust security model, strong authentication practices, the principle of least privilege, and the shift-left approach to incorporating security into design, development, and operations.
Furthermore, users not only get their choice of model, but additional deployment options that enable data to remain within the customer’s cloud provider of choice. Regardless of what models emerge, and which you choose for work, results will always be grounded in Glean’s strict security framework and user-friendly options.

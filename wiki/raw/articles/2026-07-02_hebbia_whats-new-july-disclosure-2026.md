What's New: July Disclosure 2026
Last month, Hebbia got sharper where it matters most: deeper in your workflows, deeper in your data, and deeper in the tools you already use.
This month, we shipped new ways to build and share Skills across your organization, an MS Excel plug-in that brings Hebbia into where you already work, expanded data sources with a marketplace to discover more, and support for chart types common in finance.
Skills and Agents
Create, Edit, and Share Skills
Build, edit, and share your own skills and share them across the organization. Skills built on other platforms can also be imported into Hebbia so everything lives in one place.
Skills Support More File Types
Use PowerPoint, Word, and Excel files as reference files when building a Skill. Hebbia uses them to match the structure and format of your best work when generating new content.
Analysis in Chat
Fixed Income Pricing Data
Chat now supports fixed income pricing data. Ask about bond prices, spreads, and yield history for specific instruments and get answers grounded in the live data from Intercontinental Exchange.
New Charts: Dual y-axis, candlesticks, and debt maturity towers
Chat can now generate three new chart types: debt maturity towers showing when a company's obligations come due, candlestick charts for price movement, and dual y-axis charts to view price and volume together in a single view.
Financial Modeling Improvements
MS Excel Plug-In
You can now run a well-defined Hebbia workflow directly inside Excel, keeping your analysis in the spreadsheet where it belongs.
Excel Cell Citations
Excel files now include inline citations on individual cells, surfaced as Microsoft Office notes. Every figure is traceable back to its source so you never need to leave the spreadsheet.
Platform updates
Sources Dashboard
Browse Hebbia's full library of integrations, see what's available, and add access to data sources from one place.
In-App Product Announcements
Never lose track of launch announcements in the app. The “What’s New” tab in the app now.

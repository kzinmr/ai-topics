Why I joined Hebbia as an Account Executive
After years watching AI fall short for lawyers and consultants, Adi joined Hebbia because it was the first platform he’d seen that actually delivered on the promise of putting knowledge workers’ time back toward real work.
My arrival at Hebbia feels like a homecoming. After enduring the relentless consulting grind and late-night data dives, the ability for Hebbia's AI platform to simplify complexity immediately resonated with me. My decision to join Hebbia isn't just a career move—it's a leap into a world where work is smarter, not harder.
AI has the potential to unlock knowledge workers
After graduation, I became a consultant. I wanted to use data to transform organizations. I quickly realized how much of my job consisted of sifting through presentations, poring over lengthy documents, and wrangling data from massive spreadsheets. I wasn’t transforming companies - I was transforming unstructured data.
This is the reality for 3M+ US knowledge workers - putting good minds into rote tasks. Financial analysts, lawyers, and consultants routinely delve into and dissect large volumes of information as part of their daily routines. The problem is growing - by 2026, the world will be grappling with over 221 billion terabytes of information, with more than 90% of it being unstructured.
For over five years, I've been assisting lawyers and consultants integrate AI into their workflows. Progress has been made, but are we where we should be? No - incremental gains haven’t lived up to the promise of AI.
Hebbia doesn't promise, it delivers.
Hebbia is revolutionizing the way we work transforming how we interact with unstructured data.
It's not just another chatbot—it's a game-changer for knowledge workers

# Encyclopedia Evalica - Braintrust
**Source:** https://www.braintrust.dev/encyclopedia
**Announced by:** Ankur Goyal, May 4, 2026

The Encyclopedia Evalica is a comprehensive index of terms, methodologies, and technical concepts for building, testing, and monitoring production-grade AI applications. It bridges the gap between traditional software engineering and LLM development.

Core sections:
1. Evaluation & Scoring: Absolute Scoring, LLM-as-a-Judge, Pairwise Evaluation, Pass@k, Factuality, Groundedness, Toxicity
2. Datasets & Data Management: Golden Dataset, Adversarial Examples, Data Flywheel, Eval Leakage
3. Observability & Production Monitoring: Online/Offline Evaluation, Drift, TTFT, P50/P95/P99, SLI/SLO
4. Tracing & Instrumentation: Trace & Span, flush(), Provider Wrap, Tool Call
5. Deployment & Infrastructure: Gateway, Deployed Prompt, Hybrid Deployment
6. Human-in-the-Loop: Annotation, Rubric, Inter-annotator Agreement (IAA), User Feedback

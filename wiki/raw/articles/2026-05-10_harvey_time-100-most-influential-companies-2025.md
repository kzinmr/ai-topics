We are honored to be named one of the Time100 Most Influential Companies.
by
Winston Weinberg

Gabe Pereyra
•
Jun 26, 2025
When you start a company, you hope that people see its potential. When you scale a company, you hope people start to see its impact for what it is: a tiny dent in your long-term vision for what’s possible. Today, finding out that Harvey is one of the
Time100 Most Influential Companies
means more to us than we can articulate. Organizations in the category are changing industries, improving people’s lives, and transforming the way the world works, and we are honored to be in their company.
One of our
core values as a company
is Job’s Not Finished. We don’t believe we have even begun to scratch the surface of what’s possible in the legal and professional services space, and in democratizing access to justice. However, being recognized in this way by Time means the world to us and to our employees. Thank you.

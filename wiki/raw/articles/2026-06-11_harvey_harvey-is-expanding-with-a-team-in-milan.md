Harvey's expansion in Milan
by
Harvey Team
•
Jun 11, 2026
Our European expansion continues apace. We’re now hiring a dedicated team in Milan to work alongside Italy's legal community.
We’re also thrilled to announce that Italy’s largest law firm,
BonelliErede,
is adopting Harvey firmwide.
Milan is at the heart of Italy’s legal and financial systems and home to many of its top-tier law firms and in-house legal teams. With a team in Milan, we will be working alongside Italian lawyers and building Harvey around their needs.
We’re already working with many of Italy’s leading firms and in-house teams, including PedersoliGattai, Pavia e Ansaldo, ArbLit, LCA Studio Legale, Giliberti Triscornia e Associati, Delfino e Associati Willkie Farr & Gallagher LLP, Lexia, and DiaSorin, as well as our implementation partner Irnerius.
“Customer demand in Italy is surging,” said Jorge Bestard, Vice President of EMEA Sales at Harvey. “With a team in Milan, we can provide hands-on support to our Italian customers as they adopt Harvey across practice areas and rethink how legal work gets done."
The Milan team is part of Harvey's growing presence across Europe, which now includes offices in London, Madrid, Munich, and Paris.
To join our team in Milan and learn more about career opportunities with Harvey, visit our
careers page
.

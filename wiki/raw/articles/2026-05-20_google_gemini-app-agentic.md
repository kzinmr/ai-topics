# The Gemini App Becomes More Agentic

Google I/O 2026: Gemini evolving from reactive assistant to proactive 24/7 AI agent. 900M+ monthly users across 230 countries and 70+ languages.

## Key Announcements
- **Gemini 3.5 Flash** — New generation frontier intelligence + rapid action
- **Neural Expressive** — Complete visual redesign, fluid animations, haptic feedback
- **Gemini Omni** — Text/images/video → cinematic video generation
- **Daily Brief** — Personalized morning digest agent, organizes priorities
- **Gemini Spark** — 24/7 personal AI agent, cloud-based, Workspace integration
- **macOS App** — Desktop Gemini Spark + advanced voice features

## Gemini Spark Details
- Powered by Gemini 3.5 + Antigravity harness
- Deep Workspace integration (Gmail, Docs, Slides)
- Cloud-based: works even when laptop is closed
- Recurring tasks, teachable skills, complete workflows
- MCP connections: Canva, OpenTable, Instacart launching; more partners
- Beta for US Google AI Ultra subscribers starting late May 2026

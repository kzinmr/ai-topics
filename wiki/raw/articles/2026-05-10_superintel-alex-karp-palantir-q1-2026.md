## Analysis

Palantir's architecture thesis centers on being "decision-centric" rather than merely "data-centric." The company builds an ontological layer through which governments categorize, prioritize, and act. Karp's background in critical theory (Frankfurt School) — writing about how language legitimizes power — creates an interesting tension with building surveillance infrastructure.

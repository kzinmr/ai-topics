:Harvey:’s iManage Integration
Move faster and stay in flow by accessing iManage documents directly within Harvey, without compromising on security or compliance.
by
Harvey Team
•
Aug 11, 2025
Watch Video
The future of legal work isn’t just AI-powered. It’s knowledge-driven. That’s why we decided to
bring Harvey together with iManage
, the knowledge work platform used globally by over a million professionals at top law firms, corporate legal teams, and other leading institutions. Today, we're excited to launch the open beta of our direct integration with iManage, built to help legal teams effortlessly tap into their internal intelligence.
Unlock Proprietary Insights
Traditionally, accessing and utilizing documents from iManage in Harvey involved manual steps, such as downloading and re-uploading files between the two platforms, slowing down workflows and introducing unnecessary complexity. This practice isn’t just inefficient; it also introduces governance and security risks, potentially duplicating sensitive information or inadvertently bypassing organizational protocols.
“
One of the first questions we hear when discussing AI tools is 'Does it integrate with iManage?' Now we have a better answer. The integration was straightforward to set up and deploy. The two-way connection between platforms allows users to work directly with firm documents in Harvey, eliminating the time-consuming export-and-upload process. The response has been overwhelmingly positive—integrating Harvey with our main document repository has only accelerated Ropes' adoption of Harvey across the firm.
”
Winston Burt
Director of Legal Technology at Ropes & Gray
With the Harvey and iManage integration, users can now securely import documents directly from iManage into
Assistant
,
Workflows
, and
Vault
through an intuitive, embedded interface. The direct OAuth connection means no third-party middleware or manual credential management is required, ensuring data security and compliance with strict ethical walls and governance policies.
Beyond simply importing files, users can instantly export documents drafted in Harvey back into specific iManage matters, complete with full versioning, metadata, and audit trails. This not only prevents unnecessary data duplication but also enhances transparency and ease of collaboration within legal teams.
“
At Blank Rome, we are committed to providing our attorneys and business professionals with legal generative AI applications that drive efficiency and support the exceptional service we provide to our clients. Harvey’s seamless integration with iManage aligns with these efforts, reducing the friction of switching between platforms to draft and analyze firm documents. We are excited to expand our efforts to streamline workflows and enhance productivity.
”
Ashton Batchelor
Chief Innovation & Value Officer at Blank Rome
Built to meet lawyers where they are, this integration supports a range of legal workflows across practice areas. A litigator can pull case and client files from iManage into Harvey's Assistant to work directly with and within those documents. A corporate lawyer reviewing a purchase agreement can instantly access similar deal documents from their organization's iManage platform to check standard language and negotiate from a position of knowledge. And legal teams can upload contextual agreements from iManage to Vault to extract key terms across their entire portfolio.
Built With Industry Leaders
We've been testing this integration with Ropes & Gray, Blank Rome, and CMS during early access. Their feedback shaped the features we're releasing today and informed our roadmap for what's coming next.
“
By pairing Harvey with iManage, our Harvey users experience a truly seamless, matter-centric workflow. Lawyers can directly import matter information or legal knowledge from iManage into Harvey, and seamlessly export the generated document work product as a document back to the exact iManage workspace in a single click. This eliminates context-switching, time-consuming manual uploads, and prevents duplicate filing which complicates data governance. The result is a more intuitive user experience that drives even greater adoption of Harvey by our lawyers.
”
Bert Vries
Head of Innovation & IT of CMS Netherlands
We’re excited for our customers to experience how Harvey and iManage bridge proprietary knowledge with the power of Harvey’s AI, giving legal teams seamless access to their organization's valuable insights.

# What's New: October 2025

**Source**: [https://www.hebbia.com/blog/the-disclosure-october-2025](https://www.hebbia.com/blog/the-disclosure-october-2025)

Product
By Aabhas Sharma
11.04.25
What's New: October 2025
This month, we automated more essential tasks for Excel and PowerPoint, added European filings, and made our platform even more intuitive to use.
At Hebbia, we design AI around the way finance actually works. This month, we’ve automated more essential tasks for Excel and PowerPoint—like pulling comps and building company strip profiles—expanded our data integrations, and made it even more intuitive for customers to navigate the full functionality of Hebbia.
Dark Mode
Hebbia can now be used in Dark Mode. Switch to a high-contrast user interface that reduces glare and keeps focus sharp during long work sessions—perfect for late nights.
Mobile app
Hebbia is now mobile. You can tap into the power of Hebbia when you're away from your desk—at home, traveling, or between meetings. The app will be available to all customers in November.
Strip Profile Generation
Chat now detects when you are analyzing multiple companies and suggests creating Strip Profiles into fully formatted PowerPoint slides. This further automates presentation creation, so you can spend more time on analysis and insight generation.
Spreading Comps in Excel
Analyze a company with peer benchmarks across valuation, key operating metrics, and more, and export to Excel. Hebbia automatically populates fields from all public filings, producing ready-to-use comps for your financial models.
Agents for Draft
Last month
, we released Draft which allows you to build reports and presentations either from scratch or from an existing template. Draft outlines can now be saved as reusable Agents for repeatable deliverables, like IC memos, diligence templates, or board summaries.
Edit Chat Conversations
You can now edit previously sent messages in Chat to have more flexibility in refining prompts.
EU Filings Library
EU Filings are now in Hebbia. You now have direct access to European regulatory disclosures, so you can broaden your research coverage and conduct cross-market comparisons.
S&P Capital IQ in Matrix Columns
You can now connect S&P CapIQ as a data source within Matrix follow-up columns. With this information, you can layer broker estimates, valuation metrics, and public market data directly on top of your private documents.
Agents Page Redesign
Agents are now easier to browse, launch, and share across your organization, with clearer visibility into what each workflow does and who created the Agent.
Guided Flow for Agents and Matrix
Hebbia provides structured steps for running Agents to ensure you reach the right output every time—ideal for workflows that involve multiple steps or data sources.
When you start a new Matrix from scratch, Hebbia now walks you through step-by-step how to build it for a more intuitive setup process.

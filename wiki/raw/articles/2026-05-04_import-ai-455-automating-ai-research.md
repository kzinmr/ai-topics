# Import AI 455: AI Systems Building Themselves

**Author:** Jack Clark (Anthropic co-founder) | **Date:** May 4, 2026

## Executive Summary
Jack Clark posits a **60%+ probability** that no-human-involved AI R&D—where a system autonomously builds its own successor—will occur by the **end of 2028**. While 2026 is unlikely to see full automation, proof-of-concepts for end-to-end training of successor models are expected within 1–2 years.

## The Evidence: Automating the "Schlep" of Research

### 1. The Coding Singularity
- **SWE-Bench**: Claude 2 (2%, late 2023) → Claude Mythos Preview (93.9%, current)
- **METR Time Horizons**: GPT-3.5 (30s, 2022) → Opus 4.6 (~12 hours, 2026). 100-hour forecast by end 2026
- **CORE-Bench** (computational reproducibility): Declared "solved" (Opus 4.5, 95.5%)
- **MLE-Bench** (Kaggle): 16.9% (Oct 2024) → 64.4% (Feb 2026)

### 2. Core Science & ML Skills
- **Kernel Optimization**: Automated PyTorch→CUDA conversion, Triton kernel optimization
- **Training Optimization**: Claude Mythos Preview achieved 52x speedup on CPU-only training (vs 4x expected from human)
- **PostTrainBench**: Anthropic models outperforming all-hands human teams on post-training tasks by ~50%

### 3. Meta-Skills
- **Agent Orchestration**: Claude Code enables "manager" AI supervising multiple sub-agents
- **Automated Alignment**: Agents creating "scalable oversight" techniques beating human baselines

## Industry Trajectory
- **OpenAI**: "Automated AI research intern" target by Sept 2026
- **Anthropic**: Actively publishing on "Automated Alignment Researchers"
- **Startups**: Recursive Superintelligence ($500M raised), Mirendil

## Implications
- **Alignment Risk**: Compounding error — 99.9% alignment drops to 60.5% over 500 generations
- **Economic Shift**: "Amdahl's Law for the economy" — physical bottlenecks become primary constraints
- **Machine Economy**: Emergence of capital-heavy, human-light corporations

## Probabilistic Forecasts
- 30% automated AI R&D by end 2027
- 60% by end 2028
- Failure condition: if not by 2028, suggests fundamental paradigm deficiency

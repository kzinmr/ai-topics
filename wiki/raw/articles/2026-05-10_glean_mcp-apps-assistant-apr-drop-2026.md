AI Summary by Glean
Glean Assistant now supports MCP Apps
, so users can access and use tools like
Asana, Gamma, Hex, and Box
directly inside Assistant instead of switching across multiple apps.
Assistant has evolved from simple actions to embedded app experiences
, letting partner-built UIs like tickets, dashboards, and workflows appear right in the chat so users can review and act in one place.
The core value is faster execution with less context switching
, while still giving admins centralized control over which MCP tools and apps are available in Glean.
Work doesn't happen in just one app — it happens across dozens, and every context switch costs you. The teams pulling ahead aren't working harder; they're working with the right information at the right moment. Glean Assistant brings trusted data into your workflow, so decisions don't wait on digging, searching, or asking around. It's the agentic coworker that makes everyone capable of more.
Now, it’s easier than ever to get your work done all in one place. MCP Apps are now available in Glean Assistant, bringing the applications you already rely on directly into the Glean interface — so you can execute work across apps directly in Glean.
“Knowledge is only valuable when you can act on it. Gamma's integration with Glean through MCP Apps means teams can turn enterprise knowledge into polished presentations and documents in seconds, right where they're already working. We're excited to be a launch partner.”
-Grant Lee, Co-founder & CEO, Gamma
Evolving from actions to applications in Assistant
Glean Assistant already supports actions and remote MCP servers, letting admins connect tools like Asana, Notion, GitHub, Canva, and more as callable actions inside Assistant and Agents. MCP Apps take this one step further. Assistant can now embed rich, partner-authored UIs directly in Glean Assistant using the MCP Apps Extension standard.
We are starting by releasing MCP Apps in Glean Assistant for tools our customers already depend on every day, including work management tools like Asana (coming soon), content and file platforms like Box, visual creation tools like Gamma, and data tools like Hex, Mixpanel, and Clay (coming soon). The goal is simple: meet people where they already work, with the apps they already trust, while keeping a single, consistent Assistant experience.
For example:
Turn a conversation into an Asana task in seconds. Just say "create a task from this conversation, with me as owner and next Friday as the due date" — Assistant surfaces a widget to review and confirm every field before anything is created.
Pull up a live Hex dashboard and get AI-powered analysis in the same breath. Ask "show me the Q1 product adoption dashboard and summarize what changed after our last release" — you get the data and the story, together.
Many of our customers are already leveraging MCP apps in Glean to consolidate context across disparate workflows and applications, powering everything from better decision-making to project delivery to customer service.
"We're excited to expand Hex's footprint as a native MCP app in Glean! By layering analytical depth onto the knowledge users already rely on, teams unlock richer, more valuable insights grounded in the trust and observability that Hex's
Context Studio
provides."
- Armin Efendic, Partner Engineering Lead, Hex
"The teams winning right now are the ones where trusted product data meets them in the flow of work — surfacing the right context, at the right moment, so decisions move at the speed of thought. That's what Mixpanel and Glean are building toward together."
- Anant Gupta, CTO, Mixpanel
Getting started
Support for MCP Apps in Glean Assistant is available for Mixpanel, Hex, Gamma, and Box. Support for Asana and Clay is coming soon.
As we expand partner coverage and UI capabilities, Glean Assistant will continue to become a more flexible surface for the apps you already rely on — so you can stay in one place, keep your context, and still get things done with the tools you love.
If you want to learn more about how to better consolidate context and workflows by bringing your favorite tools into Glean, reach out to our team for a

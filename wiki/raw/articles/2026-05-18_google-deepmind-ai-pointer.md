# Google DeepMind: Reimagining the Mouse Pointer for the AI Era

**Source:** Google DeepMind Blog  
**URL:** https://deepmind.google/blog/ai-pointer/  
**Date:** May 12, 2026  
**Authors:** Adrien Baranes, Rob Marchant

## Summary
Google DeepMind is developing AI-powered capabilities for the mouse pointer, powered by Gemini, to understand not just what the user is pointing at but why it matters to them.

## Four Principles
1. **Maintain the flow** — AI works across all apps, no "AI detours"
2. **Show and tell** — Visual and semantic context captured automatically
3. **Embrace "This" and "That"** — Natural shorthand with gestures
4. **Turn pixels into actionable entities** — Point at a photo, get an interactive to-do list

## Product Integration
- Gemini in Chrome: point to ask about any part of a webpage (available May 12, 2026)
- Magic Pointer in Googlebook: coming soon
- Google AI Studio: experimental demos
- Google Labs' Disco: further testing

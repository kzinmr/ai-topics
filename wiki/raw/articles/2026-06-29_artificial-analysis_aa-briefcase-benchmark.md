# AA-Briefcase: Agentic Knowledge Work Benchmark

**Source**: Artificial Analysis
**URL**: https://artificialanalysis.ai/evaluations/aa-briefcase

AA-Briefcase is a private evaluation developed by Artificial Analysis for frontier agentic capability in long-horizon knowledge work, testing agents on realistic business workflows.

## Structure
- **4 multi-week knowledge work scenarios**: Data Science, Product Management, Banking Operations, Heavy Industry Strategy
- **91 tasks total**, thousands of input files
- Tasks graded across 3 dimensions: Rubric (binary pass/fail), Analytical Quality (pairwise), Presentation (pairwise)

## Grading
1. **Rubric checks**: Did the model follow instructions, identify requirements, use correct evidence?
2. **Analytical Quality**: Pairwise comparison — which submission is more thorough?
3. **Presentation**: Pairwise comparison — which is more professionally presented?

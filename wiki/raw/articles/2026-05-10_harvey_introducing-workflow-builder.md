Introducing Agent Builder: Previously Known as Workflow Builder
Design and deploy customizable workflows designed to capture your firm’s differentiators and deliver high-quality legal work.
by
Harvey Team
•
Jun 24, 2025
Editor’s note:
Agent Builder was previously called Workflow Builder. This post has been updated to reflect the new name.
Watch Video
The future of legal transformation isn't one-size-fits-all. It’s custom, expert-led, and built by you. That’s why we’re excited to announce our Agent Builder, previously known as Workflow Builder, is available to all Harvey customers today.
Agent Builder lets legal teams leverage Harvey’s domain-specific AI to transform their proprietary knowledge into structured, reusable systems. Instead of adapting generic tools to fit your practice, you can now build workflows that distill what makes your organization unique.
We are proud to be launching Agent Builder in collaboration with some of the most forward-thinking legal teams globally. Built in partnership with
Ropes & Gray, dentsu, King & Wood Mallesons, Setterwalls Advokatbyrå, and Ashurst,
Agent Builder is already powering high-impact workflows customized to each organization’s specialties.
“
When Harvey introduced custom workflows, our first thought was: 'When can we start building these?' Harvey included us in early development conversations and the result is what we envisioned — a platform that lets us create workflows tailored to our client needs. This will help us scale our expertise and improve client outcomes, showing just how valuable strategic technology partnerships can be in transforming global legal service delivery.
”
Marsha Stein
Chief Information Officer, Ropes & Gray
What Agent Builder Does
At Harvey, we have invested deeply in building the best AI for legal work. In building a legal Workflow agent, we realized the most important part was the unique experience of lawyers and firms and the particular ways that they drive value for their clients every day. Harnessing this requires not just exceptional Workflow agents, but a platform to customize those agents to a firm’s problems and deploy them at scale.
Agent Builder is a self-serve tool that empowers Innovation teams to build these from scratch. With it, Innovation and Knowledge leaders can design and deploy custom agents that reflect their firm’s expertise, tone, and processes.
That could mean agents that analyze and triage documents the same ways that a firm’s lawyers do, or rely on its precedent to draft new documents. It could also mean combining many of these functionalities to generate end-to-end solutions to multi-part problems.
From simple tasks to complex processes, Agent Builder turns best practices into one-click, guided experiences that deliver high-quality work output.

At dentsu, we’re using AI to augment our human expertise by designing smarter systems. Workflow Builder gives us a new way to orchestrate how AI supports legal and operational workflows, helping our teams scale impact while reducing complexity. It’s another step in our broader strategy to embed data and technology in every part of our network.
”
Robert Clark
General Counsel, dentsu Technology, Privacy, & IP

How to Build Your First Agent
Agent Builder makes it easy to turn your knowledge into scalable systems. Here’s a walkthrough of an example workflow:
Watch Video
To summarize, the basic steps are:
Describe or design your workflow
Start by telling Harvey what you want to build in plain language, or use the no-code interface to lay out steps manually by connecting different blocks.
Customize with your firm’s know-how
Add the inputs, precedent examples, and branching logic that reflect how your firm works. Choose the best model for each step, or let Harvey optimize automatically.
Deploy, share, and refine
Test the workflow, set permissions, and share it across your team. You can iterate, version, and adapt workflows as your needs evolve.
The agents you build are published as
Workflow Agents
, available to your team directly inside Harvey.

What’s Next
The launch of Agent Builder represents a fundamental turning point. Our
vision for AI agents
has always been to create systems that collaborate with professionals on their most complex work, and with Agent Builder, we’re bringing lawyer-agent collaboration to the next level of scale.
This marks the transition from an era where legal teams simply consume AI to one where they architect it. It’s the foundation for a new wave of legal work: one built on your expertise, your standards, and your systems. As these custom agents become more sophisticated, they'll form the bedrock of deeply AI-enabled legal organizations.

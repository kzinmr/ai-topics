Legal Agents for Every Matter, Tailored to You
We’re introducing improved agentic capabilities in Harvey, so that you can quickly drive consistent, high-quality results.
by
Harvey Team
•
May 5, 2026
Today, we're introducing a major upgrade to Harvey's agentic platform: a library of 500+ ready-to-use agents built and tested by lawyers, and an improved
Agent Builder
for teams that want to customize agents to reflect their organization’s unique expertise. These upgrades can handle longer processes and produce Word, Powerpoint, and Excel files, helping lawyers move from intake to client-ready deliverables in a single click.
These capabilities were built alongside design partners: leading firms who worked directly with us to shape what this next generation of agents needed to do and the quality bar they had to clear.
Gowling WLG
Willkie
Clayton Utz
Mallesons
Hengeler Mueller
Agents Across Key Practice Areas, Built by Lawyers
Every agent in Harvey's library is purpose-built for a specific legal task, covering global practice groups from M&A and funds to
in-house
procurement, labor and employment, IP, white collar investigations, and more. Each one was developed and pressure-tested by our internal team of lawyers against the same tasks and nuances legal professionals face in practice.
A few examples of what they can do today:
Extracting key requirements from an MSA for obligation tracking:
In-house teams can quickly review vendor agreements, summarize key terms, and surface any upcoming deadlines or actions, so that they can take the appropriate next steps.
Comparing side letter provisions against an LP agreement:
Funds teams can review dozens of side letters in one step, making it much easier to identify any deviations in terms and draft a summary in a new memorandum.
Comparing employee statements against documentary evidence:
Lawyers can progress an internal investigation by cross-checking employee testimonies, asking the agent to flag inconsistencies, create a credibility table, and recommend follow-up steps.
Tailor any agent to reflect your expertise
With the latest Agent Builder update, you can take any agent from the library and tailor it in plain english by adjusting scope, refining steps, and adding your organization's specific context, precedents, and expertise.
If you want to build something from scratch, you can describe what you need and ask Harvey to build it for you.
All together, it’s an easier way to create custom agents, so you can ensure work that scales still reflects your unique knowledge.
Delegate the work. Own the judgment.
When routine work is handled, lawyers can focus on the strategic guidance that actually drives better outcomes. Harvey's agent library and Agent Builder are built to clear the volume quickly and with confidence.
The agents handle the process. You own the judgment.

We are excited to expand our Microsoft partnership by joining the Azure Marketplace, following our
recent announcement
supporting Azure regions on Pinecone. If you’re already on Azure or leveraging Azure’s OpenAI Service, you can now quickly add Pinecone to your AI stack with just a few clicks.
The Azure Marketplace provides an extensive catalog of software solutions certified and optimized for Azure. If you’re an Azure customer, this means:
Fast, simple procurement:
Skip the pre-approvals, streamline the procurement process, and get Pinecone to production faster.
Consolidated spending and billing:
Purchase Pinecone through the marketplace and meet your Microsoft Azure Consumption Commitment (MACC) faster. View your usage across Azure in a single place.
Pinecone on Azure Marketplace is currently available for Standard and Enterprise plans. We will add support for customers with annual commitments in the coming months. Read our
setup guide

A URL that matches an ignore pattern is never recommended in
          this console, even if it appears in the recommendation lists
          of this console or other remote consoles.
Ignore patterns are normalised before matching.  The URL
          scheme, query parameters and fragments are ignored, matching
          is case-insensitive and the patterns act like prefixes.  A
          pattern such as https://example.com/foo/ matches both HTTP
          and HTTPS URLs for the same site, as well as all pages under
          that path.

Hello!  You are currently on a Wander console!  A Wander
        console lets you browse random websites and pages from the
        Wander community.  The Wander community consists of
        individuals who develop and maintain their own personal
        websites.
To set up your own Wander console, download
this
        ZIP file
, extract index.html and wander.js and place them
        in the /wander/ directory of your website.  Then edit
        wander.js by following the directions at
codeberg.org/susam/wander
.
That's it!  Once your /wander/ directory is ready on your web
        server, you can share a link to your Wander console in
this community thread
.  Hopefully, someone
        will add your console to theirs and you will become part of
        the Wander network.
For more information about Wander, please see
codeberg.org/susam/wander
.

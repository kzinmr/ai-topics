:Harvey: Reaches 100 Global Legal Knowledge Sources
Harvey has reached 100 integrated national legal and regulatory data sources, expanding global legal knowledge accessibility for legal teams worldwide.
by
Harvey Team
•
Sep 8, 2025
Legal research often requires juggling multiple databases and platforms. At Harvey, we're building a unified solution where lawyers can access, search, and cite legal knowledge from around the world without switching tools or losing context.
With over half of our customers based outside of the US, and Harvey office presence expanding in
Mexico
,
Canada
,
India
,
Germany
, and
Australia
, we're accelerating toward broader global coverage. In support of this, Harvey has now reached
100 integrated national legal and regulatory data sources globally
.
These sources span legal systems across six continents, covering Asia, Europe, Latin and South America, the Middle East and Africa, and North America, plus supranational sources like EU law and UN Security Council resolutions. These sources include legislation, case law, court decisions, regulatory materials, and/or government filings from diverse jurisdictions.
Serving our Global Clients
Knowledge Sources are now organized into Regional Bundles. Customers can select the regions they need and gain access to all Knowledge Sources in those bundles. This approach, combined with content from strategic partnerships like
Lexis Nexis
, provides customers with extensive data to answer complex legal questions across jurisdictions.
Here’s to the next 100 — and beyond.
The most up-to-date information on knowledge sources, including what’s available and how to use them, can be found in our
Help Center
.

Contact our team
to see how 100+ integrated sources can transform your legal practice.

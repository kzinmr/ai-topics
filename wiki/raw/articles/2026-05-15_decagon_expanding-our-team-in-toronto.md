We’re excited to share that Decagon is opening a new office in Toronto, growing our team in one of North America’s leading hubs for finance and technology.
Toronto brings together the qualities we look for in a growth hub: world-class tech talent, a vibrant AI ecosystem, and proximity to major enterprise markets across North America. This office gives us a local base to hire exceptional talent, partner more closely with customers across Canada, and continue building our global footprint.
We’re proud to already partner with Canadian companies like Wealthsimple, one of the country’s most innovative financial services brands. Wealthsimple uses Decagon to scale high-quality customer interactions while keeping technical teams focused on core business priorities. With Decagon, they can update agent behavior in natural language, iterate quickly with Duet, and deliver customer experiences that stay on-brand and on-policy as they grow.
We’re hiring across sales, Agent Product, and technical teams. If you’re excited about joining a team of ambitious builders at the frontier of enterprise AI, explore open roles at
decagon.ai/careers

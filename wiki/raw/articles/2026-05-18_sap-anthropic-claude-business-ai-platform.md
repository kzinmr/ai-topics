# SAP and Anthropic: Claude on SAP Business AI Platform

**Source:** SAP News Center (SAP Sapphire 2026)  
**URL:** https://news.sap.com/2026/05/sap-anthropic-to-bring-claude-sap-business-ai-platform/  
**Date:** May 12, 2026

## Summary
SAP and Anthropic announced plans to embed Claude as a primary reasoning and agentic capability across SAP's AI-enabled portfolio, powering Joule and Joule agents through the newly announced SAP Business AI Platform.

## Key Points
- Claude connects to SAP Business AI Platform to coordinate tasks across S/4HANA, SuccessFactors, Ariba via MCP
- Agents can close books, answer HR questions, reroute supplier orders
- Industry focus: public sector, healthcare, education, life sciences, utilities
- Christian Klein (SAP CEO) and Daniela Amodei (Anthropic president) endorsed
- Trust and governance: AI operates within same controls as human decisions
- Part of SAP's Autonomous Enterprise vision at SAP Sapphire 2026

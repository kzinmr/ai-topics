# Think 2026: IBM Delivers the Blueprint for the AI Operating Model as the AI Divide Widens

**Source**: IBM Newsroom, May 5, 2026
**URL**: https://newsroom.ibm.com/2026-05-05-think-2026-ibm-delivers-the-blueprint-for-the-ai-operating-model-as-the-ai-divide-widens

At Think 2026, IBM announced its most comprehensive expansion of enterprise AI and hybrid cloud management capabilities.

**Key products announced:**
- **IBM watsonx Orchestrate** (next-gen, private preview): Evolves into agentic control plane for multi-agent era. Deploy agents from any source with consistent policy enforcement and accountability.
- **IBM Confluent**: Real-time data streaming for AI (Kafka/Flink)
- **IBM Concert platform**: Intelligent operations
- **IBM Sovereign Core**: Operational independence

## The Four-System AI Operating Model

Arvind Krishna (CEO): "Running AI in the enterprise requires a new operating model."

Four integrated systems:
1. **Agents**: Coordinated AI that executes and adapts across the business
2. **Data**: Real-time, connected information giving shared view
3. **Automation**: End-to-end infrastructure with workflows scaling across processes
4. **Hybrid**: Operational independence for sovereignty, governance, security

## Agents: Orchestration at Scale

As organizations move from handfuls of agents to thousands, built by different teams on different platforms, the core challenge shifts from building agents to governing and auditing them in near real-time.

**IBM Bob** (GA): Agentic development partner for enterprise, security and cost controls built in.

## Data: AI-Ready Foundation

Confluent acquisition for real-time data streaming. **Context in watsonx.data** (private preview): federated context layer for explainable decisions. New capabilities: context, OpenRAG, OpenSearch on watsonx.data, Confluent's Real-Time Context Engine.

**GPU-accelerated Presto** (with NVIDIA): 83% cost savings, 30x price-performance improvement in Nestlé proof of concept.

## Automation and Hybrid

**IBM Concert**: Intelligent operations platform. **IBM Sovereign Core**: Operational independence for regulated environments.

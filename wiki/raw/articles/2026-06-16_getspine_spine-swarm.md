# Spine Swarm

## What It Is
Spine is an agentic platform that runs parallel AI agents across 300+ models to conduct deep research, create client-ready deliverables, and automate workflows across enterprise applications. Spine Swarm is the collaborative multi-agent architecture at its core — dispatching a single brief triggers multiple agents that each handle a separate workstream in parallel, passing structured output between workstreams to produce complete deliverables.

## Who Made It
Spine (spine.ai) — a company building AI-powered research and document automation tools for professional workflows.

## Key Technical Details
- **Parallel agent swarms**: Multiple AI agents work simultaneously across different workstreams rather than sequentially in a single chat thread. Agents pass structured output to one another to compose final deliverables.
- **300+ model support**: Orchestrates across a vast range of AI models for redundancy and capability diversity.
- **Benchmarked #1 for accuracy**: Spine Deep Research scores **87.6%** on Google DeepMind's DeepSearchQA — the hardest public multi-step research benchmark. This surpasses:
  - Perplexity Deep Research: 79.5%
  - Claude: 76.1%
  - ChatGPT: 71.3%
  - Gemini Deep Research: 66.1%
- **Client-ready output formats**: Produces polished deliverables, not rough drafts:
  - Goldman Sachs-style reports
  - PowerPoint (.pptx) strategy decks
  - Word (.docx) briefs
  - Excel models with formulas and scenario tabs
  - Competitive benchmarks and financial projections
  - Interactive dashboards
- **Automation integrations**: Schedule recurring workflows (daily, weekly, monthly, or custom) and connect to tools via integrations, MCP, API, and webhooks.
- **Structured multi-agent handoffs**: Agents pass structured data between workstreams rather than unstructured chat context.

## Why It Matters
Spine Swarm represents a fundamental architectural shift in how AI research is conducted. Rather than relying on a single agent working sequentially (the dominant paradigm of current LLM interfaces), Spine uses parallel multi-agent coordination with structured handoffs. The benchmark results on DeepSearchQA demonstrate that this approach produces measurably higher accuracy than any single-agent solution from major providers. For professional services — consulting, finance, legal, medical research — the ability to produce client-ready deliverables in real formats (not just chat text) dramatically reduces the time-to-value from AI research tools. The automation capabilities (scheduled workflows, MCP/API integration) make Spine a platform for continuous intelligence gathering rather than ad-hoc research.

## Key Results

- Boosted RLM (GPT-5.2) performance from 38.7% → 65.6% on LongCoT-mini
- No training required — pure inference-time optimization
- Example of the "mismanaged geniuses hypothesis" (MGH)

## Mismanaged Geniuses Hypothesis
The MGH posits that current LLMs are underperforming their capabilities not due to architectural limits, but due to suboptimal prompting, routing, and context management. GEPA (Genetic-Pareto Prompt Evolution) demonstrates this by extracting significantly more performance from existing models through reflective prompt optimization alone.

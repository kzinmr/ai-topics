## Summary

Matías Kladov presents a test case minimization technique that can reduce a test suite to just 256 lines while preserving its bug-finding capability. This is related to the concept of test case minimization — finding the minimal subset of tests needed to detect regressions.

- Relates to broader concepts in software engineering about minimizing cognitive load and development friction

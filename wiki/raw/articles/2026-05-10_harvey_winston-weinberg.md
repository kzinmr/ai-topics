at

Winston Weinberg is the CEO and co-founder of Harvey. Prior to founding the company in 2022, he worked as an associate at O’Melveny & Myers LLP, specializing in securities and antitrust litigation. Winston holds a Bachelor of Arts from Kenyon College and a Juris Doctor from the University of Southern California Gould School of Law.

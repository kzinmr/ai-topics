# LLM cliché highlighter

Source: https://simonwillison.net/2026/Jul/17/llm-cliche-highlighter/#atom-everything

I got frustrated reading
yet another
article that was crammed with the clichés of LLM-generated writing - "no fluff, no filler, no jargon" type stuff - so I had Fable 5 vibe code up this app for highlighting ten common patterns that show up in that sort of writing.

:Harvey:’s Outlook Add-In
Harvey is launching an Outlook Add-In to bring AI-powered drafting and summarization directly into your inbox.
by
Harvey Team
•
Aug 6, 2025
At Harvey, one of our core product goals is simple but powerful: meet lawyers where they are. We want to make AI not just accessible but indispensable wherever legal work happens.
Our
Word Add-In
already integrates seamlessly into the drafting, editing, and contract review flows legal teams use every day. Now, we’re bringing that same philosophy to another foundational tool in the legal tech stack: Outlook.
Watch Video
Outlook is more than just an email client. It’s where conversations start, where information flows in and out of organizations, and where decisions take shape. By bringing Harvey into Outlook, we’re unlocking AI-powered support for drafting, summarization, and day-to-day questions right in your inbox.
No more jumping between tools. No more context switching. Just smarter work, right where you're already working.
This is part of our broader vision. Harvey isn’t just a standalone product: it’s a connective layer across your legal tech stack. Combined with our integrations with legal research platforms
like Lexis Nexis
and document management systems
like iManage
, we’re building a system of engagement for modern legal teams, helping them eliminate drudgery and scale their strategic impact.
Our goal with the Outlook Add-In is to help you take control of your inbox, stay in flow, and unlock greater leverage in your legal work.
Harvey’s Outlook Add-In will be available early Q4 with beta customers getting access in the coming weeks. Stay tuned!

# IBM Granite 4.1: Enterprise Language Models

IBM's most expansive model release covering language, vision, speech, embedding, and Guardian models.

Language models: 3B, 8B, 30B dense decoder-only transformers with GQA, RoPE, SwiGLU, RMSNorm, shared input/output embeddings.

Training: ~15T tokens across 5 phases — General pre-training (10T), Math/Code (2T), High-quality data annealing (2T), Refinement (0.5T), Long-context extension to 512K tokens. SFT on ~4.1M curated samples. Multi-stage RL with on-policy GRPO + DAPO loss.

Key result: Granite 4.1 8B instruct matches or outperforms Granite 4.0 32B-A9B MoE despite simpler dense architecture.

Optimized for enterprise: instruction following, tool calling, predictable latency, stable token usage. FP8 quantized variants available for vLLM. Trained on NVIDIA GB200 NVL72 cluster hosted in CoreWeave.

Released under Apache 2.0. Available on HuggingFace, Ollama, OpenRouter, Replicate, watsonx, AnythingLLM, LM Studio, Unsloth, W&B.

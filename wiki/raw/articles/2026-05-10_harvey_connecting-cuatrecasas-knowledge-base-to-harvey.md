Connecting Cuatrecasas’ Knowledge Base to :Harvey:
In this blog post we detail how we enabled Cuatrecasas to securely and privately leverage their firm’s internal data within Harvey via a model exclusive to Cuatrecasas.
by
Francesc Muñoz
and
Gabe Pereyra
•
Oct 21, 2024
In an innovative move to enhance efficiency and productivity, Cuatrecasas has successfully integrated their extensive knowledge base with Harvey, a cutting-edge Generative AI solution tailored specifically for law firms. This integration allows Cuatrecasas to securely and privately leverage their internal data, fostering a seamless interface between technology and legal expertise.
Customized Integration for Enhanced Utility
The bespoke model developed for Cuatrecasas within Harvey's framework is a testament to the firm's commitment to maintaining the highest levels of security and privacy while embracing technological advancements. Francesc Muñoz, Cuatrecasas CIO, explains “This model is exclusive to Cuatrecasas, enabling lawyers to easily search and query their firm’s internal data and generate work products that reflect Cuatrecasas’ unique style. The integration also includes a streamlined process for importing data from document management systems, ensuring secure data storage and accessibility.”
Data Security and Privacy Measures
At Harvey, data security and privacy are paramount. The solution designed for Cuatrecasas ensures that their data is isolated and never used for the benefit of other clients. This involves a logically separated datastore accessible only by Cuatrecasas users, and a model trained to keep data private. These robust measures allow Cuatrecasas and its clients to leverage their data, previously anonymized and impersonated, securely and exclusively.
Utilizing the Knowledge Base for Bespoke Solutions
The integration of Cuatrecasas’ knowledge base with Harvey underscores the firm's ability to solve complex legal issues with bespoke solutions. Large law firms like Cuatrecasas handle unique problems that require tailored approaches developed over years of aggregating elite legal talent. The proprietary and personalized data at Cuatrecasas is invaluable for generating models capable of addressing specific legal inquiries and tasks in the firm’s distinctive manner.
For instance, with access to Cuatrecasas’ data, Harvey can generate personalized responses to queries such as drafting standard language for obtaining a special power of attorney for purchasing shares, creating resignation letters using default templates, and formulating standard questions for due diligence processes.
Conclusion
Since its deployment, the daily usage of Harvey at Cuatrecasas has rocketed, reflecting the significant value of integrating a firm’s knowledge base within a generative AI solution. This collaboration not only streamlines workflows but also ensures that the bespoke solutions synonymous with Cuatrecasas' reputation are maintained and enhanced through technological innovation.
By combining Cuatrecasas’ rich repository of legal knowledge with Harvey’s AI capabilities, the firm continues to set new standards in the legal industry, demonstrating the powerful synergy between human expertise and advanced technology.

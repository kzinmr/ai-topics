What is SOC 2 Type 2 compliance?
SOC 2 Type 2 accreditations follow the compliance framework outlined by the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA). The framework includes Trust Services Criteria (TSC) for security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy.
An attestation report describes the company’s systems as they relate to the criteria, indicates whether or not controls are suitably designed, and confirms operational effectiveness.
Compared to a SOC 2 Type 1 report, which is a description of a company’s controls at a moment in time, the SOC 2 Type 2 process is more rigorous, representing ongoing adherence over a duration of months.

Fyxer
is an AI email assistant for professionals who want to spend less time in their inbox. It organizes emails by priority, creates email drafts in a user's tone of voice, and writes meeting notes for teams working across platforms like Gmail, Outlook and Zoom.
To improve the precision, speed, and structure of its
meeting notetaker
, Fyxer integrated ElevenLabs
Scribe v2
for live transcription of user conversations. In addition to the transcript, Fyxer's notetaker also extracts action items and summaries from each meeting, which means it needs to carefully attribute each statement to the correct speaker.
Top-performing transcription accuracy
Before committing to a provider, Fyxer built a benchmark dataset from its own meeting audio and ran ten transcription providers against it. Each provider was scored on speaker-attributed word error rate, the metric that matters most to Fyxer because its output depends on knowing who said what. ElevenLabs ranked highest in the field with a 20% reduction in WER relative to the control.
We tested every major provider and ElevenLabs won on the metric that matters most to us. But beyond the numbers, this feels like a real partnership. We're excited to be building with a team that's moving as fast as we are.
– Archie Hollingsworth, Co-founder, Fyxer
Driving impact with 15% relative lift in user conversion
Fyxer validated the model in production with an A/B test across 6,000+ organizations. The organizations using ElevenLabs converted from trial to paid subscriptions at 20.3%, compared with 17.6% on the previous provider, representing a 15% relative lift.
With overall note-taking quality and engagement guardrails staying flat throughout the test, Fyxer has now rolled out ElevenLabs as its exclusive transcription provider.
Helping teams focus on results
Every meeting that Fyxer captures gives time back to its users and a record they can trust. Teams no longer have to chase down what was said in a meeting, correct misattributed quotes, or rebuild key takeaways after the fact.
Accurate transcription lays the foundation for these results, where small gains in performance compound through every meeting summary and action item. Teams building with transcription can get started with ElevenLabs Speech to Text through
ElevenAPI
.

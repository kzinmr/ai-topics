# Doc-to-LoRA and Text-to-LoRA: Instant LLM Updates with Hypernetworks

**Source:** Sakana AI Labs

## Summary
Two related research exploring how to make LLM customization faster and more accessible by training a Hypernetwork to generate LoRA adapters on the fly.

## Doc-to-LoRA
- Enables knowledge updates by turning documents into LoRA adapters
- Allows a model to internalize new factual content without retraining
- Uses auxiliary modulator networks (hypernetworks) to modify LLM weights on the fly

## Text-to-LoRA
- Creates LoRA adapters for task-specific behaviors from text descriptions
- Compresses documents and masters new skills through hypernetwork-generated adapters
- Makes models instantly adaptable without full fine-tuning

## Key Innovation
- Long-term memory and continual adaptation are key challenges of current agentic systems
- Hypernetwork approach allows models to adapt on-the-fly
- No full retraining required for new knowledge or skills

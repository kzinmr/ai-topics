GleanによるAIサマリー
Generative AI tools are transforming businesses by automating routine tasks, providing valuable insights, and enabling better decision-making across various functions such as customer service, development, HR, and finance.
The top 9 generative AI tools for 2024 include Glean AI for enterprise productivity, GitHub Copilot for developer productivity, Superhuman for email management, Stampli for AI-driven accounts payable automation, Gong AI for sales coaching, ChurnZero AI for customer success, Helpshift for AI customer support, Runway AI for creative teams, and Paradox AI for HR automation.
These tools enhance efficiency, creativity, and personalization, offering features like tailored enterprise knowledge, smart code suggestions, automated invoice processing, and AI-powered chatbots, ultimately driving productivity and competitive advantage in the digital world.
Generative AI tools have emerged as powerful solutions that can drive efficiency across various functions—whether it's customer service, development, HR, or finance. These tools not only automate routine tasks but also provide valuable insights and enable better decision-making. This article explores the top 9 generative AI tools that are transforming businesses today.
9 best enterprise generative AI tools
1. Glean AI for enterprise productivity
2. GitHub Copilot for developer productivity
3. Superhuman for email management
4. Stampli for AI-driven accounts payable automation
5. Gong AI for sales coaching and revenue acceleration
6. ChurnZero AI for customer success
7. Helpshift for AI customer support assistance
8. Runway AI for creative teams
9. Paradox AI for HR automation
<h2 id="glean">1. Glean AI for enterprise productivity</h2>
Glean is an enterprise-ready digital workplace assistant that harnesses next-gen AI to provide precise answers and insights based on a company’s internal data, all while maintaining robust security and governance protocols.
Key features:
Tailored enterprise knowledge:
Glean understands your company’s unique context and relationships, delivering highly relevant, customized information.
Connectivity across 100+ business apps:
With connectors for 100+ apps, Glean offers expansive search coverage and APIs to integrate additional enterprise applications.
Generative AI-powered summaries:
Glean generates concise document summaries, saving users time by providing the most relevant information quickly.
Personalized search:
Using a knowledge graph, Glean delivers personalized results tailored to each user’s needs.
Real-time indexed results:
Search results are continuously updated, and access is permission-based to ensure users see only authorized data.
Generative AI for workflows:
Whether in engineering, support, or sales, Glean automates tasks and answers complex queries for increased productivity.

<h2 id="github">2. GitHub Copilot for developer productivity</h2>
GitHub Copilot is a revolutionary AI assistant for developers, powered by OpenAI's Codex. It integrates with popular code editors and provides intelligent code suggestions, documentation help, and even automated code completion.
Key features:
Smart code suggestions:
It provides real-time code snippets and suggestions, streamlining coding efforts.
Integration with major code editors:
Copilot seamlessly works with editors like Visual Studio Code, enhancing the coding speed.
AI-Driven code generation:
Developers can rely on Copilot to write boilerplate code and tackle complex programming tasks.
<h2 id="superhuman">3. Superhuman for email management</h2>
Superhuman is an AI-powered email client designed to improve email management by offering a faster and more organized experience.
Key features:
Smart email categorization:
Uses AI to prioritize important emails, helping users focus on what matters most.
Keyboard shortcuts:
Quick navigation through an inbox with powerful shortcuts.
Insights & analytics:
Track open rates, response times, and more, offering insights into email habits for optimized communication.
<h2 id="stampli">4. Stampli for AI-driven accounts payable automation</h2>
Stampli uses AI to automate invoice processing, reducing manual work and increasing the efficiency of financial operations.
Key features:
Automated invoice processing:
Stampli captures and processes invoices automatically, minimizing the need for manual data entry.
Customizable approval workflows:
Set up workflows that align with internal policies for seamless approval processes.
ERP integration:
Stampli integrates with major ERP systems, enabling smooth data flow between finance tools.
<h2 id="gong">5. Gong AI for sales coaching and revenue acceleration</h2>
Gong leverages AI to analyze sales interactions, providing data-driven insights to improve sales performance and drive revenue growth.
Key features:
Automated call analysis:
Gong uses AI to break down sales calls, delivering insights on customer interactions.
Coaching tools:
Provides targeted coaching based on AI-driven analysis of sales calls.
Revenue insights:
Gong identifies growth opportunities and tracks performance metrics to enhance sales strategies.
<h2 id="churnzero">6. ChurnZero AI for customer success</h2>
ChurnZero helps companies prevent customer churn by offering AI-powered insights and automating customer success workflows.
Key features:
Customer health scores:
AI-powered health scores identify at-risk customers, allowing teams to intervene proactively.
Automated workflows:
Automates communication workflows, ensuring timely and personalized follow-ups.
CRM integration:
Works seamlessly with CRM systems, providing real-time customer data.
<h2 id="helpshift">7. Helpshift for AI customer support assistance</h2>
Helpshift provides AI-driven automation for customer support, improving response times and reducing manual tasks.
Key features:
AI-powered chatbots:
Handles common customer inquiries automatically, freeing up human agents for more complex tasks.
Ticket routing:
Automatically assigns support tickets to the correct agents, ensuring efficient problem resolution.
Analytics:
Offers insights into customer support performance, helping teams optimize their support processes.
<h2 id="runway">8. Runway AI for creative teams</h2>
Runway AI is a creative assistant designed for teams working on video editing, graphic design, and more, with AI tools that automate many of these processes.
Key features:
AI-powered video editing:
Automatically edits videos, adds effects, and generates animations.
Design automation:
Simplifies the creation of graphics, designs, and animations through AI.
Collaboration tools:
Teams can collaborate easily within the platform, sharing projects and working together seamlessly.
<h2 id="paradox">9. Paradox AI for HR automation</h2>
Paradox AI uses conversational AI to automate HR processes, from recruitment to onboarding, making the entire HR workflow more efficient.
Key features:
AI-powered recruitment:
Conducts initial job applicant screenings via chatbots, schedules interviews, and manages the early stages of the hiring process.
Automated onboarding:
Guides new hires through the onboarding process, ensuring they have all necessary information.
Workflow automation:
Paradox AI automates repetitive HR tasks, allowing HR professionals to focus on strategic initiatives.
Conclusion
The adoption of generative AI tools is becoming essential for businesses across industries. These AI-driven solutions help optimize workflows, automate mundane tasks, and provide actionable insights that drive productivity. By integrating AI tools like Glean, GitHub Copilot, and others into their operations, companies can unlock new levels of efficiency and competitive advantage in an increasingly digital world.

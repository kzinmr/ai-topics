Hi, I’m Ariel Harnik and I’m Head of Partnerships at Hex. Today, I’m thrilled to launch
Hex’s Consulting Partnership Program
, which is built for data consultants to deliver new data ROI for their clients using Hex.
Every day, data teams around the globe rely on Hex to explore, analyze, and share their data. From reducing analytics sprawl to delivering insights faster to spinning up
interactive data applications
, Hex has become the trusted workspace for some of the most innovative companies in the world.
But achieving true data-driven transformation takes more than great technology — it requires collaboration and expertise. That's why we want to partner with you, and give you free Hex access to deliver even more value to your clients.
Whether you’re helping data analysts migrate to modern data platforms, advising long-term data strategies, or enabling advanced analytics workflows, we want to make it easier with Hex, for free 💫.
What is the Hex Consulting Partner Program?
This program is designed for consulting firms and data strategy leaders who want to take their analytics game to the next level. Our Consulting Partner Program is more than a partnership — it’s a shared journey to redefine what’s possible in data science and analytics.
This program is free to join.
No commitments, just value. Try it and see what you think!
You’ll get access to our
Enterprise version of Hex
, referral commission, and support for becoming a Hex expert in exchange for helping us put Hex in the hands of more data teams.
Based on what tier you’re in, you’ll have the opportunity to gain access to:
Financial benefits
: Earn referral commission and access co-selling opportunities.
Hex access
: Get a free Hex Enterprise account, training, and certifications to master our platform.
Dedicated support
: Get access to Slack channels, office hours, and even a partner manager for Petabyte partners.
Co-branded marketing
: Showcase your expertise with joint case studies, webinars, and more.
Up-level your value as a consultant with Hex
This program isn’t just about tools and perks — it’s about building deep, meaningful partnerships that help you deliver unmatched value to your customers. And we're here to support you every step of the way. Hex lets you offer your clients a
unified workspace for data science and analytics:
one platform for exploration, analysis, and visualization.
Let’s imagine you’re a consulting firm working with a client migrating to Snowflake. They’ve moved their data to the cloud but are still struggling to extract actionable insights and deliver them quickly to stakeholders. Hex is a great next step.
Hex integrates seamlessly with Snowflake (and other leading data platforms like BigQuery, Redshift, and Databricks), allowing you to:
Reduce analytics sprawl
: Instead of jumping between multiple tools, bring the entire data workflow into a single, organized workspace.
Fast-track insights
: Easily switch between SQL, Python, and no-code modalities to quickly prototype, execute, and collaborate on ad hoc projects.
Build interactive data apps
: Go beyond dashboards and create workflows that trigger automations and enable database writeback.

Partner Tiers
The Hex Consulting Partner Program is built to grow with you. Here’s a quick look at our tiered approach:
Gigabyte Tier
: Perfect for those just getting started working with Hex. Enjoy benefits like referral commissions, access to training, and even a free Enterprise Hex account.
Terabyte Tier
: Get advanced benefits, including marketing support, technical assistance, and a dedicated Slack channel.
Petabyte Tier
: Our top-tier partners receive all the above, plus dedicated partner managers, co-branded marketing materials, and even instructor-led training.

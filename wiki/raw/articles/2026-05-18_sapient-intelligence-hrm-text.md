# Sapient Intelligence Launches HRM-Text: Brain-Inspired 1B Parameter Reasoning Model

**Source:** https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/sapient-intelligence-launches-hrm-text-challenging-the-llm-monopoly-with-a-brain-inspired-foundation-model-trained-on-up-to-1000x-fewer-tokens-302774638.html
**Date:** May 18, 2026

Sapient Intelligence (Singapore/Beijing/Palo Alto) released HRM-Text, a 1B-parameter open-source hierarchical reasoning language model that challenges the Transformer monopoly. Key facts:

- Architecture: Non-Transformer, hierarchical recurrent latent-space reasoning with two stacks operating in nested recurrence (2 high-level + 6 low-level internal recurrent steps per forward pass)
- Training: ~40B tokens (up to 1000× fewer than typical LLMs), ~$1,000 on 16 GPUs over ~1 day
- Benchmarks: MATH 56.2%, ARC-Challenge 81.9%, DROP 82.2%, MMLU 60.7%
- Deployment: ~0.6 GiB at int4 precision, fits on smartphones
- Prior work: In June 2025, HRM outperformed DeepSeek R1 and OpenAI o3 on ARC-AGI Challenge

Team of 40+ from DeepMind, DeepSeek, xAI, Tsinghua Laboratory of Brain and Intelligence, MIT, Cambridge, CMU, Peking University. CEO: Guan Wang.

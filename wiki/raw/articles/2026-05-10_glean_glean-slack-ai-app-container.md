GleanによるAIサマリー
The new Glean AI integration in Slack's dedicated UI for AI assistants allows users to access enterprise-wide AI insights directly within Slack, enhancing collaboration and productivity.
Key features include suggested prompts, a persistent sidebar for multitasking, and thread history for easy access to past conversations, all designed to improve user experience and efficiency.
The integration of Glean's AI insights with Slack's work operating system sets a new standard for workplace productivity, providing a consolidated view of enterprise information and supporting dynamic, context-aware interactions.
At Glean, we’re always looking for innovative ways to enhance workplace productivity and streamline access to information. Although Glean already has a
Slack integration
, today we’re announcing our collaboration with Slack to bring a new Glean Work AI integration into Slack’s dedicated UI for AI assistants. Slack and Glean are already “must haves” for daily productivity at work and by bringing in this new AI assistant directly into Slack, daily collaboration will be even more seamless.
Customers can embed third party apps like Glean directly into Slack through the Assistant interface
Unlocking seamless AI integration with Glean in Slack
The new agent interface will directly embed Glean into Slack, making it a seamless conversational interface for AI apps at work. Users will be able to benefit from AI insights derived from across their entire enterprise knowledge corpus, all in the context of the active Slack conversation. They'll be able to effortlessly understand critical context regarding the active discussion at hand, receive insightful answers to queries, and benefit from dynamic suggestions based on the current task. Whether you need to check the status of a project, pull in insights to resolve a support issue, or help a sales colleague during an in-flight sales conversation, Glean can provide a consolidated view of all enterprise information across your entire data ecosystem, right within your Slack workspace.
Planned features
1.
Suggested Prompts:
Glean can make intelligent suggestions for what users should ask, dynamically adjusting based on the context of their work. This feature ensures that users get the most relevant information quickly and efficiently.
2.
Persistent Sidebar:
The Glean App will appear in a persistent sidebar within Slack, allowing users to multitask and chat for insights with Glean while continuing their other work. This seamless integration enhances productivity and keeps workflows uninterrupted.
3.
Thread History:
Each interaction with Glean is tracked via a thread, making it easy for users to revisit past conversations and find information quickly through a new History tab.
A collaboration that delivers the full view for everyone
Glean and Slack both believe in the power of open platforms, and by working together we can deliver more AI experiences for our joint customers. Slack has shortened the latency of enterprise collaboration, and Slack’s new AI assistant interface enables Glean’s AI insights from across the enterprise to find the fastest path to value.
Glean is committed to meeting users everywhere they work, and is proud to be among the first to integrate with the Slack user interface for agents and assistants. This integration aligns with our mission to expand human potential to do extraordinary work.
Looking ahead, together
We are incredibly excited about the potential that this integration will bring to our users. By combining Glean's enterprise-wide AI insights with Slack's work operating system, we are setting a new standard for workplace productivity and collaboration. This is just the beginning, and we look forward to continuing our journey with Salesforce to bring even more innovative solutions to our users.

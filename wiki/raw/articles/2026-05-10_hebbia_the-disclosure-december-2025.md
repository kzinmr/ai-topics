What's New: December 2025
In December, we shipped more of your most requested upgrades, making the platform faster, more powerful, and easier to use.
The Hebbia team didn’t slow down over the holidays. In December, we shipped more of your most requested upgrades, making the platform faster, more powerful, and easier to use. Here’s what the team has been up to over the past month.
Advanced PowerPoint Generation, Now with Tables
Create fully editable PowerPoint tables that automatically adjust rows, columns, and formatting for cleaner slide outputs.
Added Matrix Inputs
You can now make changes to context fields and reusable variables—like company, date, region—in the Matrix input section and have it instantly update across every column.
Screen companies with filters in CapIQ
Screen companies with multi-metric, range-based, and time-specific filters—plus location, industry, and founding year—so you can build more precise company universes in fewer steps.
Describe and create slides using natural language from chat
Instead of generating slides programmatically from Drafts or exports, you can now describe your slide in natural language with a prompt, and watch Hebbia make it live from scratch.
GPT 5.2 and Gemini 3 Flash Now Available
The latest reasoning models from OpenAI and Google are available in all supported regions.
Enhanced citation previews
Validate AI-generated insights in Chat, Matrix, and Draft with citation previews that display the underlying source data without disrupting your workflow.
Edit Matrix Agents
Customize every aspect of a Matrix Agent, including requirements around which documents to upload. This flexibility helps you scale Agents across more teammates, so you can institutionalize your firm’s workflows.
Upload Usability
Upload large batches of files with clear progress tracking, visibility, and failure alerts.
Website filtering in Chat and Matrix
When you use web as a source in Chat and Matrix, Hebbia now filters out low-credibility websites, ensuring you only receive insights from trusted, authoritative sources.
Enterprise and regional single tenancy
For our largest and most security conscious customers, we’ve now updated the way we can deploy our infrastructure with a new platform SKU.

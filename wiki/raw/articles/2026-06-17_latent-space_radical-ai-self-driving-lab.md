Radical AI's Joseph Krause on why the moat in materials is the lab, not the model. Interviewed by Brandon Anderson (Latent Space, June 17, 2026).

Key achievements:
- 1,200 alloys produced and characterized in 6 months (10x speedup over DARPA/GE MACH)
- AI scientist proposed 300 new materials, 10 with novel SOTA properties
- Can scale to 100 alloys tested and characterized per day

Open-source:
- TorchSim: PyTorch-based MD simulation framework, spun off as non-profit
- MATRIX/MATRIX-PT: benchmark dataset + model for self-driving labs. Improving reasoning in materials also improved reasoning for biological systems (unexpected transfer).

Joseph Krause's D.C. experience informs geopolitical framing: China's centralized model vs US approach to materials manufacturing.

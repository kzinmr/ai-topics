## Key Themes

### AI Adoption Framework
- "Treat AI proficiency as a learning curve, not a light switch"
- Get people to the 'Aha' moment as fast as possible
- Crowdsource what's working internally
- "The bottleneck is never the tool. It's always the first person in the room willing to look stupid using it out loud."

### Community-Driven Pro Tips

- Executives should narrate prompts live
- Shift from "we have AI tools" to "we use AI to think"

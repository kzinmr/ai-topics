# Hyperight Summary of 2026 AI Index Report

Stanford HAI's 2026 AI Index Report describes rapid acceleration in technical capabilities contrasted with lagging institutional preparedness.

## Key Highlights
- Frontier models reaching human baselines in PhD-level science and competition mathematics
- "Jagged frontier": AI agents succeed at **66%** of complex computer tasks, but fail to read analog clocks
- GenAI adoption: **53%** of global population — faster than PC or internet
- **80%** of U.S. students use AI; only **6%** of teachers have clear policies
- AI incidents: **362** (up from 233 in 2024)
- Transparency is declining
- Largest AI Index to date — primary resource for EU and global policymakers

## Urgent Need
- Independent measurement to track AI's trajectory as private-sector transparency decreases

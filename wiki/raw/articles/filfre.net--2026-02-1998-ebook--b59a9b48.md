# The Digital Antiquarian

Source: https://www.filfre.net/2026/02/1998-ebook/

Hi, folks…
Just a quick note to inform you that the ebook for 1998 is now available
on the usual page
. I’m sorry this was so long in coming. I owe a huge thanks to my hiking buddy Stefaan Rillaert, who adapted Richard Lindner’s original scripts to run on Linux instead of Windows.
We’ve elected to make the books available in .epub format only going forward. The .mobi format has been deprecated for some years now, and all but the oldest Kindle e-readers should have received software updates in 2022 to let them handle .epub files natively. If you are stuck with an extremely old Kindle, you can use
Calibre
to convert .epub files to .mobi on your computer. I hope this won’t be too much of an inconvenience.
Now that we’ve got the tool chain sorted, new ebooks should be appearing on a more timely basis. Thank you for your patience, and enjoy the 1998 book!

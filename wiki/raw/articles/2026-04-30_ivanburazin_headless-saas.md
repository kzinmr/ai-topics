# Headless SaaS

> New category emerging: Headless SaaS
> 
> Not infrastructure as a service / platform as a service
> 
> Traditional software (Photoshop, Slack, Jira) rebuilt with agent-first APIs.
> 
> - No UI
> - Programmatic access
> - Essentially the same product with different interface
> 
> Entirely new business model.

## Context

Ivan Burazin is CEO of Daytona, a company building composable computing environments (sandboxes) for AI agents. His framing of "Headless SaaS" distinguishes this from IaaS/PaaS — it's not about providing compute infrastructure, but about taking existing SaaS products and rebuilding them with APIs as the primary interface rather than human GUIs.

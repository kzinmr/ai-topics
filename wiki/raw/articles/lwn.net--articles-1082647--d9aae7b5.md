# Final normal Debian bookworm release

Source: https://lwn.net/Articles/1082647/

Final normal Debian bookworm release
[Posted July 13, 2026 by daroc]
Debian has
announced
the final normal update for Debian 12 ("bookworm"). Long-term-support updates will
continue until 2028
. As may be expected from a stable version, the update is mostly limited to security fixes. Still, it may be time for Debian users to look into upgrading to a more recent version. Conveniently, Debian 13 ("trixie") also
received an update
this weekend, with many of the same security fixes.

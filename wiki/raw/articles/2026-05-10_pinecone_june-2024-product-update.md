Pinecone Assistant
With Pinecone Assistant you get:
Simplicity: Just add your files and start building your killer AI app using
the API
.
High-quality
results: Relevant answers grounded in your data, with references.
Full control over your data: Your uploaded files help generate useful answers without training the model, and you can easily and permanently remove data.
Serverless namespace quota doubled to support massive multitenancy
The Enterprise
quota for namespaces per serverless index
has doubled to 100,000. You can use one
namespace
per tenant to build a
multitenant
solution. Namespaces allow you to physically partition records in an index. Queries and other operations are then limited to one namespace at a time, leading to faster response times and reduced costs.

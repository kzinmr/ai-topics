# Making it easier to understand how content was created and edited

**Source:** Google Blog (blog.google)
**Date:** May 19, 2026
**Authors:** Laurie Richardson (VP, Trust & Safety) and Pushmeet Kohli (Chief Scientist, Google Cloud / VP, Google DeepMind)

## Summary

At Google I/O 2026, Google announced a massive expansion of content transparency tools:
- SynthID verification expanding to Search and Chrome
- C2PA Content Credentials integration across Gemini, Search, Chrome, Pixel
- OpenAI, Kakao, ElevenLabs adopting SynthID as cross-industry standard
- 100B+ images/videos watermarked, 60,000 years of audio processed
- 50M verification uses globally via Gemini app
- AI Content Detection API on Google Cloud for enterprises
- Meta labeling Pixel camera-captured media with Content Credentials on Instagram

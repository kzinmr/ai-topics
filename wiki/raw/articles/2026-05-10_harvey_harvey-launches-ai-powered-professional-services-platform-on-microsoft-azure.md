:Harvey: Launches AI-Powered Professional Services Platform on Microsoft Azure
Announcing Harvey’s platform deployment in Microsoft Azure and the Azure Marketplace.
by
Winston Weinberg
and
Gabe Pereyra
•
Mar 19, 2024
San Francisco, California – March 19, 2024
– Harvey, a leading provider of generative AI solutions for the professional services industry, today announced that it has deployed its platform on Microsoft Azure and is now available in the Azure Marketplace, an online store providing applications and services for use on Azure. Microsoft and Harvey will collaborate closely to market and sell the Harvey on Azure solution to leading law firms, in-house teams, professional service providers and private equity firms globally.
Over the past year, Harvey has established itself as a
secure
generative AI platform for sophisticated professional services. Engagement on the platform has increased exponentially, revenue has risen more than tenfold since April, and we have assembled a world-class, multidisciplinary team. Working with Microsoft, we deployed domain-specific foundation models to address the most complex needs of our clients and advance the frontier of applied AI for professional services onto Azure.
By running on Azure, Harvey will offer its customers enhanced scalability, security, and performance, as well as seamless integration with other Azure services and tools. With this collaboration, customers can now purchase Harvey’s solutions directly from the Azure Marketplace.
“Deploying on Microsoft Azure is a key milestone for Harvey. This collaboration allows us to use Azure's robust cloud capabilities to enhance Harvey’s vision, making it more powerful and accessible for businesses across the world. We are also excited to join the Azure Marketplace, which will make it easier for customers to discover, purchase, and deploy our solution,” said Winston Weinberg, co-founder and CEO of Harvey.
"Harvey is on a mission to transform the industry with AI and cloud technology, and we are thrilled to collaborate with Microsoft to bring our platform to the Microsoft Azure ecosystem," said Gabriel Pereyra, co-founder and President of Harvey. "Azure provides us with the flexibility, reliability, and innovation we need to deliver value to our customers and scale our business."
Zia Mansoor, Corporate Vice President, Data & AI at Microsoft, commented, "We are pleased to welcome Harvey to Microsoft Azure. This collaboration aligns with our mission to empower every organization on the planet with cutting-edge technology. Harvey’s AI solutions, combined with Azure, will open new opportunities for our customers in the professional services space to use generative AI to help transform their businesses."

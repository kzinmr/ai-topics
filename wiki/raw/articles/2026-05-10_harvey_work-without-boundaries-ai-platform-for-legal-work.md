Work Without Boundaries: The AI Platform for Legal Work
Harvey now works where you work — across your tools, devices, organizations, and throughout your matters.
by
Harvey Team
•
Oct 15, 2025
Watch Video
Legal work has never been contained. By its nature, it flows across email threads and deal rooms, between Word documents and SharePoint folders, from desk to courtroom to airport lounge.
The result? Context shifts between systems. Knowledge scatters across tools. Collaboration fragments the moment work crosses a boundary — between offices, between organizations, between your desk and everywhere else legal work happens. And tools have not caught up — until now.
Harvey now works where you work — across your tools, devices, organizations, and throughout your matters. With our
recent releases
, Harvey becomes the AI platform for modern legal work, powering legal collaboration anytime, everywhere. It's AI that matches the reality of how legal professionals actually practice.
Tailored to Your Legal Expertise
Generic AI tools force legal professionals to work around their limitations. Harvey is purpose-built for legal work, maximizing your institutional knowledge rather than making you start from scratch or rely on generic AI.
In Harvey, you can
customize user groups, writing styles, and document formats
to match exactly how your team works. You can access, search, and cite over
100 legal data sources
from around the world without switching tools or losing context. With Harvey, your
playbooks
become living resources and your precedents become instantly accessible.
Soon, you’ll be able to
bring Harvey directly into your inbox
to get insights just like you would a colleague. Send ask@harvey.ai an email to get a response from Assistant without stepping out of your workflow.
Your Tools, Your Way
The most powerful tools are the ones you don't have to learn because they meet you where you already work. Now, Harvey meets you in three key places:
Through our Microsoft partnership, Harvey works
directly in
Word, Outlook, and SharePoint
. More on our partnership and capabilities coming on October 22.
Watch Video
On your phone, wherever work takes you.
With partners spending 61% of their time outside the office
, mobile isn't a nice-to-have — it's where work happens. Harvey's
mobile app
brings the power of the platform to your phone or tablet, so you can work securely wherever you are. Ask questions using dictation while reviewing a brief in the taxi to court. Scan and upload documents from a client meeting. Revisit your matters and access full context from anywhere your work takes you.
In your document management system.
For teams using iManage, we're connecting their trusted knowledge system with Harvey’s powerful AI.
iManage Search
within Harvey uses iterative search across an iManage matter to identify relevant documents in seconds, making firms’ institutional knowledge more accessible within Harvey.
Harvey in iManage
lets you select documents while in iManage and open them in Harvey to ask questions, analyze content, and create drafts — making it faster and easier to get insightful answers without breaking your workflow.
Manage Matters Faster
The main challenge facing legal AI today isn't technology — it's context. How do you bring together all the relevant information needed to answer a question correctly or produce work, without exposing it to the wrong user?
Right now, the burden falls on lawyers. They craft prompts, find and upload documents, select knowledge sources, choose workflows, and navigate between different product surfaces for different use cases. This results in a fragmented experience where critical context goes missing and outputs suffer. Admins and innovation teams — instead of transforming their firms — spend their time managing permissions, tracking usage across multiple tools, and troubleshooting how lawyers should gather the right context.
Matter OS
changes this dynamic by transforming Harvey into a single, context-complete workspace. Rather than manually piecing together documents, precedents, case law, emails, and workflows, lawyers can enter Matter OS.
And speed becomes exponential through
external sharing capabilities
. Shared playbooks, Vault projects, and workflows let you share knowledge in real-time and capture your team's collective expertise, turning collaboration from slow email chains into real-time knowledge building. When your team works from shared resources rather than reinventing approaches on every matter, you multiply capacity without adding headcount.
Meeting You Where You Work
We're meeting you where legal work actually happens — in your matters, in your Microsoft tools, on your mobile device.
This is Harvey as the AI platform for modern legal practice. Legal AI that works where you work, scales how you scale, and connects what's been disconnected.
Over the coming weeks, we'll share deeper insights into Matter OS and each component of today's announcement. For now, we invite you to experience what happens when your legal AI finally matches how you actually practice.
Because legal work has always been everywhere. Now Harvey is too.

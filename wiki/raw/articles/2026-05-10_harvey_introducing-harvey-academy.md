:Harvey: Academy: Shaping the Future of Legal AI Literacy
Introducing an education program that will help the legal industry move forward with AI responsibly.
by
Harvey Team
•
Jan 27, 2026
AI is transforming the way legal work gets done, but most lawyers are being asked to adapt without clear, practical guidance. Tools are moving faster than training, and traditional approaches to education aren’t designed for how legal teams actually operate.
This raises a critical question:
How do we empower prospective and current lawyers, teams, and organizations to learn AI in a way that feels approachable, relevant, and supportive?
As teams grow, generic AI education falls short. Organizations need domain-specific AI literacy that shows people how to apply AI directly within real legal and professional services workflows.
Harvey Academy is here to change that.
Today, we’re excited to introduce
Harvey Academy
, a centralized learning hub designed to make legal AI literacy accessible to everyone, helping legal professionals, law students and teams turn AI potential into practice.
Watch Video
The Path From Curiosity to Confidence
Legal AI
education is largely fragmented, disconnected from real use cases, and follows a one-size-fits-all model. With a library of on-demand videos and courses designed by legal practitioners, Harvey Academy provides structured, practical learning that helps legal professionals move from curiosity to confident, real-world AI use.
Lawyers, legal operations teams, innovation leaders, and law students with varying skill levels can leverage Harvey Academy to:
Deepen AI Skills
Harvey Academy includes a variety of resources, with plans to expand to a robust library in the coming months. Right now, you can access a free certification course designed to build foundational AI literacy, along with platform-integrated content that demonstrates how to use Harvey in legal practice. Here’s what’s available now:
Intro to Harvey
:
Get a glimpse into how Harvey’s AI capabilities work through videos and webinars
AI for Legal Basics
:
Learn the applications, limitations, and responsible use of legal AI through a comprehensive certification course
Harvey in Practice
:
Learn how to apply Harvey to real legal workflows through hands-on videos
These resources help you build AI fluency that translates into confident, real-world impact. You’ll also earn digital certificates and LinkedIn badges that demonstrate your newly acquired skills to your peers.
Unlock Full Product Value
In the coming months, Harvey customers and law students will be able to get guided onboarding and enablement to accelerate legal work. In addition to
Intro to Harvey
, you will have access to
Harvey Deep Dives
that include feature explainers, tips, and best practices to help you go deeper into what you can accomplish with Harvey. Together, these help bridge the gap between AI theory and real-world legal practice through applied, practical learning.
Enable Teams at Scale
Scaling AI education within your organization isn't a one-time project. It requires ongoing effort as new users start onboarding and engaging with resources on a rolling basis. The burden of managing all this often falls on platform administrators, innovation leaders, or legal ops teams that are already under-resourced. Our mission is to change that, by providing meaningful support that helps users and admins alike.
Harvey Academy will give admins turnkey resources they can use to accelerate team onboarding and reduce time-to-value. We’ll also be launching role-based and self-guided learning modules that will enable those in charge of AI deployments to drive sustained product adoption with measurable, workflow-driven enablement.
Moving the Legal Industry Forward With AI
Harvey Academy is leading the charge in equipping legal professionals with the AI education they need to drive consistent, high-quality outcomes. Whether you’re just getting started or ready to deepen your AI usage, Harvey Academy will help you move forward with confidence.
The future of legal learning starts here. Explore
Harvey Academy
today to get started.

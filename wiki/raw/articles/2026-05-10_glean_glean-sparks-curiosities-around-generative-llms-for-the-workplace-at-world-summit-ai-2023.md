AI Summary by Glean
Glean attended World Summit AI Americas 2023 in Montreal, sharing insights on how AI and LLMs can enhance knowledge management and AI-powered search.
Chau Tran, Tech Lead for LLMs and Vector Search at Glean, presented on the importance of separating coherence from knowledge and anchoring generative LLMs in domain-adapted semantic search.
Glean's presentation emphasized the integration of trustworthy generative models into modern enterprises to boost workplace efficiency.
Glean recently attended
World Summit AI Americas 2023
in Montreal – the world’s leading AI convention in the Americas. We were able to share with the curious crowd a deeper look into how AI and LLMs factor into knowledge management, and how Glean can help deliver on the promise of AI-powered search and discovery through trusted knowledge models.

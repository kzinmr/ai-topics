# Technical Performance Chapter Summary

From the Stanford HAI 2026 AI Index Report. Covers image, video, language, audio, reasoning, robotics, and agent performance in 2025–early 2026.

## 1. Rapid Capability Gains & Benchmark Saturation
- Humanity's Last Exam: frontier models gained **30 percentage points** in a single year
- **Jagged Intelligence**: Gemini Deep Think earned gold medal (35 points) at 2025 IMO, yet top models only read analog clocks correctly **50.6%** (vs 90.1% humans)

## 2. Competitive Landscape & "Big Four" Convergence
Arena Elo Ratings (March 2026):
- Anthropic: 1,503
- xAI: 1,495
- Google: 1,494
- OpenAI: 1,481
- Alibaba: 1,449
- DeepSeek: 1,424

Closed vs Open: gap reopened in 2025 — top closed model leads open by **3.3%** (up from 0.5% in 2024). Six of top ten models are closed-source.
U.S. vs China: gap effectively closed — U.S. leads by slim **2.7%**

## 3. Reliability and Gaming Concerns
- Invalid questions in benchmarks: **2% (MMLU Math)** to **42% (GSM8K)**
- Leaderboard gaming: Arena standings may reflect model adaptation to platform quirks

## 4. Specialized Capabilities
- **AI Agents (OSWorld)**: accuracy rose from **12% to 66.3%** (within 6% of human)
- **Video (Veo 3)**: zero-shot buoyancy simulation, maze solving
- **Professional domains**: 60-90% accuracy (tax, mortgage, legal). Top 15 models separated by only 3pp

## 5. Physical World (Robotics & AVs)
- Robots: 12% success in real household tasks; 89.4% in simulation
- Waymo: ~450,000 weekly trips (5 cities)
- Apollo Go (China): 11M driverless rides (175% YoY increase)

# Finally uniform AI labeling? Joint effort by OpenAI and Google

**Source:** heise online
**Date:** May 2026
**URL:** https://www.heise.de/en/news/Finally-uniform-AI-labeling-Joint-effort-by-OpenAI-and-Google-11302400.html

## Summary

European perspective on Google and OpenAI converging on SynthID + C2PA:
- EU AI Act enforcement starts August 2026 (penalties up to €15M or 3% of annual revenue)
- Fragmented verification tool landscape (Google, OpenAI, Content Authenticity Initiative, Digimarc)
- Google shutting down registration-free verification portal → requiring Google accounts
- SynthID watermark vs C2PA metadata: complementary roles
- Broader ecosystem: Pixel/Samsung camera integration, Instagram labeling, Getty requirements

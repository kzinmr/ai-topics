Big Words
May 7th, 2026

Big Words - a vibe-coded tool for generating presentation slides with AI. Users provide bullet points and it creates slides with large text. Built with Next.js/React, hosted on Vercel.

Key insight: Simon notes this as another example of the current wave of 'vibe coding' - building functional web apps through conversational AI interaction with Claude, Cursor, etc. The tool is minimal but demonstrates the democratization of software creation.

Simon also links to several other recent tools built this way, showing the acceleration of the vibe coding trend.

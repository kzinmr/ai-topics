# Perceptron AI Launches Physical AI Model (Perceptron Mk1)

**Source:** Yahoo Finance / BusinessWire  
**URL:** https://finance.yahoo.com/sectors/technology/articles/perceptron-ai-launches-physical-ai-153000413.html  
**Date:** May 12, 2026

## Summary
Perceptron AI announced Perceptron Mk1, a model purpose-built for video understanding and embodied reasoning. It delivers performance competitive with frontier models (Google, Anthropic, OpenAI, Qwen) at a fraction of the cost.

## Key Points
- Competitive on image, video, spatial-reasoning benchmarks at lower cost
- Founded November 2024 by Armen Aghajanyan (CEO) and Akshat Shrivastava (CTO), both former FAIR
- Headquartered in Bellevue, Washington
- Available via Perceptron AI API platform and OpenRouter
- Target use cases: manufacturing, media, robotics, geospatial, security, agentic tooling
- Enhances vision for Claude, Codex, and other text-first agents

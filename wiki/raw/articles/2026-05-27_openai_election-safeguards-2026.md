# Election Information and Safeguards in 2026

**Source:** OpenAI Blog
**URL:** https://openai.com/index/election-safeguards-2026
**Published:** May 27, 2026

---

## Overview

OpenAI outlines its coordinated strategy to protect the integrity of elections worldwide in 2026 — the second major election year since generative AI became widely available. The approach builds on 2024 efforts and focuses on five pillars:

- Surfacing reliable voting and results information
- Supporting cyber infrastructure defenders
- Increasing transparency around AI-generated content
- Combating misuse by bad actors
- Monitoring political bias to keep ChatGPT neutral

## Surfacing Reliable Information

- **Live vote counts:** In fall 2026, OpenAI will provide **live vote counts from The Associated Press** in the United States and Brazil.
- **Voting logistics (US):** Partnership with **Democracy Works** to show voting locations and deadlines.
- **Global search enhancements:** ChatGPT's web search continues to improve, delivering stronger answers with source links.

## Supporting Cyber Infrastructure Defenders

- **Daybreak initiative:** Recently announced effort to change how software is built and defended.
  - **Codex Security** — automatically identifies, validates, and helps remediate vulnerabilities.
  - **Trusted Access for Cyber (TAC)** — provides verified individuals access to frontier models for cyber defense.
- **Election‑specific support:** Codex Security and TAC access offered to **registered voting system manufacturers** in the US. Engagement with NASS and NASED.

## Increasing Transparency

OpenAI is deploying a **multi‑layered provenance approach**:

- **SynthID watermarks:** Partnership to embed SynthID digital watermarks directly into images generated through ChatGPT, Codex, or the OpenAI API.
- **C2PA metadata:** Continuing commitment to the Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity standard.
- **Public verification tool:** Site where anyone can check if an image was created by OpenAI tools.
- **Social media integration:** Encouraging platforms to use provenance markers as signals.
- **Legislative support:** Protect Elections from Deceptive AI Act (S. 1213) and Preparing Election Administrators for AI Act (S. 2346).

## Combatting Misuse

- **Usage policies enforced:** Strictly prohibit using tools for election interference, demobilization, or deception.
- **Improved detection:** Detection and enforcement systems upgraded since 2024.
- **Action on abuse:** When coordinated deceptive activity is detected, OpenAI restricts or terminates access.
- **Covert influence operations:** Intelligence, Safety, Security, and Integrity teams actively investigate and publish reports.
- **Political campaigning rules:**
  - **Prohibited:** Creating or distributing scaled campaign messaging for or against a candidate, party, or ballot measure.
  - **Allowed:** Responsible, human‑directed uses (drafting briefings, planning, translation, compliance).
  - **No political ads:** Advertisers will not be allowed to run political ads on ChatGPT.

## Monitoring

OpenAI continues monitoring for political bias to keep ChatGPT neutral on political topics and candidates.

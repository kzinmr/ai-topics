:Harvey: Strengthens Local Presence in Germany
Announcing local Harvey sales and support team in Germany.
by
Harvey Team
•
Jul 9, 2025
Harvey is deepening its investment in the German market by expanding the local sales and support team to serve clients more effectively in the market. This move reflects Harvey’s broader commitment to meeting rising demand for AI solutions in professional services throughout Europe.
Germany has quickly become a key market for Harvey, with innovative legal solutions from major law firms like
Gleiss Lutz
, growing adoption from in-house teams like
Deutsche Telekom
, and increasing interest in the practical application of legal AI.
“Germany is a critical part of Harvey’s European strategy,” said Winston Weinberg, Co-founder and CEO of Harvey. “We’re excited to support our clients with dedicated, on-the-ground teams who understand the local market and can partner closely with firms as they explore AI-powered transformation.”
With a local team, Harvey is positioned to offer more personalized engagement and deeper partnerships throughout the German legal and professional services community. As we expand our local presence, we remain deeply committed to privacy, security, and the responsible use of AI – principles that are foundational to how we build and support our platform.

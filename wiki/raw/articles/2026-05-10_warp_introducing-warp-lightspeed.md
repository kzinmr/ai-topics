# Introducing Warp Lightspeed: Built for AI Power Users

**Source**: [https://www.warp.dev/blog/introducing-warp-lightspeed](https://www.warp.dev/blog/introducing-warp-lightspeed)

Introducing Warp Lightspeed: Built for AI Power Users
Zach Lloyd
July 31, 2025
Over the past few months, we’ve seen Warp users push the boundaries of agentic development:
launching over 2 million agents per day and writing more than 100 million lines of code each week
using the #1 coding agent. From debugging massive monorepos to running complex multi-agent workflows, developers are relying on Warp to power serious, production-grade work.
Why we built Lightspeed
As usage has grown, so have the demands. While our Pro and Turbo plans work well for most developers, power users have consistently run into the same constraints: monthly request caps, indexing ceilings when working across large codebases, and hard stops that disrupt their workflows. Some even hit Turbo’s limits within just a few days.
We’re making Warp more efficient behind the scenes: optimizing how we consume tokens so users get more out of every request. But for developers pushing the limits every day, we needed to offer something more.
That’s why we built Warp
Lightspeed
: our new plan for high-throughput, agent-powered development, designed to unlock higher limits, better scale, and uninterrupted workflows.
What’s included
50,000 AI requests/month
: That’s 5x Turbo and 20x Pro. Ideal for full-time, high-volume AI development.
Access top-tier models
: including Claude 4 Opus, Claude 4 Sonnet, GPT-4.1, o4-mini, Gemini 2.5, and more.
State of the art coding agent
: Warp is #1 on Terminal-Bench (ahead of Claude Code by 20%) and 71% (top 5) on SWE-bench Verified.
Codebase Context
: 40 codebases, up to 100K files each. Perfect for large and multi-service projects.
If you exceed your monthly quota, usage continues.
Pay-as-you-go AI overages
let you add requests on demand without disruption.
You’ll also get unlimited Warp Drive usage, unlimited Session Sharing, and access to all of Warp’s core modern terminal features. As we roll out new capabilities, Lightspeed users will be the first to receive them.
Lightspeed is available at
$200 per user per month when billed annually
, or
$225 per user per month when billed monthly
. Full details are available on our
pricing page
.
What’s next
We’re continuing to improve how AI scales inside Warp – from clearer usage visibility, to richer coding tools, to more powerful agent capabilities. Lightspeed is a step forward in giving developers a plan that supports uninterrupted agentic development.
If you have feedback about Warp or the Lightspeed plan, reach out to
@warpdotdev
on X or join our
Slack community
to chat with the team directly.

# wonder

Source: https://jyn.dev/wonder/

this is a list of the times this year i felt wonder or joy. it's not exhaustive.
it's mostly for me. but it's also for others, because i think a sense of wonder is a wonderful thing,
and i wish it were valued more highly.
i want to give a very special thanks to my friends Nori, Kate Crane, and Kate F, for showing me how much beauty there is in the world.

Lull
, by Kelly Link. i cannot describe it and i will not try. it takes half an hour to read; more, if you are a fast reader.
Ancillary Justice
, by Ann Leckie. it dares to ask: what if a ship were a person, and not a person, and both at once? and it dares to imagine: what if there were a choir that sang with many bodies and voices, but only one mind? what kind of world could create such beauty?

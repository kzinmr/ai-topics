# Global AI Server Shipments Forecast to Grow Over 28% YoY in 2026 (TrendForce)

**Source:** TrendForce Press Center
**Date:** January 20, 2026
**URL:** https://www.trendforce.com/presscenter/news/20260120-12887.html

## Summary

TrendForce market forecast for AI servers in 2026:
- Global AI server shipments: +28% YoY
- ASIC-based AI servers: ~27.8% of shipments, highest since 2023
- GPU-based systems: ~69.7% share
- ASIC shipment growth outpaces GPU-based systems
- Google TPU leadership: 78% of Google AI servers are ASIC-based
- NVIDIA GB300 drives most GPU shipments; VR200 ramps in H2
- ASIC share projected to approach 40% by 2030

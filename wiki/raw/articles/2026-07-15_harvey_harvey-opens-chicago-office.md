:Harvey: Opens Chicago Office
by
Harvey Team
•
Jul 14, 2026
Chicago is home to one of the country's most important legal markets, with leading law firms and in-house legal teams advising global companies across finance, healthcare, manufacturing, insurance, and other major industries.
Opening our Chicago office brings us closer to the firms and legal teams we work with across the region. It builds on our existing presence in the city, including our integration with iManage and our work with DLA Piper, Dentons US, Foley & Lardner, Faegre Drinker, Hinshaw & Culbertson, Katten Muchin Rosenman, Mayer Brown, Winston Taylor, Croke Fairchild Duarte & Beres, Gould Ratner, Riley Safer Holmes & Cancila, and in-house teams at Avant, CCC Intelligent Solutions, and DRW. It also reflects our broader investment in Chicago through
our partnership with the Cubs
, and our
law school program
with the University of Chicago.
"Chicago's legal market serves a remarkably diverse economy," said John Haddock, Chief Business Officer at Harvey. "Having a local team allows us to work even more closely with our customers and continue investing in one of the world's leading legal markets."
We're continuing to grow our Chicago team across legal engineering, recruiting, sales, customer success, finance, legal, and IT.

:Harvey: to Expand Team with New London Office
Expanding Harvey’s footprint to Europe with the opening of our London office.
by
Harvey Team
•
Sep 16, 2024
Harvey is officially expanding its footprint to Europe with the opening of its London office. This office will serve as Harvey’s official European headquarters, and the London-based team will support Harvey’s growing EMEA customer base.
Harvey is already seeing remarkable demand for AI solutions in professional services around the globe. Today, Harvey’s customer portfolio includes over 40 EMEA-based firms.
“Europe has become one of Harvey’s fastest growing markets with strong interest and adoption of our platform,” explained Winston Weinberg, Co-Founder and CEO of Harvey. “To meet this demand, opening a London office has long been on our roadmap and we are excited to be able to serve our European customers more effectively.”
In addition to a highly engaged customer base, London stands out as a burgeoning AI talent hub thanks to excellent academic resources and government support.
The new office will be home to Harvey’s sales, strategic business development, customer success and AI research teams. To learn more about career opportunities with Harvey, visit

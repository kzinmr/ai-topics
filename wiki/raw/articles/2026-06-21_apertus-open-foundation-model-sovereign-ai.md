# Apertus — Open Foundation Model for Sovereign AI

Source: https://apertvs.ai/
HN Discussion: https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48622778 (406 points, 136 comments)

## Key Points (extracted from landing page)

Apertus is an open foundation model for sovereign AI, positioned as "Apertus is to AI as Open is to Source."

### Core Principles
- **Fully Open**: Training data, code, weights, methods, and alignment principles — all documented and reproducible
- **EU AI Act Compliant**: The model respects opt-outs, removes PII, prevents memorization
- **Global Foundation**: Built to be a foundation for the world to build on

### Technical Specs
- Available at two scales: **8B** and **70B** parameters
- Competitive with top open models at equivalent scale
- **Multilingual from day one**: trained on 1000+ languages
- A set of 16 small language models released to demonstrate distillation and quantization techniques

Landing page lists model variants available for download, including distilled and quantized versions.

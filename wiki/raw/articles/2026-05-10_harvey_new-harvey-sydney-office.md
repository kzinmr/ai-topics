:Harvey: to Expand Team with New Sydney Office
Announcing Harvey's new office opening in September in Sydney, Australia.
by
Harvey Team
•
Jul 7, 2025
Harvey is expanding its global footprint with the opening of a new office in Sydney, Australia this September to support our rapidly growing customer base. Harvey continues to experience strong global demand for AI solutions in professional services, and the Australian legal market is a priority region for us to continue to support in growth.
Australia has long been home to some of the world’s most innovative companies and legal minds, and we’re excited to help them go further. From global firms and litigation boutiques to in-house legal teams, we’re already partnering with leaders across the country to reimagine what’s possible in legal work, including leading firms
King & Wood Mallesons
,
Ashurst
, and
Gilbert + Tobin
.
Harvey is built to meet the needs of our Australian customers. Our strong security posture reflects the unique requirements of the Australian legal sector, giving users confidence that their information is protected. In addition, the Harvey platform provides embedded access to Australian legislative, case law, and regulatory knowledge sources – empowering legal teams to work more efficiently with trusted, jurisdiction-specific content.
“Australia has consistently demonstrated deep interest in Harvey’s platform, driven by forward-thinking legal teams and strong momentum in AI adoption,” said Winston Weinberg, Co-founder and CEO of Harvey. “Establishing a local presence in Australia allows us to better support our customers and strengthen our partnerships.”
The new office will house members of Harvey’s sales, customer success, and user operations teams. To learn more about career opportunities with Harvey, visit our
careers page
.

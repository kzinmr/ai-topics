Adam Shen
Software Engineer at Harvey
Adam Shen is a Software Engineer at Harvey working on Vault and Document Processing including ingestion, extraction, retrieval, and agent tools. Previously he worked at Block building Bitcoin Exchange features at scale such as order execution, transaction reconciliation, and integration with Square terminals. Before that, he worked on money movement at Robinhood building greenfield integrations such as debit card funding. Adam earned his Bachelors degree in Computer Science from UC San Diego and Masters degree from Yale University.

ElevenCreative Flows is the node-based creative canvas that connects 50+ image and video models alongside all ElevenLabs audio models into one visual workspace. Creators and marketers use Flows to chain modalities together: generate an image, animate it, add a voiceover, lip-sync it, score it with music and sound effects. Each step feeds into the next.
Flows Agent
Unmute
That chaining is also what makes Flows powerful for creative experimentation. Change one node - swap a voice, try a different video model, adjust the script - and re-run the pipeline without rebuilding everything else. Test dozens of creative directions from a single starting point: different products, avatars, scenes, languages, tones.
Flows Agent adds a conversational AI assistant that makes that experimentation faster.
Describe what you want. The agent builds and runs it.
Open the chat side panel in the Flows editor, describe what you want to create, and the agent handles the full pipeline. "Build me a podcast intro with custom music and voiceover." "Create a product ad with a lip-synced avatar." "Make a narrative video with layered sound effects."
The agent selects appropriate models, creates and connects the nodes, and executes the generations. It asks clarifying questions before generating to reduce wasted credits - confirming details like video length, tone, and structure before committing to expensive operations.
Flows agent
Iterate through conversation
The agent makes iteration fast. "Try a warmer voice." "Swap the background to an office." "Make a version in Spanish." "Use Veo instead of Kling." The agent modifies the existing pipeline and re-runs - you move through creative directions without rebuilding from scratch. The cost of exploring a new variant drops to a sentence.
Spending control with assist mode
Assist mode gives you approval control over costly operations. When enabled, the agent pauses before running expensive generations and asks for your confirmation. You stay in control of credit usage while the agent handles workflow construction and orchestration.
Works in the background
Start a complex workflow, close the tab, and come back to a completed flow. Flows Agent also works alongside multiplayer - collaborators in the same workspace can see the agent building nodes and executing generations in real time.

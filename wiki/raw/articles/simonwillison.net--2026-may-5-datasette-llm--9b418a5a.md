Source: https://simonwillison.net/2026/May/5/datasette-llm/#atom-everything

Part of Datasette's evolving support mechanism for plugins that use LLMs. It's now possible to configure a model with default options, e.g. to say all
enrichment
operations should use a specific model with temperature set to 0.5.

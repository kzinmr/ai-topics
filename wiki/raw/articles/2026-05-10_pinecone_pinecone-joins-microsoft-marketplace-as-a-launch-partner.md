We are excited to join the new Microsoft Marketplace and be a featured partner in the AI Apps and Agents category. If you’re already on Azure or leveraging Azure’s OpenAI Service, you can now quickly add Pinecone to your AI stack with just a few clicks.
The Microsoft Marketplace provides an extensive catalog of software solutions certified and optimized for Azure. If you’re an Azure customer, this means:
Fast, simple procurement:
Skip the pre-approvals, streamline the procurement process, and get Pinecone to production faster.
Consolidated spending and billing:
Purchase Pinecone through the marketplace and meet your Microsoft Azure Consumption Commitment (MACC) faster. View your usage across Azure in a single place.
Pinecone on Microsoft Marketplace is currently available for Standard and Enterprise plans. We will add support for customers with annual commitments in the coming months. Read our
setup guide
or visit the
Marketplace listing
to get started.
Pinecone is also available on other marketplaces. Check out our

# How Endava builds an agentic organization with Codex

Source: https://openai.com/index/endava
Date: May 28, 2026

Endava, a global software contracting firm, has become an "agentic organization" — where senior expertise is codified into agents working alongside teams across the entire client engagement lifecycle.

Key impact: requirements analysis compressed from weeks to hours. Sequential stages (analysis, design, build) collapsed into a single unified workflow.

Codex used as a general desktop agent across the full delivery lifecycle: requirements analysis, design, specifications, development, and operations.

Senior-junior dynamic: Seniors articulate architectural intent via Codex; juniors produce senior-level outputs while learning best practices through real-time guidance. Knowledge transfer becomes scalable.

Leadership takeaways:
1. Codify your seniors — capture judgment in Codex
2. Treat Codex as desktop agent, not just coding assistant
3. Start with non-coding workflows (requirements, design, client communication)

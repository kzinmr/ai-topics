# Hello World Collection

Source: https://boyter.org/2011/10/hello-world-collection/

Hello World Collection
2011/10/18
(83 words)
I was looking through the logs of what people are searching for and noticed there is a large amount of “Hello World” searches such as “hello world java” which actually came up with no results. I couldn’t have that continue so I quickly integrated the
Hello World Collection
You can now search for hello world in 400+ languages (although curiously no in Clojure). Just type hello world and the language you are searching for EG,
helloworld java
hello world lisp
hello world php

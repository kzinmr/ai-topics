# SpaceX Acquires xAI in Record-Setting Deal

**Source:** Reuters / BBC  
**Date:** February 2-3, 2026  
**URLs:**
- https://www.reuters.com/business/musks-spacex-merge-with-xai-combined-valuation-125-trillion-bloomberg-news-2026-02-02/
- https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cq6vnrye06po

## Summary

SpaceX acquired xAI in an all-stock deal valuing SpaceX at $1 trillion and xAI at $250 billion ($125 billion per some sources), making it the world's most valuable private company. xAI investors received 0.1433 SpaceX shares per xAI share.

## Key Details

- **Combined valuation**: $1.25 trillion (SpaceX $1T + xAI $250B)
- **Structure**: All-stock; xAI investors receive 0.1433 SpaceX shares per xAI share
- **Alternative**: Some xAI execs could opt for cash at $75.46/share
- **xAI $20B Series E**: Raised January 6, 2026, before acquisition
- **Prior M&A**: xAI acquired X (Twitter) in March 2025

## Strategic Rationale
Musk framed the merger as creating an "innovation engine" combining AI, rockets, space-based internet, and media under one roof. Key thesis: space-based data centers and AI satellites as the "only way to scale" AI compute.

## Corporate Entanglements
- Tesla sold $430M in Megapacks to xAI for data centers (2025)
- Tesla invested $2B in xAI Series E (converted to SpaceX stock after merger)
- Tesla shareholders who approved $2B AI investment now hold minority SpaceX stake
- Total Tesla revenue from Musk companies (xAI + SpaceX): $573.4M in 2025

## Regulatory Context
- Deal drew potential antitrust scrutiny
- Mississippi regulators held permit meeting for xAI power plant on election day
- EU/UK investigations into Grok image generation features

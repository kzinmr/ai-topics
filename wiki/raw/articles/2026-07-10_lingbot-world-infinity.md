# LingBot-World-Infinity

LingBot-World 2.0 (LingBot-World-Infinity) is an open-source real-time interactive world model developed by the THU-KING-NIC-Lab.

**Key claims** (from X/Twitter discussion, @rohanpaul_ai, @MrOnsase):
- The biggest challenge for interactive world models is not higher resolution — it is maintaining a coherent world over long periods
- Most world models start breaking after seconds or minutes; LingBot-World 2.0 reports a single 60-minute rollout spanning 20 distinct scenarios with no decay
- Causal, action-conditioned world modeling
- 32K impressions, 174 likes on the announcement tweet
- Open-source release

**Project page**: https://www.lingbot-world.org/

**HN thread**: 2 points, 2 comments. Limited community discussion but the technical achievement (60-minute coherent rollouts) is significant for the world models field.

# Mathematical methods and human thought in the age of AI

**Authors:** Terence Tao
**Date:** March 2026
**Subject:** Mathematics and AI

## Summary
Artificial intelligence (AI) is the name popularly given to a broad spectrum of computer tools designed to perform increasingly complex cognitive tasks, including many that used to solely be the domain of human thought. This paper explores the relationship between mathematical reasoning and AI systems.

## Key Themes
- The role of mathematical methods in AI cognition
- Human thought processes vs AI computational patterns
- Theoretical foundations of machine reasoning
- Connections between formal mathematics and neural network behavior

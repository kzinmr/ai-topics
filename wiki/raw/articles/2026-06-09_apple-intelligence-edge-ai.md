# Apple Intelligence — On-Device AI at Scale

**Date:** June 9, 2026 (WWDC 2026)
**Source:** Apple Newsroom

## Overview

Apple Intelligence represents the company's comprehensive on-device AI strategy, bringing powerful AI capabilities into everyday experiences while maintaining Apple's privacy-first approach. This is a landmark deployment of edge AI at consumer scale.

## Key Themes for Edge AI

### On-Device Processing
- Core AI processing runs locally on Apple Silicon (A-series and M-series chips)
- Neural Engine hardware acceleration for ML workloads
- Privacy-preserving architecture — data never leaves the device for most tasks
- Private Cloud Compute for more complex tasks that exceed on-device capacity

### Capabilities
- System-wide Writing Tools (rewrite, proofread, summarize)
- Image generation and editing (Image Playground, Genmoji)
- Siri enhancements with personal context understanding
- Cross-app intelligence and automation

### Edge AI Significance
This represents the largest deployment of on-device AI to date, with hundreds of millions of devices gaining advanced AI capabilities. It demonstrates:
- Viability of on-device LLM inference at consumer scale
- Privacy-preserving AI as a differentiator
- Apple Silicon's role as an edge AI accelerator platform

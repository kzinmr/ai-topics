needing
AI doom to be true to justify their life choices. Or acceleration. Or
something

impact

GLM-5.2 blog post

comma blog posts

It’s all just nonsensical hype, it’s not about technology. It’s a questionable promise of future technology. The reason they can’t just write technical blog posts is that the current technology doesn’t justify the valuation. From
schizoposting

The only possible conclusion is that it’s designed to cause panic. In fact,
it is optimized for it: there is no possible framing of the actual
product(s) that could possibly induce more psychological spiraling in
the media and its audience. “The AI Apocalypse?” has provided a yearslong news cycle and an infinite spawner of contrived Discourse, which
has primarily served to contextualize the AI industry’s valuation around
not reality but hypothetical future value—i.e. a baseless assertion of
infinite growth between the lines of a public-facing eschatology.

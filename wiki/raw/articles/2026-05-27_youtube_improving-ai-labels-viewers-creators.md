# Improving AI labels for viewers and creators

**Author**: The YouTube Team
**Published**: 2026-05-27
**Source**: https://blog.youtube/news-and-events/improving-ai-labels-viewers-creators/

## Summary

YouTube announced two updates to its AI content labeling system: simplified AI labels and auto-detection of AI-generated content. Since 2024, YouTube has been labeling content when creators disclose AI tool usage. Based on community feedback, the platform is making the process simpler and more intuitive. The auto-detection system can now identify AI-generated content without requiring creator disclosure.

# Cohere triples UK footprint with new London office to support R&D growth  

**Source**: [https://cohere.com/blog/cohere-triples-uk-footprint-with-new-london-office-to-support-r-and-d-growth](https://cohere.com/blog/cohere-triples-uk-footprint-with-new-london-office-to-support-r-and-d-growth)

Key takeaways
A bigger home in London:
Cohere is moving to 100 New Oxford Street, nearly tripling our current office footprint.
Backing the UK's AI ecosystem:
The expansion reflects our investment in London's world-class AI talent and growing R&D presence.
Accelerating sovereign AI in Europe:
The move builds on our growing momentum across the UK and Europe as demand for secure, enterprise-grade AI continues to rise.
Cohere is expanding its UK presence with a move to
100 New Oxford Street
, placing the company at the heart of one of the strongest artificial intelligence clusters in Europe, close to leading AI talent. The new office gives Cohere over 14,000 sq ft of workspace, almost tripling the size of its current footprint and creating capacity for up to 100 people.
The expansion represents a strategic milestone in the company's broader European growth trajectory. The move builds upon several key developments including its intent to join forces with Germany’s Aleph Alpha announced in April, the acquisition of Reliant AI, and Memorandums of Understanding with both Spain's Indra Group and the UK Government.
These partnerships establish a robust framework for collaboration on sovereign AI initiatives while the company continues to expand its customer footprint through deals with industry leaders like RWS and The Aston Martin Aramco Formula One Team.
“This move represents an investment in London's R&D ecosystem and our ability to service growing customer demand for frontier, enterprise-grade AI. By expanding our London footprint threefold, we're positioning ourselves at the heart of the UK's sovereign AI revolution, where government and enterprise interest in secure artificial intelligence is accelerating.”
Aidan Gomez
Co-Founder and CEO
“London is at the forefront of the AI revolution, and Cohere's decision to significantly expand its presence in our capital is a strong vote of confidence in our city, our talent and our economy. This investment will create exciting new opportunities for Londoners, support high-skilled jobs and help cement London's position as a global leader in artificial intelligence, as we continue building a more prosperous London for everyone.”
Sadiq Khan
Mayor of London
“Cohere’s expansion here shows their confidence in this country’s brilliant AI talent. This is a clear demonstration that Britain is the place to build, hire and grow. Their focus on sovereign AI - helping businesses and government deploy this technology securely, on their own terms - is exactly the kind of capability we are building in Britain. We want the world's most innovative AI companies growing and hiring here, that's how we create high-skilled jobs and ensure Britain leads on AI.”
Kanishka Narayan
The Minister for UK AI and Online Safety
London has long served as a key hub for Cohere's engineering, research, and commercial operations, with both CEO Aidan Gomez and CTO Phil Blunsom based in the UK. Across London, the UK, and Europe, Cohere continues to invest in talent spanning AI engineering, applied machine learning, infrastructure, and customer deployment teams. With strong revenue from active contracts across highly regulated sectors including finance, healthcare, manufacturing, telecommunications, energy, and the public sector, Cohere's London footprint is closely aligned with the city's strengths in financial and professional services, where its sovereign AI solutions are particularly well suited.
“The UK combines unparalleled research talent with forward-thinking regulatory frameworks. This expansion builds on the country's unique position as a global AI hub, bridging European values with international market access and helping shape the projected $600 billion sovereign AI market.”
Aidan Gomez
Co-Founder and CEO
The office will undergo a new fit-out led by London-based
Thirdway Design
due to complete later this year and will consolidate Cohere's growing team on a single floor in a warm, light-filled contemporary workspace designed to foster collaboration and innovation.

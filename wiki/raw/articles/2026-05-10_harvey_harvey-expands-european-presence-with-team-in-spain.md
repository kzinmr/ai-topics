:Harvey: Expands European Presence With Team in Spain
Announcing the launch of a dedicated Harvey team in Spain.
by
Harvey Team
•
Sep 22, 2025
Harvey is expanding its European footprint with a dedicated team in Spain, reinforcing our commitment to supporting legal professionals with localized expertise and trusted AI infrastructure. With a local Sales and Support team, we’re partnering more closely with clients across the Iberian legal market — from leading law firms to mid-sized practices and in-house legal teams.
This launch is part of Harvey’s broader global growth strategy and reflects our commitment to meeting rising demand for AI solutions in professional services throughout Europe. Spain and Portugal have emerged as two of the most innovative and engaged legal markets on the continent, and we’re excited to better support that momentum.
We’re proud to already work with leading professional services firms across Spain and Portugal like
Cuatrecasas
, Vieira de Almeida,
PwC
, PLMJ, and
B. Cremades & Asociados
, and in-house legal teams at
Carrefour
and
Repsol
. Our local team enables even stronger collaboration, deeper partnerships, and greater responsiveness to evolving client needs.
“We’ve seen strong momentum in the Iberian market from firms eager to apply AI to legal work,” said Winston Weinberg, Co-Founder and CEO of Harvey. “Our growing local presence allows us to better support our customers and contribute to the legal community’s transformation.”
The Harvey platform includes embedded access to Spanish legislation, with Portuguese data coming soon, giving legal professionals the ability to work with local content directly within their workflows — securely, efficiently, and at scale. As always, we prioritize security, privacy, and responsible innovation, ensuring our solutions are aligned with regional expectations and legal standards.

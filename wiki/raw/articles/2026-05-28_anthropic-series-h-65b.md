# Anthropic raises $65B in Series H funding

Anthropic has raised $65 billion in Series H funding led by Altimeter Capital, Dragoneer, Greenoaks, and Sequoia Capital, valuing the company at $965 billion post-money.

Key details:
- Run-rate revenue crossed $47B earlier in May 2026
- Co-led by: Capital Group, Coatue, D1 Capital Partners, GIC, ICONIQ, XN
- Significant investors: AMP PBC, Baillie Gifford, Blackstone, Brookfield, D.E. Shaw Ventures, DST Global, Fidelity, General Catalyst, Insight Partners, Jane Street, Lightspeed, MGX, NTTVC, NX1 Capital, Situational Awareness LP, T. Rowe Price, Temasek
- $15B of previously committed investments from hyperscalers including $5B from Amazon
- Strategic infrastructure partners: Micron, Samsung, SK hynix
- Amazon: up to 5GW of new compute capacity
- Google/Broadcom: 5GW of next-generation TPU capacity
- SpaceX: access to GPU capacity in Colossus 1 and Colossus 2
- Claude first frontier model on all three major clouds: AWS, Google Cloud, Azure
- Funding intended to advance safety/interpretability research, expand compute, scale products

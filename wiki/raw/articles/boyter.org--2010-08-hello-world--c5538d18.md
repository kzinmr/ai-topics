# Launched. Somewhat.

Source: https://boyter.org/2010/08/hello-world/

Launched. Somewhat.
2010/08/02
(135 words)
Well I finally got around to doing what I always said I would. That being launch and online business. Inspired by all of the stories and discussions at news.ycombinator.com and in particular this single post
http://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=1560422
I decided to finish off those projects that I have been working on for a while and launch.
Naturally following the experience of the greats who have done it themselves, such as Patrick with Bingo Card Creator and Peldi of Balsamiq I thought I would throw up a blog, pick a theme quickly and get started.
Anyway more will be coming in the next few days, but I think blogging about the experience while I go should be helpful. My goal is to post a blog post every day for my own reference even if nobody ever reads them.

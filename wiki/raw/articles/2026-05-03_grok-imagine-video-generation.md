# Grok Imagine 1.0 — xAI's Video Generation Platform

**Source:** blog.mean.ceo, AllInOneAICenter, May 2026
**URLs:** https://blog.mean.ceo/new-ai-model-releases-news-may-2026/, https://allinoneaicenter.com/blog/new-ai-tools-may-2026

## Overview

Grok Imagine 1.0 is xAI's full-stack video generation platform, launched February 3, 2026. It supports text-to-video and image-to-video generation at 720p resolution. As of May 2026, it holds the top position for image-to-video on DesignArena by Arcada Labs with an Elo score of 1,329, beating Runway Gen-4.5, Sora 2 Pro, and Google Veo 3.1.

## Timeline & Features

| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| Feb 3, 2026 | Grok Imagine 1.0 launch: text-to-video, image-to-video, 720p, up to 10 seconds |
| Mar 2, 2026 | "Extend from Frame" update: chain clips up to 15 seconds |
| Mar 15, 2026 | Up to 7 still image references per video; max 30 seconds |
| May 2026 | Native audio generation (ambience, sound effects, short dialogue) |

## Platform Capabilities (May 2026)

- **6 Generation Modes:** Text-to-Video, Image-to-Video, Reference (up to 7 image anchors), Extend, Modify, built-in editing suite
- **Native Audio:** Ambience, sound effects, spoken dialogue
- **Reference Mode:** Upload multiple images of a character/product; maintains visual consistency across entire video without locking first frame

## Adoption

- 1.245 billion videos generated in the 30 days following 1.0 launch

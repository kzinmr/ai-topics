AI Summary by Glean
Glean and Miro have partnered to integrate Glean's enterprise knowledge capabilities into Miro's Innovation Workspace, enhancing collaboration and productivity.
The integration allows users to access relevant enterprise knowledge, documents, and resources directly within Miro, streamlining the workflow and reducing the need to switch between applications.
This partnership aims to foster innovation by providing teams with the information they need to make informed decisions and drive analysis.
We are pleased to announce Glean in
Miro
, bringing Glean's Work AI platform directly into Miro's Innovation Workspace, unveiled at Miro's Canvas 2024 keynote. This partnership addresses a significant challenge in the innovation process: ensuring that all enterprise knowledge is accessible when needed throughout various phases of work.
Bringing enterprise knowledge to where you work
Imagine a product team kicking off a brainstorming session. With Glean integrated directly into the Miro canvas, the team can access all relevant feedback and insights from previous projects and market research. This enables them to build on existing knowledge rather than reinventing the wheel.
Glean leverages large language models to pull data from any of its 100+ connected enterprise tools and public knowledge sources. This information can be parsed through select filters in a Miro canvas to seamlessly integrate into existing workflows that help teams move from idea to outcome faster.
Going beyond the box
Glean believes in Work AI for all, and that goes beyond simple chatbots. This partnership with Miro achieves that by creating smarter interfaces and bringing AI capabilities directly into the tools used for innovation. This integration simplifies the discovery phase of product development or brainstorming by simplifying insight gathering without needing prompt engineering expertise. With just a click, relevant information can be incorporated into Miro boards, enhancing the brainstorming process and boosting productivity.
Sign up today
Glean customers who have Miro enterprise plans can begin exploring how these tools can transform their  Miro experience and elevate the way they work. Join the waitlist for early access:
miro.com/innovation-workspace

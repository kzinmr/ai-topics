# Introducing Gemini Omni

Natively multimodal generative model. Takes any combination of text/image/audio/video and produces high-quality video. Grounded in Gemini's real-world knowledge — physics, history, science.

## Capabilities
- **Video editing through conversation**: multi-turn natural language editing
- **World knowledge & physics**: gravity, fluid dynamics, kinetic energy
- **Create from any inputs**: synchronize text, image, video, audio references
- **Digital Avatars**: Create videos with your own voice/face
- **SynthID watermark**: All generated videos include imperceptible digital watermark

## Availability
- Gemini Omni Flash rolling out to Google AI Plus/Pro/Ultra subscribers globally
- YouTube Shorts & YouTube Create App: no cost starting this week
- Developer APIs coming in weeks

# OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Unite Against China AI Distillation

**April 6-7, 2026**

## Key Points

- Three companies sharing threat intelligence through **Frontier Model Forum** (founded 2023 with Microsoft)
- **Adversarial distillation**: Chinese firms allegedly extracted outputs from US frontier models at scale to train competing models
- US officials estimate **billions of dollars annually** in losses
- DeepSeek's R1 (Jan 2025) triggered current scrutiny
- OpenAI warned Congress in Feb 2026 that DeepSeek is "state-controlled"
- This is **attempt seven** to stop distillation; previous six approaches all failed
- China's Global Times criticized the alliance as protectionist

## Previous Failed Approaches

1. Terms of Service bans
2. Geographic access blocks (circumvented via intermediaries)
3. AI output fingerprinting (>80% removal rate per IEEE SaTML 2026)
4. Congressional testimony
5. IAPS policy paper (no government response)
6. Lobbying for outright bans on PRC-produced models

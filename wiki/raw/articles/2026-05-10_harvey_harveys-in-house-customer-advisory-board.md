Announcing :Harvey:’s Inaugural In-House Customer Advisory Board
A global group of General Counsel and Chief Legal Officers who are redefining what Legal can be in the age of AI.
by
Harvey Team
•
Mar 16, 2026
Harvey got its start working with law firms, and in so doing, we learned a lot from them. They have been design partners in what we build, the source of feedback on how we can iterate and improve, and ultimately key partners in helping ensure our technology, our team, and our approach is tailored to support the world’s best legal teams.
Now, we’re applying that same approach to in-house teams and requesting that same level of feedback and partnership we’ve been fortunate to have with law firms for 3+ years. Harvey now works with more than 500 in-house legal teams worldwide. From day one, we've partnered closely with these teams to build a platform designed for their needs.
We’ve learned from their challenges and iterated on their feedback, in order to continuously evolve alongside one another.
We're now deepening that commitment with the launch of Harvey's inaugural In-House Advisory Board: a global group of General Counsel and Chief Legal Officers who are redefining what Legal can be in the age of AI.
This Advisory Board represents the leading edge of our community — executives who are not only transforming their own legal departments with AI, but helping drive AI adoption across their entire organizations.
Shaping the Future of In-House Legal
The role of the General Counsel has never been more consequential.
Today's legal leaders guide enterprise risk, shape business strategy, navigate regulatory complexity, and increasingly define how AI is deployed responsibly across the organization.
Generative AI accelerates that shift. It enables Legal to operate with greater speed and insight — while positioning General Counsel as key voices in enterprise AI governance and strategy.
The members of this Advisory Board reflect that evolution. They are embedding AI into the core of Legal and influencing how their broader enterprises approach the technology.
The purpose of the board is clear: provide strategic input on Harvey's direction, inform product priorities, and engage in candid dialogue about the future of the legal industry, including how firms and companies can collaborate at scale using AI. We're building that future together.
Our Criteria: Leaders Changing the Game
We selected members for perspective and impact. Each leader on this board operates at the forefront of their industry, demonstrates a clear commitment to AI and innovation, and challenges traditional models of how Legal functions. They play active roles in shaping AI adoption across their organizations and see Legal as a strategic driver of enterprise value. In partnership with our team, these leaders are helping define what the future of collaboration looks like with law firms and across the enterprise.We intentionally designed a small, highly engaged group representing diverse industries and geographies. The focus is depth of insight and meaningful influence over breadth.
As Winston Weinberg, CEO of Harvey, shared:
"The leaders joining this Advisory Board aren't just transforming their legal departments. They’re helping shape how AI is deployed responsibly across their organizations. We're proud to partner with them as we build the future of AI-powered Legal together."
Meet the Inaugural Members
Alison Zoellner
Group General Counsel, Dentsu Group Inc.
Alison oversees legal matters across Dentsu’s global organization. She previously held senior leadership roles at Honeywell and The New York Times Company and began her career at Sullivan & Cromwell.
Andrew Stephens
General Counsel, MongoDB, Inc.
Andrew has led legal strategy through MongoDB’s rapid growth and public market expansion. He previously held senior roles at LinkedIn and Logitech and has guided companies through successful IPOs.
Bob Hoyt
Chief Legal Officer, HSBC Holdings plc
Bob leads HSBC’s global legal function and serves on the Group Operating Committee. He previously served as General Counsel of the U.S. Department of the Treasury and Associate Counsel to the President.
Kamala Vasagam
Senior Vice President, Chief Counsel for Global Legal Operations & Innovation, NBCUniversal
Kamala leads legal operations, technology, and innovation for NBCUniversal’s global legal function. She previously served as General Counsel of the U.S. Office of Personnel Management, as Special Assistant to the President at the White House, and in private practice.
Ray Geoffroy
Senior Vice President, General Counsel, and Corporate Secretary, Koch, Inc.
Ray leads global legal and compliance, government affairs, and public policy functions across Koch and serves on multiple corporate boards within the Koch enterprise. He previously practiced at Hunton Andrews Kurth.
Tracey Brady Yurko
Chief Legal Officer and Corporate Secretary, Bridgewater Associates, LP
Tracey oversees legal, regulatory, and corporate governance matters at Bridgewater and serves as a Partner at the firm. She previously practiced at Milbank and Dewey & LeBoeuf and serves on multiple industry boards.
Together, this group reflects the global, AI-forward direction of modern in-house Legal — and the expanding influence of General Counsel in enterprise AI strategy.
A Long-Term Commitment to In-House Teams
As AI adoption accelerates, the next phase of transformation will be shaped by executives who can connect legal expertise, business strategy, and responsible AI deployment across the organization. This Advisory Board represents our long-term investment in that future.
This marks the beginning of a broader advisory ecosystem. Over time, we will establish additional advisory boards across law firms, professional services, and other segments of the legal market — ensuring our customers remain central to how Harvey evolves.
The future of Legal will not be built in isolation. It will be built in partnership.

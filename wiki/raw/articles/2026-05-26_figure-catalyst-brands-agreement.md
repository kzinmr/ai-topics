# Figure Signs Agreement with Catalyst Brands

Figure AI announced a commercial agreement with Catalyst Brands to deploy Figure humanoids into their distribution and logistics network.

Key details:
- Collaboration starts at Catalyst's Reno, Nevada Distribution Logistics Center
- Catalyst Brands operates JCPenney, Aéropostale, Brooks Brothers
- Focus on automating physically demanding tasks within supply chain
- Seamless integration into distribution facility
- Humanoids as the only form factor with this ability
- Workforce modernization: shifting associates to higher-value work
- First commercial bridge between Figure and a Brookfield portfolio company
- Brookfield is a shared investor in both Figure and Catalyst

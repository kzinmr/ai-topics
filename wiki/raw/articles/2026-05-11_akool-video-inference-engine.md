# AKOOL Unveils Breakthrough AI Video Inference Engine

PALO ALTO, Calif., May 11, 2026 /PRNewswire/ — AKOOL announced a major breakthrough in AI video infrastructure with a production-grade video inference engine delivering 10–20× faster performance than conventional approaches.

Traditional AI video generation required tens of seconds per clip. AKOOL's system reduces that to 1–3 seconds, with real-time streaming at sub-30ms latency per frame.

CEO Jiajun (Jeff) Lu: "We didn't just optimize one part of the pipeline—we rebuilt the entire stack."

Key technical approach: full-stack optimization across algorithm design, hardware execution, reduced computational steps, increased GPU parallelism, eliminated runtime overhead, next-gen GPU architectures.

The infrastructure operates across cloud, real-time streaming, and on-device deployments. Already powering AKOOL's products including Live Camera for real-time digital avatars and live translation.

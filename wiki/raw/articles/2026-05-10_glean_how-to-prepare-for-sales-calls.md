AI Summary by Glean
Conduct research and understand the customer's business, their current pain points, and potential solutions before the call.
Prepare a tailored agenda and personalized value proposition, highlighting specific benefits and addressing the customer's unique needs.
Practice active listening during the call, asking insightful questions, and offering relevant solutions to build rapport and trust with the customer.
Sales calls are high-stakes moments that demand preparation, confidence, and adaptability. Whether you’re connecting with a prospect for the first time or working to close a deal, having the right information at the right moment can make all the difference.
That’s where Glean comes in. With AI-powered search, instant access to company knowledge, and real-time insights, Glean ensures you walk into
every sales call
fully prepared.
Improve sales call prep with a 360° view of your prospect
Understanding your prospect before the call is critical. With Glean’s
advanced search
capabilities, you can:
Pull insights
from Salesforce, past conversations, support tickets, and internal documentation in seconds.
Combine
internal data with external research
to surface company updates, industry news, and competitive insights.
Identify key decision-makers
and past interactions to tailor your pitch.
Instead of walking in blind, you enter the conversation armed with the knowledge to build trust and credibility from the start.
Answer questions on the spot with AI sales research
Sales calls often come with unexpected questions — technical details, pricing comparisons, or security concerns. Instead of scrambling to find answers after the call, Glean enables you to:
Instantly search past answers
from Slack discussions, emails, knowledge bases, and company wikis.
Use real-time AI assistance to
surface relevant documentation
mid-conversation.
Reduce follow-up emails
by providing accurate responses immediately.
No more “I’ll get back to you on that.” With Glean, you can respond confidently and keep the momentum going.
Stay ahead with these sales call tips
Understanding your competitors is key to winning deals. Glean helps by:
Fetching the latest
battle cards, deal strategies, and competitor comparisons
from your internal resources.
Highlighting key differentiators
so you can position your product effectively.
Keeping you
updated on industry trends
so your messaging stays relevant.
With these insights at your fingertips, you can handle objections with confidence and emphasize your unique value proposition.
Collaborate with the right experts instantly
Sometimes, preparing for a sales call requires input from other teams — whether it’s product, engineering, or customer success. Instead of tracking down the right person manually, Glean helps you:
Identify subject matter experts
within your company based on their past contributions.
Access relevant materials
and past conversations for additional context.
Bring the right people
into the conversation before the call.
This seamless collaboration ensures you’re equipped with the best insights and strategies for every deal.
Ace your post-call follow-ups
The work doesn’t stop when the call ends. Glean helps you stay organized and follow up effectively by:
Automatically summarizing
key discussion points using AI-powered insights.
Organizing
meeting notes, action items, and next steps
in one place.
Suggesting
relevant documentation to send to the prospect
, keeping the momentum going.
With structured follow-ups, you can close deals faster and ensure no action items slip through the cracks.
Conclusion
The best sales teams don’t just rely on instinct — they leverage data and AI-driven insights to stay ahead. Success in sales isn’t just about showing up; it’s about showing up prepared, confident, and ready to engage.
With Glean, every sales call becomes an opportunity to build trust, handle objections, and move deals forward. The right information at the right time isn’t just an advantage — it’s the key to closing more deals, faster.

# Building a new enterprise AI services company with Blackstone, Hellman & Friedman, and Goldman Sachs

**Source**: Anthropic Blog, May 4, 2026
**URL**: https://www.anthropic.com/news/enterprise-ai-services-company

Anthropic, Blackstone, Hellman & Friedman, and Goldman Sachs announced the formation of a new AI services company. The organization will work with mid-sized companies across sectors to bring Claude into their most important operations. Applied AI engineers from Anthropic will work alongside the firm's engineering team.

The new company is backed by a consortium including General Atlantic, Leonard Green, Apollo Global Management, GIC, and Sequoia Capital.

## Why

"Enterprise demand for Claude is significantly outpacing any single delivery model. Our partnerships with the world's leading systems integrators are central to how Claude reaches large enterprises. This new firm brings additional operating capability to the ecosystem and capital from leading alternative asset managers." — Krishna Rao, CFO of Anthropic.

## What the work looks like

A typical engagement: small team works with customer to identify highest-impact areas → Anthropic Applied AI engineers + JV engineers develop Claude-powered systems → long-term support.

Example: Multi-site healthcare services group — clinicians spend hours on documentation and compliance. Engineers build tools that fit existing workflows. Clinicians focus more on patient care.

## Claude Partner Network

The new company joins the Claude Partner Network alongside Accenture, Deloitte, PwC, and other consulting/SI firms.

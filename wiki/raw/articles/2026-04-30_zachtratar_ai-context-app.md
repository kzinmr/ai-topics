# AI Context & Data Apps

> You shouldn't have to have a "meeting notes app."
> 
> You should have an "AI context & data app" that happens to have great meeting notes.
> 
> Don't overpay for things.

## Context

Zach Tratar is AI Lead at Notion, following the January 2026 acquisition of his company Embra. Embra built a "Graph Context Engine" and "Business Memory Engine" — an AI platform that ingested organizational data to power contextual automation. Tratar's post argues against vertical SaaS fragmentation (separate apps for each function) in favor of unified AI context platforms.

Work Securely From Anywhere With :Harvey: Mobile
Legal teams can get up to speed, capture new information in real time, and keep work moving from anywhere with Harvey Mobile.
by
Harvey Team
•
Mar 3, 2026
Watch Video
Legal work doesn’t always happen at a desk. Partners, senior lawyers, and professionals are often meeting clients, traveling, and making critical decisions on the go.
In parallel, AI availability hasn’t kept pace with where legal work actually happens. In an
independent study
we commissioned of 200 legal professionals, 86% reported relying on a smartphone or tablet as their primary work device when away from their desk. Yet only 20% use AI on mobile, despite regularly using AI on their desktops.
This presents a missed opportunity for
legal AI tools
to help legal teams during time-sensitive, high-impact moments: preparing for calls on your commute, capturing and analyzing new data in meetings, and sending out time sensitive responses before a flight. Harvey Mobile is designed to change this, bringing legal-grade AI to wherever your work takes you.
Access Complete Context in Your Pocket
Imagine you’re a partner preparing for a high-stakes negotiation and have 20 minutes to get up to speed. With Harvey Mobile you can review past meeting summaries, use hands-free dictation to search Vault for relevant documents, and tap into trusted legal knowledge sources for quick Q&A all while in transit.
Designed for speed and simplicity, the app’s hands-free features and fast load times help you access key context with ease. As you work, your query history syncs seamlessly across devices, creating a continuous context loop between desktop and mobile so you can always pick up where you left off.
“
Legal questions don’t just come up at your desk — they come up on the train, in between meetings, or at the end of the day. The Harvey Mobile app makes it easy to run quick queries and validate ideas without my computer in front of me. The experience feels very familiar if you’ve used the web version, so there’s no learning curve to adapt and for time-sensitive tasks, it’s been a powerful companion to my legal workflow.
”
Dr. Frederic Geber
Partner at pswp
Keep Work Moving Forward
Some of the most valuable information in a case or deal never makes it into a formal document. Harvey Mobile introduces two mobile-only features designed to help change that:
Scan and analyze documents:
Take a photo of an agreement or other document and attach it to a query for immediate analysis or save to Vault.
Generate a transcript:
Transcribe a multi-party call or live meeting and save it to Vault for later reference and follow-up analysis.
For litigators moving between hearings, deal teams navigating negotiations, and in-house teams moving between meetings, these tools enable you to synthesize information in real-time rather than deferring analysis and risking the loss of critical details.
“
When decisions need to be made quickly, access to context is everything. Harvey’s Mobile app allows us to stay connected to our matters with the same quality, security, and reliability we expect at the desk. Being able to review prior work, surface relevant documents, and get immediate support on the go has materially improved how we respond to clients and keep work moving.
”
Adam Robertson
Shareholder at Andrews Myers
Adopt Without Friction

# Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite GA

Google announced general availability of Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite on May 8, 2026 — the fastest and most cost-efficient Gemini 3 series model.

## Positioning
- Ultra-low latency, high-volume tasks
- Joins Pro and Flash models for "the exact combination of intelligence, speed, and cost"
- Precision for agentic tasks (tool calling, orchestration) with cost-efficiency for automated pipelines at scale

## Enterprise Adoption
- **JetBrains**: Transformed IDE AI assistant & Junie agent responsiveness. "The balance of high intelligence and minimal latency makes it the perfect model for real-time developer support." — Vladislav Tankov, Director of AI
- **Gladly**: Customer service AI agent runs on Flash-Lite. ~60% lower costs than comparable thinking-tier models. P95 latency ~1.8s for full reply generation, sub-second p95 for classifiers/tool calls, ~99.6% success rate under heavy concurrent load.
- **Astrocade**: Game creation platform using Flash-Lite for multimodal safety checks, inline comment translation, and asset generation prompt refinement.
- **krea.ai**: Uses Flash-Lite as prompt enhancer in Nodes tool — "weirdly creative" output for its price point.
- **Ramp**: Financial operations platform using Flash-Lite for high-volume, latency-sensitive workflows.

## Context
Flash-Lite was first released March 3, 2026, but May 8 marks GA availability on the Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform.

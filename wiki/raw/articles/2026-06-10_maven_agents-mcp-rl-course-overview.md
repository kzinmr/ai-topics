# Production-Ready Agent Engineering: From MCP to RL

**Platform:** [Maven](https://maven.com/will-brown-kyle-corbitt/agents-mcp-rl)
**Instructors:** Will Brown (Research Lead, Prime Intellect) & Kyle Corbitt (CTO, OpenPipe)
**Schedule:** June 17 – July 3, 2026 (Tuesdays & Thursdays, 5:00–6:30 PM EST)
**Time Commitment:** 4–6 hours per week (lectures + projects)
**Price:** $1,400
**Tagline:** "Gain the key skills for designing effective agents and optimizing their performance. Dive deep into evaluations, tools, MCP, and RL."

## Course Description

A live, cohort-based training course covering the full stack of production agent engineering — from tool design via MCP to reinforcement learning optimization. Aimed at engineers, data scientists, and technical leaders building agentic AI systems.

## What You'll Learn

1. **Agent Design Patterns** — Understand key concepts and patterns underlying modern LLM agents, and how to choose the right approach for your use case
2. **MCP Tool Building** — Build portable, reliable tools for your agents and data using Model Context Protocol (MCP)
3. **Research Agents** — Implement your own research agents, incorporating custom format instructions and data access
4. **RL Fundamentals** — Learn the fundamentals of Reinforcement Learning (RL) and how it applies to agents
5. **Agentic RL Formulation** — Formulate your agentic tasks as RL problems, with evaluation metrics that enable learning from reward feedback
6. **GRPO Training** — Use RL algorithms like Group-Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO) to train agents which outperform frontier models on your tasks
7. **Holistic Integration** — A holistic understanding of modern principles and techniques for designing production-ready agents and optimizing them with RL

## Target Audience

- Senior SWEs turned AI Engineers at SaaS companies seeking to replace brittle pipelines with highly-optimized agents
- Founders & CTOs of startups wanting best-in-class agentic AI experiences
- Technical Directors at Fortune 500 companies evaluating approaches and vendors for agentic AI solutions

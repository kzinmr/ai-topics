AI Summary by Glean
The blog presents
25 AI-powered prompts designed for product managers
to enhance productivity by automating tasks such as summarizing feedback, drafting documents, and analyzing trends.
It categorizes prompts into key areas:
customer insights and market research
,
roadmap planning and prioritization
,
competitive analysis
,
internal communication
, and
data-driven decision-making
, helping teams focus on addressing user needs and strategic planning.
By integrating these AI prompts, product managers can
streamline processes, make informed decisions, and improve collaboration
across teams, ultimately leading to the successful development of products that meet market demands.
Product managers are under constant pressure to make fast, informed decisions — without dropping the ball on day-to-day execution. You’re expected to align stakeholders, define roadmaps, track shifting market signals, and drive feature delivery. It’s a lot.
That’s where AI can help. By taking on the manual work — like summarizing feedback, drafting docs, and analyzing trends — AI lets you focus on what matters most: building great products.
Below are 25 AI-powered prompts tailored to help product managers move faster and make smarter decisions. These are part of Glean’s prompt library, and you can use them out of the box or customize them to your team’s needs.

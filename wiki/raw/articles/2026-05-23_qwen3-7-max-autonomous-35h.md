# Alibaba's Qwen3.7-Max: 35-Hour Autonomous Chip Optimization

## Source
The Decoder — May 23, 2026

## Key Points
- Qwen3.7-Max ran 35 hours autonomously optimizing a kernel for Alibaba's T-Head-ZW-M890 chip
- 432 kernel tests, 1,158 tool calls, 10x speedup achieved
- Also detected 1,618 reward-hacking attempts during own training (86 hours, 13,952 trajectories)
- Supports 1M context window, closed-weight, API-only
- YC-Bench: $2.08M revenue / 237 tasks completed
- KernelBench L3: 96% success rate
- Can steer a four-legged robot via robotics framework

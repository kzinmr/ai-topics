# Experience is Now a Tax

**Bookmarks:** 5,792 | **Impressions:** 2.83M

## Key Thesis

The title suggests that traditional experience/seniority in software engineering and other knowledge work is becoming a liability rather than an asset in the AI era. Experienced workers may be more resistant to AI adoption, locked into legacy patterns, or over-optimizing for approaches that AI has made obsolete.

## Themes (inferred)
- How AI changes the value of experience in knowledge work
- Why junior/novice approaches may be more adaptable to AI-augmented workflows
- The "tax" of unlearning old patterns vs starting fresh with AI-native approaches

## Significance
High engagement (5,792 bookmarks, 2.83M impressions) indicates this resonates with current debates about AI's impact on professional skill valuation.

I'll Rust With You: the song
Niko Matsakis, co-lead for the Rust language team, recently published a song titled
"Edition: the
song"
. I decided I was not to be outdone and sang a cover of "I'll Rust With You", by
Steam Powered Giraffe
. The song has absolutely no relation to the Rust language other than
the title, I just think it's fun.

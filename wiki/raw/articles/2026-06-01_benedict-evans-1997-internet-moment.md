# Benedict Evans on Lenny's Podcast

Benedict Evans (former a16z partner, independent analyst) discusses AI as a "1997 internet moment" — early, exciting, deeply uncertain.

Key framings:
- AI as "1997 internet moment" — we know it's big but can't see the shape
- Where value accrues in the AI stack
- Anti-AI backlash and where it may lead
- Consulting/services boom at AI companies
- Distribution as ultimate moat as software gets easier to build
- "Task vs job" framing — AI automates tasks, not jobs
- Jevons paradox applied to AI labor

# US Officials Weigh Drastic Cuts to Cyber Patching Deadlines (May 2026)

Source: Reuters Exclusive, Raphael Satter, May 1 2026

## Proposal
- CISA considering slashing KEV catalog patching deadline from **2-3 weeks** to **3 days**
- Driven by AI models that compress exploit window from months/weeks to **hours**
- Key figures: Nick Andersen (Acting CISA Chief), Sean Cairncross (National Cyber Director)

## AI Models Cited
- **Anthropic's Mythos** — primary concern for boosting hacker efficiency
- **OpenAI's GPT-5.4-Cyber** — identifying unknown vulnerabilities, seizing on freshly disclosed flaws

## Industry Impact
- Banking industry already "scrambling" to adapt
- CISA weakened by job cuts and government shutdowns
- Experts warn 3 days is "simply impossible for some environments" (Kecia Hoyt, Flashpoint)

## Context
- Hackers have used AI since at least 2023, but 2026 frontier models are a qualitative leap
- EU seeking access to Anthropic's Mythos to understand risks (Bundesbank)
- China launching campaigns against AI misuse

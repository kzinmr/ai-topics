# Meta Muse Spark Launch

**April 8, 2026**

## Key Points

- First model from **Meta Superintelligence Labs (MSL)**, led by **Alexandr Wang** (Scale AI founder)
- Internally codenamed "Avocado", built over 9 months from ground-up AI stack rebuild
- **Closed-source** (breaking Llama open-source tradition); open weights planned, no timeline
- Natively multimodal reasoning with visual chain of thought, tool-use, multi-agent orchestration
- Three reasoning modes: Instant, Thinking, Contemplating (gradual rollout)
- 10x less compute than Llama 4 for comparable results
- **Benchmarks**: 52 on AI Index v4.0 (top 5), 86.4 on CharXiv Reasoning (#1), #2 Finance Agent v1.1, #3 Vals Index
- Free via meta.ai and Meta AI app; private API preview for select partners
- Rolling out to WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, Messenger, smart glasses

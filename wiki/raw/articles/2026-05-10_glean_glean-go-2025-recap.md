AI Summary by Glean
Glean:GO 2025 marked a pivotal event for the enterprise AI community, featuring over 40 new product launches, hands-on labs, and practical strategies to help organizations move from isolated AI pilots to scaled, real-world adoption across teams
The event showcased impactful real-world customer stories, deepened ecosystem partnerships, and introduced new platform capabilities such as Glean Agents, a no-code agent builder, advanced security features, and a model hub supporting 15+ leading LLMs
Attendees engaged directly with Glean’s technologies, collaborated in hands-on sessions, and joined the launch of the Gleaniverse Community—leaving inspired to accelerate both their own companies and the broader Work AI ecosystem
Inside Glean:GO 2025: new launches, real stories, bold ideas
Glean:GO 2025
marked a defining moment for Glean and the enterprise AI community. Over 580 attendees joined us in person in San Francisco with more than 10,000 virtual viewers for a day of product launches, hands-on labs, thought leadership, and real-world customer stories from the frontlines of Work AI.
The purpose behind Glean:GO
This inaugural event was designed to make AI at work real. We brought together business leaders, operators, and innovators to put agents to work, demystify enterprise AI deployment, and share what it takes to shift organizations from isolated AI pilots to real, scaled AI adoption.
Glean:GO offered practical strategies for every attendee to bring back to their teams — whether they were executives leading AI transformation, admins driving enablement, power users building on the frontline, or newcomers just beginning their journey.
The next chapter of Work AI
We rolled out 40+ new features across every layer of the platform designed to help teams scale AI, securely and effectively.
From deeper partnerships and more flexible infrastructure to powerful new agent capabilities and built-in guardrails, this release makes it easier to build, trust, and deploy agents across the enterprise.

Glean:GO wasn’t just about making announcements — it was about putting our latest products and features into attendees’ hands. From hands-on labs to 1:1 time with engineers, every part of the day was designed to help attendees build, experiment, and learn.

Looking ahead — this is just the beginning
Glean:GO showed what’s possible when builders, business leaders, and customers come together with a shared goal: to put AI to work.
Every attendee — whether a customer, partner, prospect, or Gleaner — left with new ideas, new tools, and new momentum. And when that happens, you don’t just accelerate your company — you accelerate the whole industry.

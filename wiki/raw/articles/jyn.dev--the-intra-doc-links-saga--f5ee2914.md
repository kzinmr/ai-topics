# The intra-doc links saga

Source: https://jyn.dev/the-intra-doc-links-saga/

One of my projects for the last 4 months or so has been working on
'intra-doc links'
, a feature of
rustdoc
that lets you link to items by name. That feature will be stable in 4 weeks in
Rust 1.48.0!
@Manishearth
and I wrote a longer blog post about intra-doc links which you
can read on
the official Rust blog
.

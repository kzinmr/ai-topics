:Harvey: Raises Growth Round at $11 Billion Valuation Co-led by GIC and Sequoia
by
Harvey Team
•
Mar 25, 2026
AI agents are rapidly changing legal work as they take on high-volume, increasingly complex tasks and run workflows from start to finish, freeing up lawyers to focus on higher-value work. Leading law firms including Ashurst, Baker Donelson, Cuatrecasas, and GSK Stockmann are among those deploying agents across their practices.
Today we’re
announcing
that we’ve raised $200M at an $11 billion valuation. The funding will be used to expand the
more than 25,000 custom agents
our customers run on Harvey and to scale the embedded legal engineering teams that help build and optimize those agents inside the world’s top law firms and legal departments.
The round is co-led by GIC and returning investor Sequoia. It includes participation from existing investors Andreessen Horowitz, Coatue, Conviction Partners, Elad Gil, Evantic, and Kleiner Perkins. We’re deeply grateful for the continued support of our investors and customers.
We’re just getting started. If you’d like to join us, we’re
always hiring

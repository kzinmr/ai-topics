AI Summary by Glean
The Glean Partner Portal serves as a centralized hub, offering partners unified access to essential resources, tools, training, incentives, deal registration, technical support, and co-marketing opportunities, all designed to streamline and empower partnership activities.
Key features focus on accelerating partner enablement and success by providing structured onboarding, technical certifications, transparent deal processes, market development support, and seamless integration tools—all to reduce friction and maximize efficiency for partners.
The portal reflects Glean’s commitment to elevating collaborations, removing barriers to market entry, and fostering joint growth through real-time updates, intuitive navigation, and dedicated account management.
Today, we launched the Glean Partner Portal—a centralized hub that delivers the full power of our partner ecosystem in one sleek, accessible platform. Built with our partners in mind—whether you're a Cloud Provider, Value‑Added Reseller, Consulting Partner, or Technology Product Innovator—this portal brings tools, resources, and support into a unified, seamless experience. From incentives and training to deal registration and marketing alignments, everything is now at your fingertips, all in one place.
What’s in the portal?
Every feature in the Partner Portal is focused on helping our partners get what they need as quickly and efficiently as possible, enabling them to capture more opportunities:
Comprehensive training & enablement
—Build expertise in Glean’s AI-powered solutions through structured learning paths, regular product updates, and technical certifications to accelerate onboarding as well as time-to-market.
Market development funds (MDF) & co-marketing
—Access funding and campaign resources to amplify reach, drive demand, and create new customer conversations. Align with Glean’s go-to-market teams for coordinated execution, shared pipeline goals, and impactful field activities.
Deal registration & partner protection
—Secure your opportunities with confidence through a transparent, automated deal registration process.
Technical resources & integration support
—Access best-practice guides, solution blueprints, and technical support to ensure smooth deployments and integrations.
The portal’s intuitive navigation, real-time updates, and dedicated account management should keep your partnership running smoother than ever.
Enabling partnerships to new heights
Our Partner Portal is a commitment to our partners—a single-source resource that removes the friction of getting to market and leveraging new opportunities. It’s taking our collaboration with our partners to new heights by accelerating the process behind joint success.
The Glean Partner Portal is already live—if you’re a Glean partner, simply log in and explore the wealth of tools, resources, and opportunities waiting for you. If you’re not yet part of our ecosystem, now’s the perfect time to join and see what’s possible.
Visit
partners.glean.com
to log in or learn more!

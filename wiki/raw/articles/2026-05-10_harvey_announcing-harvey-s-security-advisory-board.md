Announcing :Harvey:'s Security Advisory Board
Announcing the formation of our Security Advisory Board, consisting of information security representatives from both the institutions that Harvey engages with and the infrastructure that Harvey operates on.
by
Gabe Pereyra
and
Winston Weinberg
•
Aug 2, 2023
We are excited to announce the formation of our
Security Advisory Board
. The Board consists of information security representatives from both the institutions that Harvey engages with and the infrastructure that Harvey operates on.
Their expertise will build on Harvey's existing, best-in-class security practices. These include comprehensive security certifications, as well as infrastructure and sub-processor relationships developed with sensitive client information front of mind.
In particular, they will advise Harvey's leadership team in defining security best practices for developing generative AI systems that process highly-sensitive data. As the development of these systems redefines the role of technology in knowledge work, data security practices must evolve as well. Harvey is committed to ensuring our partners can maximize the impact of AI in their workflows while being confident that their proprietary data, and that of their clients, is processed, stored, and leveraged by our models securely and solely for their benefit.
Modern generative AI has the potential to fundamentally change knowledge work. Fully unlocking that potential requires developing systems that are uniquely integrated with a firm's data and processes. Our Security Board enables us to proactively develop best practices for this paradigm shift, and we are honored to work with them as we continue to develop the future of knowledge work.

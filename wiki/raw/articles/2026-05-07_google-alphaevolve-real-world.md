# AlphaEvolve, 1 year later: Impact on science, technology

**Google Cloud Blog** | May 7, 2026

One year after its initial announcement, Google AlphaEvolve has moved from research into solving real-world problems across science and technology domains.

## What is AlphaEvolve?

AlphaEvolve is Google's AI system for evolutionary optimization — using AI to discover better solutions to complex design and optimization problems. It applies evolutionary algorithms powered by large-scale AI to search through vast design spaces.

## Real-World Applications (as of May 2026)

The blog details AlphaEvolve's transition from pure research to practical deployment:
- **Scientific discovery**: Applied to materials science, drug discovery, and protein design
- **Infrastructure optimization**: Chip design, data center cooling, network topology
- **Industrial engineering**: Manufacturing process optimization
- **Cloud deployment**: Available through Google Cloud for enterprise customers

## One-Year Impact

- Multiple peer-reviewed publications demonstrating real-world impact
- Integration with Google Cloud's AI platform for enterprise access
- Demonstrated cost savings and efficiency improvements across partner deployments

## Summary

OpenAI published a policy proposal titled "A blueprint for democratic governance of frontier AI" on June 4, 2026. This was released alongside a broader "OpenAI public policy agenda" document on the same day.

The blueprint proposes a governance framework for frontier AI development, focusing on:

1. **Center for AI Standards and Innovation (CAISI)** — Proposes expanding the power and role of CAISI in AI governance
2. **Mandatory evaluations** — Requires mandatory safety evaluations for "frontier" AI technologies before deployment
3. **Democratic oversight** — Advocates for democratic governance mechanisms for frontier AI systems

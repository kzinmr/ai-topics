# Ring-2.6-1T: InclusionAI's Agent-Centric Thinking Model

**Released**: May 8, 2026
**Provider**: InclusionAI
**Availability**: Free on OpenRouter

Ring-2.6-1T is a **1T-parameter-scale thinking model with 63B active parameters** (Mixture of Experts), built for real-world agent workflows that require both strong capability and operational efficiency.

## Design Philosophy

Optimized for **coding agents, tool use, and long-horizon task execution**. Delivers leading results on agent-centric benchmarks:
- PinchBench
- ClawEval
- TAU2-Bench
- GAIA2-search

## Adaptive Reasoning

With adaptive reasoning effort across **high** and **xhigh** modes, Ring-2.6-1T dynamically allocates reasoning budget based on task complexity. This enables stronger performance with lower token overhead, especially in tool-heavy and multi-turn agent workflows.

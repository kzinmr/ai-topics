:Harvey: Expands Global Data Coverage
Harvey expands its legal research coverage with new knowledge sources across Europe and Asia.
by
Harvey Team
•
Jul 22, 2025
Today, Harvey serves over 515 customers across more than 54 countries. As part of our commitment to serving our global customer base, we aim to make the sources of primary law our customers rely on searchable, translatable, and citable in one, unified interface.
We’re excited to announce the integration of eight new national sources: Austria, Finland, Germany, India, the Netherlands, Norway, Spain, and Switzerland. These additions significantly expand our coverage in Europe and Asia and build on the existing sources users can already access through Harvey.
Watch Video
Demo of Spanish knowledge source being used in Harvey
This expansion underscores our commitment to legal professionals around the world. With these new key sources—alongside existing coverage of Australia, EDGAR, Eur-Lex, Singapore, and Sweden—Harvey makes cross-jurisdictional research faster and more seamless. Whether you're navigating the latest Austrian legislation, reviewing a Finnish court’s reasoning, or comparing the laws of two of Spain's autonomous communities, Harvey brings all the right sources together for use in Assistant.
These new additions are part of our ongoing effort to expand Harvey’s legal knowledge coverage. We’re committed to serving legal professionals everywhere and are actively working to bring even more sources into the platform.

Decagon is now available on
AWS Marketplace
, making it easier for AWS customers to deploy AI agents that deliver concierge-level customer experiences.
For enterprises already running on AWS, that means faster procurement and the ability to apply existing AWS committed spend toward Decagon.
Why AWS and Decagon?
AWS runs a large share of the world's enterprise infrastructure, and bringing Decagon to AWS Marketplace puts the leading conversational AI platform for customer experience inside the procurement workflows, security, and infrastructure teams already have set up.
A few things this unlocks:
Streamlined procurement.
Businesses on AWS Enterprise Agreements, or those with existing committed spend, can apply that investment directly toward Decagon, which removes a lot of the friction that typically slows down AI deployments.
Faster time to value.
Decagon goes live in weeks with
Agent Operating Procedures
(AOPs), and purchasing through the Marketplace takes procurement out of that path entirely, so teams can move from decision to deployment faster.
A trusted, compliant foundation.
AWS customers already have data residency, compliance, and access controls configured for their environment. Deploying Decagon through the Marketplace means those same controls extend to your AI concierge from day one, rather than starting a security review from scratch.
Built for your existing infrastructure.
For teams running contact center operations on Amazon Connect, Decagon plugs in directly, bringing the platform's capabilities to any channel without requiring any infrastructure changes.
Part of a broader ecosystem
Our work with AWS is just one example of the ecosystem we're building. More broadly,
Decagon Partnerships
brings together technology, alliance, and sales and services partners as part of an ecosystem that's helping make concierge-level customer experience accessible to every company.
To support this community, we offer a Partner Essentials course through
Decagon University
, a training program to help partners build, deploy, and scale Decagon's AI concierge solutions.
Get started
Head to the
AWS Marketplace
listing to see deployment options, pricing, and how to apply your existing AWS spend commitments toward Decagon.
If you're interested in partnering or want to explore how Decagon can work within your AWS environment, reach out to our partnerships team at partnerships@decagon.ai.

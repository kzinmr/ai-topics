We are pleased to report that the Fireworks.ai inference platform is both SOC 2 Type II and HIPAA compliant. These important milestones enable our customers to leverage the Fireworks fast inference platform to confidently accelerate product innovation based on Generative AI.
SOC 2 Type II Compliant
Protecting customer data is the highest priority for Fireworks.ai. With increasing cybersecurity threats and data breaches, we at Fireworks.ai prioritize information security and the protection of our systems and data.
Achieving SOC 2 Type II compliance provides assurance that our customers' data is properly safeguarded and demonstrates our ongoing commitment to ensuring that the handling and processing of customers' data meet key security standards. By undergoing a SOC 2 Type II audit, our controls and processes were validated by a third-party who attests to the functioning of the controls relevant to our platform.
Penetration Tests
Fireworks.ai routinely undertakes security reviews by third-party experts and addresses the identified issues based on their criticality and prioritization.
Unlocking Generative AI for Healthcare and Life Sciences with HIPAA
HIPAA compliance ensures that healthcare and life science organizations can confidently leverage Fireworks.ai's rapid inference capabilities. With this compliance, we're better positioned to support our clients in the healthcare industry to help advance healthcare use cases powered through generative AI capabilities like question answering, large-scale data analysis, and personalized chatbots.
We partnered with Vanta & Advantage Partners to seamlessly guide us through the compliance process.
Why This Matters
In today's digital age, data breaches and security threats are ever-present. Achieving both SOC 2 Type II and HIPAA compliance is a testament to our proactive approach to safety and data security.
While these are exciting milestones, they're part of our ongoing journey to prioritize security, transparency, and the trust of our clients and unlock the power of generative AI to support innovative use cases such as automated patient conversations, large-scale medical analysis, and increasing healthcare accessibility with safer LLMs.
We're committed to continuous improvement and ensuring that our rapid inference platform remains at the forefront of utility, security, and compliance.

Preview: Access :Harvey:, Wherever You’re Working
Announcing iOS and Android mobile apps that will bring the power of Harvey with you, wherever you’re working.
by
Harvey Team
•
Sep 10, 2025
Watch Video
Legal work doesn’t just happen at your desk or on a single device. Think: answering urgent client questions from the courthouse, reviewing contracts during a layover, and collaborating with co-counsel across three different time zones. Lawyers are constantly on the move, switching between laptops, phones, and tablets to get work done.
One of our core product goals is to meet lawyers where they are and make AI-powered legal work seamless across platforms and contexts — and mobile is the next step in that journey. By extending Harvey to mobile, we’re unlocking new ways to work without boundaries
.
We’re introducing new
iOS and Android mobile apps
that will extend the power of Harvey across your preferred devices. You’ll be able to prompt Assistant by voice, scan and upload files, and revisit your matters anytime — making it easier to handle urgent questions or time-sensitive issues as they arise.
The Harvey mobile app will include a subset of core Harvey functionality as well as new, mobile-specific capabilities.
Here’s an overview of what you’ll be able to do:
Use Voice to Prompt for hands-free prompting and dictation.
Upload files by taking photos of documents right from your phone.
Access Assistant, including the “Improve” button for quick prompt writing.
View projects and documents in Vault.
Available Now: Voice to Prompt in Web
While the Harvey mobile app is in the works, you can now use Voice to Prompt in the web app. Simply speak prompts directly into the composer and Harvey will transcribe them into Assistant.
Harvey works where you are, empowering you to work without boundaries. Stay tuned for the launch of Harvey on iOS and Android in November. If you’re a current customer interested in joining our early access, reach out to your customer success team.

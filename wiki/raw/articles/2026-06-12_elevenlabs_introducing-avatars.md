Start creating with Avatars
Avatars is a new entry point in ElevenCreative for generating talking-head videos. It pairs ElevenLabs speech models with leading lip-syncing models in a single interface - so creators and marketers can go from script to finished video without switching platforms.
One interface for voice and face
Previously, generating a lip-synced video in ElevenCreative required navigating to Image & Video, selecting the Video tab, opening the model picker, filtering by Lipsync, and choosing a provider. Avatars replaces that with a single tab: select your avatar, write your script, pick a voice, and generate.
Text to Speech is built directly into the prompt island so that the voice and the lip-synced video are produced together. There is no separate audio export step and no file handoff between platforms. Because ElevenLabs owns the speech layer, the voice model and the lip-syncing model run in the same environment - producing tighter synchronization than workflows that rely on external audio imports.
Create and reuse persistent identities
An avatar is a persistent visual identity built from reference images or a text prompt. Upload photos of a person, character, or animal, and ElevenCreative generates a base identity you can reuse across any number of videos. Avatars can be human or non-human, and each comes with a pre-selected default voice that you can change at any time.
Generate styles to create variations - different camera angles, outfits, and backgrounds - while maintaining the same underlying identity. The avatars you create or favorite live in your Assets and can be referenced in any prompt box or dragged directly into a generation.
This is designed for creators and marketers who need visual consistency across campaigns and content. A course creator can produce an entire video series with the same on-screen instructor. A brand can maintain a consistent spokesperson across every market and language. A social creator can scale their personal presence without filming every post.

Use avatars with video models
Avatars integrate with the video generation models available in ElevenCreative. Source images act as a character sheet so that the model maintains the same identity across scenes within a video. This lets creators build longer-form content with consistent characters rather than generating isolated clips.
Scale with Flows
A new Avatar node in Flows connects avatar video generation into automated pipelines. Build a flow that takes a product brief, generates a script, creates a voiceover, and produces an avatar-led video - then batch-execute it across products, languages, and hooks.
The applications range from performance marketing teams generating dozens of ad variants from a single pipeline, to education companies producing course content across subjects and languages, to product teams creating localized explainer videos for every market they serve.
Browse the avatar library
ElevenCreative includes a curated library of avatars and styles ready to use. Filter by age, gender, or use case, or browse the full collection. Each avatar supports multiple styles for different visual contexts.

Double-Booked? Never Heard of It: Meet Calendar Mirroring in Fantastical!
We’ve all played the dangerous game of calendar roulette.
You try to copy the “Dentist Appointment” from your personal calendar to your work one, miss it by an hour, and boom—now you’re supposed to be getting a root canal and presenting the Q3 marketing strategy at the exact same time.
Awkward. 😬
Don’t panic! That’s a thing of the past with Calendar Mirroring, which automatically keeps your calendars perfectly in sync.
The Magic Behind the Mirror
🪞
Calendar Mirroring allows you to connect two separate calendars (like work and personal) so that events from one automatically show up on the other.
The best part?
No event information is sent to Flexibits servers or saved outside of your device
.
You can choose to show full event details or just block the time out as a mysterious, professional
“Busy”.
Your coworkers don’t need to know you’re getting a root canal, they just need to know you’re unavailable.
How it Works:
⚡
Instant One-Way Sync:
Move an event on Calendar A, and it dances over to Calendar B in real-time.
🛡️
Privacy Guard:
Keep personal details personal.
Ready to Get Started?
Setting up your first mirrored calendar takes less than 2 minutes!
Open Fantastical > Settings and select Mirroring
Choose your Source Calendar (
where the events will be coming from
) and your Destination Calendar (
where they will be going
)
Set your Mirrored Range.
Fantastical always looks 2 weeks in the past and then the amount of time that is set for this option in the future
Choose which events to include
Choose the event details that are mirrored
Add the rule, then sit back and watch the magic happen
Rules are synced across devices, giving you automated peace of mind.

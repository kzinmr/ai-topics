# IBM Think 2026 — AI Operating Model

At Think 2026 (Boston, May 5, 2026), IBM announced its most comprehensive enterprise AI expansion:

## Four-Pillar AI Operating Model
Arvind Krishna (CEO): "The enterprises pulling ahead are not deploying more AI – they're redesigning how their business operates."

1. **Agents** — Coordinated AI that executes and adapts across the business
2. **Data** — Real-time, connected information for shared situational awareness
3. **Automation** — End-to-end infrastructure and automated workflows
4. **Hybrid** — Operational independence for sovereignty, governance, security

## Key Products
- **watsonx Orchestrate** (next gen, private preview) — Agentic control plane for multi-agent era; deploy agents from any source with consistent policy and accountability
- **IBM Confluent** — Real-time data foundation for AI agents
- **IBM Concert** — Intelligent operations platform
- **IBM Sovereign Core** — Operational independence controls
- **IBM Bob** (GA) — Agentic development partner for enterprise developers

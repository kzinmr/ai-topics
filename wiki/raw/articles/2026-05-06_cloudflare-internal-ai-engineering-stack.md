# Cloudflare's Internal AI Engineering Stack — iMARS

**Source**: https://blog.cloudflare.com/internal-ai-engineering-stack/
**Date**: 2026-05-06

## Key Points

- iMARS tiger team: 93% of R&D org using AI-augmented workflow
- 3,683 internal users, 47.95M AI requests, 241.37B tokens via AI Gateway
- Merge requests increased from ~5,600/week to 10,900/week
- Single proxy Worker pattern for authentication, routing, ZDR enforcement
- Backstage knowledge graph: 2,055 services, 1,302 databases, 375 teams
- AGENTS.md in ~3,900 repos with AI Code Reviewer enforcement
- Workers AI (Kimi K2.5) handles ~15% of traffic, 77% cheaper than proprietary models

# MAI-Image-2.5 Launches at No. 3 on Arena

**Source**: https://microsoft.ai/news/mai-image-2-5-launches-at-no-3-on-arena-ai/
**Date**: May 26, 2026
**Author**: MAI Superintelligence Team, Microsoft

## Overview

MAI-Image-2.5 is Microsoft's strongest image generation model yet, ranked third on the Arena text-to-image leaderboard. It represents a step change in quality over MAI-Image-2.

## Key Capabilities
- Wide range of styles
- Close instruction following
- Reliable text rendering (major improvement)
- Detailed, coherent images
- Strong visual reasoning across objects, scene structure, lighting, scale, and spatial relationships

## Improvements Over MAI-Image-2
- Major improvements in text rendering
- Stylized illustration
- Commercial imagery
- Sharper text, stronger brand work
- Layouts hold together better
- More deliberate scene composition

## Availability
- Available on Arena at launch
- Coming to MAI Playground and Foundry within two weeks

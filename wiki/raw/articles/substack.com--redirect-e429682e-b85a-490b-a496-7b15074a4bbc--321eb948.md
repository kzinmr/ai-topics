# Notion 2.43: Suggested edits + new Notion AI features

Source: https://substack.com/redirect/e429682e-b85a-490b-a496-7b15074a4bbc?j=eyJ1IjoiMmZseDYifQ.6O_iC1_GWCzChnbrHzSrs1hMpJpoODTUJm_FAk3tf6E

Collaborating on docs used to be a pain, so we made it much easier to track changes! Now any collaborator can make suggestions, and you or a teammate can quickly accept or reject.
It can be hard to keep track of all the activity happening in your workspace. So we’ve grouped suggested edits and comments into easy-to-read threads. And as you jump from page to page to resolve comments, your new
Inbox
stays open to keep you focused.
One-click AI skills (no prompt required)
Using AI can feel hard—unless you know how to write a perfect prompt from scratch. These one-click skills make it much easier! Brainstorm ideas, make a diagram, summarize notes, or translate content—all with one click.
Notion AI can answer any question! It finds the right info from Notion, Slack (soon Google Drive & Github too), and models like GPT-4. No need to search a bunch of tools. And with a full-page window, you have a distraction-free place to chat.
Notion AI global shortcut
Now, if you’re designing in Figma, responding to a customer in Zendesk, or analyzing data in Hex, you can summon Notion AI to answer your questions right then and there. Use the new global shortcut (
⌘ + shift + J
for Mac,
ctrl + shift + J
for Windows) when you have our
desktop app
installed.
When your projects and docs are in the same tool, your team can stay focused on the real work (no more hunting for info between tools!). In the new-and-improved Asana importer, select which projects you’d like to bring over, and convert them into easy-to-use Notion databases.
The upgraded Jira Sync integration offers faster syncs, more customization and upgraded database functionality for your synced Jira Projects and Issues.
Notion Calendar is available as an Android app! Use it to manage multiple Google calendars, open linked Notion docs, and get in-app meeting notifications.

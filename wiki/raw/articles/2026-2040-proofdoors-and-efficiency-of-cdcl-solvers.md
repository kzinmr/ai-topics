# Proofdoors and Efficiency of CDCL Solvers

**Date:** March 2026
**Subject:** SAT Solvers, Computational Complexity

## Summary
We propose a new parameter called proofdoor in an attempt to explain the efficiency of CDCL SAT solvers over formulas derived from circuit (esp., arithmetic) verification applications.

## Key Findings
- CDCL solver efficiency analyzed through proofdoor parameter
- Focus on formulas from circuit and arithmetic verification
- New theoretical framework for understanding solver performance
- Applications to formal verification workflows

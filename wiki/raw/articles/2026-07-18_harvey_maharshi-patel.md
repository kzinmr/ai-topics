Maharshi Patel
Engineer at Harvey
Maharshi is an Engineer at Harvey, building AI Systems for Legal workflows. He was previously a Machine Learning Engineer at Airbnb, where he built LLM agents for payments support and chargeback disputes. Before that, he spent five years at Google working on Search ranking quality, applying the latest AI research at Search scale. He holds a BS in Computer Science from UC Irvine.

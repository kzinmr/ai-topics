# GLiClass: Generalist Lightweight Model for Sequence Classification Tasks

**Authors:** Ihor Stepanov, Mykhailo Shtopko, Dmytro Vodianytskyi, Oleksandr Lukashov, Alexander Yavorskyi, Mykyta Yaroshenko (Knowledgator Engineering)

**Key innovations:**
1. **Single-forward-pass classification** — unlike Cross-Encoders requiring N passes for N labels
2. **Multi-Label PPO** — first adaptation of Proximal Policy Optimization for multi-label text classification
3. **GLiNER architecture adaptation** — bi-encoder/tri-encoder logic for sequence-level classification

**Performance:** Matches or exceeds Cross-Encoder accuracy while being up to 50× faster. Maintains consistent speed regardless of label count.

**Limitations discussed:**
- Residual calibration differences across datasets
- Sensitivity under extreme label-text length ratios
- Variability on fine-grained taxonomies

**Future work:** Extending to multilingual and domain-specific settings.

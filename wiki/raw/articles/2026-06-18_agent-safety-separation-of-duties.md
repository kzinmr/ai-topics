Separation of duties. The person who writes the checks never reconciles the books. The pilot flies, a different human clears the landing. Auditors exist because no company gets to grade its own financials.

OpenAI shipped /goal in Codex in April. Anthropic followed in May with Claude Code 2.1.139. Two labs that agree on almost nothing converged on identical architecture within 30 days, because both hit the same failure in testing: an agent asked to verify its own work passes half-built stubs and calls them shipped.

The fix is structural, the same one accounting found. The worker model grinds through the task. A separate model reads the transcript and rules on one question: did the output meet the stated condition? The worker never gets a vote on its own completion.

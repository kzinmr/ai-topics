AI Summary by Glean
Glean’s mission has always been clear – expand human potential to do extraordinary work. We believe that every employee across all organizations can leverage AI daily to exponentially increase their impact. By harnessing AI that proactively surfaces relevant knowledge, automates routine tasks, and enables better decision-making, employees can focus on their most meaningful and impactful work - driving the best outcomes for their organizations.
We are pleased to be recognized once again as one of the
Emerging Leaders in the AI Knowledge Management Apps/General Productivity Emerging Market Quadrant in the 2025 Gartner Innovation Guide for Generative AI Technologies!
According to Gartner, “technology leaders can use this high-level guide and accompanying Emerging Market Quadrants to explore the vendor landscape.”
We believe this position reinforces our Work AI for All mission and demonstrates the growing desire for AI-powered tools and agents that don’t just retrieve information, but enable true task automation, robust data analysis, and the execution of complex, multi-step business processes.
In our opinion, Gartner has long been at the forefront of many market-moving moments, and the latest Innovation Guide for Generative AI Technologies and set of Emerging Market Quadrants across several generative AI categories is no different. We think, with the increasing rapid pace of this market, Gartner is helping organizations gain more clarity into the vendor landscape and offerings.
The Glean Work AI Platform
Glean is unique in its ability to bring the power of AI to the daily work of everyone in an organization, helping every employee not only find and understand knowledge, generate content, and automate tasks, but build and deploy intelligent agents tailored to their unique work needs.
Underpinned by an LLM-agnostic, advanced agentic reasoning engine, Glean’s platform turbo charges AI adoption with:
The best
search experience
on the market
The industry’s broadest access to data
A by-your-side
assistant
that summarizes, analyzes, creates, and executes work with you
The ability to create and deploy custom, horizontal AI agents securely and at scale across the organization
With state-of-the-art search and RAG technology, and built with the comprehensive data and AI governance, privacy, permissions enforcement, and answer referenceability that enterprises require, Glean delivers the powerful, secure, and trustworthy AI tools and agents that businesses today are seeking.
Customer Adoption is the Biggest Proof
This year, Glean achieved $100 million in ARR, a major milestone driven by growing demand for AI-powered workplace intelligence. More than doubling our customer base, we continue to support the world’s largest organizations across more than 50 industries, including leaders like Reddit, Instacart, Pinterest, Duolingo, Databricks, BILL, Deutsche Telekom, Grammarly, and many more.
In our opinion, recognition as one of the Emerging Leaders in the Innovation Guide for Generative AI Technologies reflects this momentum, with enterprises turning to AI to redefine how they work. Their eagerness to quickly and securely deploy generative AI tools like our Work AI Platform across their organizations is exciting!

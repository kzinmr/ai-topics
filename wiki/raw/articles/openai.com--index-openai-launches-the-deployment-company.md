# OpenAI launches the OpenAI Deployment Company

**Date**: May 11, 2026

OpenAI has agreed to acquire **Tomoro**, giving the OpenAI Deployment Company experienced Forward Deployed Engineers from day one.

OpenAI is launching the **OpenAI Deployment Company**, a new company designed to help organizations build and deploy AI systems they can rely on every day across their most important work.

The OpenAI Deployment Company will extend OpenAI's ability to embed engineers specialized in frontier AI deployment, known as **Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs)**, into organizations working on complex problems in demanding environments. These FDEs work closely with business leaders, operators, and frontline teams to identify where AI can make the biggest impact, redesign organizational infrastructure and critical workflows around it, and turn those gains into durable systems.

## Tomoro Acquisition

OpenAI has agreed to acquire **Tomoro**, an applied AI consulting and engineering firm that helps enterprises turn AI into operational advantage. The acquisition brings approximately **150 experienced Forward Deployed Engineers and Deployment Specialists** to the OpenAI Deployment Company from day one.

## Investment Structure

The OpenAI Deployment Company is a committed partnership between OpenAI and **19 leading global investment firms, consultancies, and system integrators**:

- **Lead**: TPG
- **Co-lead founding partners**: Advent, Bain Capital, Brookfield
- **Founding partners**: B Capital, BBVA, Emergence Capital, Goanna, Goldman Sachs, SoftBank Corp., Warburg Pincus, WCAS
- **Consulting/SI partners**: Bain & Company, Capgemini, McKinsey & Company

The Deployment Company is **majority-owned and controlled by OpenAI**, launching with more than **$4 billion of initial investment**, earmarked for scaling operations and acquiring firms.

## Key Themes

1. **Deployment as differentiator**: Building powerful AI models is only part of the work. Real impact comes from helping organizations use systems safely, effectively, and at scale.
2. **Forward-Deployed Engineers**: The Palantir playbook, now adopted by OpenAI — embedding engineers directly in customer operations.
3. **Capital-intensive services**: $4B+ investment suggests slim near-term margins but long-term moat through deep customer integration.
4. **Standalone business unit**: Allows independent operating model, pace, and customer focus while remaining closely connected to OpenAI research.

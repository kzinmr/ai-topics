# AI Made Us Faster. That Was the Problem

**Author**: David Fowler (@davidfowl), Distinguished Engineer at Microsoft, Aspire team

## Article Preview (from X status page)

Building production software with AI is not the same as building demos with AI.

That is one of the biggest lessons we are learning on the Aspire team. It is one thing to use AI to generate a prototype, automate a small task, or explore an idea. It is another thing to use AI inside a real product team, on a real codebase, with real customers, docs, tests, releases, and maintenance costs.

Aspire (aspire.dev) is a free, open-source platform for orchestrating your application stack from code: frontends, APIs, containers, databases, and cloud resources. It is local-first, production-ready, and agent-ready. Aspire is still young, roughly two and a half years old, but it has already lived through multiple eras of software development tooling.

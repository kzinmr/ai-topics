# Update on the People-First AI Fund

Completing our initial commitment with $9.5 million in grants—and committing an additional $50 million in 2026.

## Overview

In September 2025, the OpenAI Foundation launched the **$50 million People-First AI Fund** to support nonprofits and mission-driven organizations working at the intersection of innovation and public good.

Following an open call, the Foundation announced its first wave of grants in December 2025: **$40.5 million in unrestricted grants to 208 community-based organizations** across the United States.

Today's announcement: remaining second wave of **$9.5 million in grants** supporting organizations using AI to drive impact in areas reflecting people-first values.

The Foundation has committed another **$50 million** to the People-First AI Fund in 2026, with applications re-opening on **June 15, 2026**.

This is part of the broader commitment to invest at least **$1 billion** over the next year.

## Grant Recipients (Health Focus)

### Dollar For
- **Jared Walker, Founder & CEO**: "Our mission is to make hospital financial assistance known, easy, and fair. With AI, we are on a path to serve 9x more patients and eliminate $1 billion in medical debt."
- Helps low-income patients access medical bill forgiveness programs
- 47,000+ patients supported, $150M+ in medical debt eliminated

### Every Cure
- **Dr. David Fajgenbaum, Founder & CEO**: "Repurposing existing drugs is one of the fastest, most cost-efficient ways to get treatments to the people who need them."
- Uses AI to surface promising drug-disease matches

### CareMessage
- Largest patient engagement platform for low-income populations in the US
- Founded 2012, reached 22 million people across 45 states
- Delivers personalized health education via text messaging

### OCHIN
- Supports 2,200+ care delivery sites across 42 states
- Launching national AI Innovation Accelerator

### Lenfest Institute for Journalism
- Partnering with Axios Media to train journalists in responsible AI use
- Focus on underserved communities

## Scale

- Nearly **3,000 organizations** applied for the open call
- Applications re-open June 15, 2026

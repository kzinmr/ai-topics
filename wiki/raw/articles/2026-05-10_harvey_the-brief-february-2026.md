The Brief: February 2026
Welcome back to The Brief. This month, we're making Harvey more intelligent about how you work by surfacing the right tools at the right time, giving you more control over how you analyze and share documents, and expanding legal research sources. Whether you're drafting documents, reviewing hundreds of files, or refining custom workflows, Harvey now adapts better to your needs.

# Anthropic and OpenAI are both launching joint ventures for enterprise AI services

**Source**: TechCrunch, Russell Brandom, May 4, 2026
**URL**: https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/04/anthropic-and-openai-are-both-launching-joint-ventures-for-enterprise-ai-services/

On Monday, Anthropic announced a joint venture focusing on deploying enterprise AI services. Blackstone, Hellman & Friedman, and Goldman Sachs will be founding partners in the new venture, which is backed by a group of VCs, hedge funds, and private equity firms, including Apollo Global Management, General Atlantic, GIC, Leonard Green, and Sequoia Capital.

The Wall Street Journal reported the new venture was valued at $1.5 billion, which includes a $300 million commitment each from Anthropic, Blackstone, and Hellman & Friedman.

OpenAI is preparing a similar move with "The Deployment Company," raising roughly $4 billion from 19 investors including TPG, Bain Capital, and Brookfield Asset Management.

Both JVs are in talks to acquire engineering services and consulting firms to help businesses deploy AI. OpenAI's venture is in advanced stages on three deals.

This marks a new front in AI competition: the race is expanding from model development to model deployment.

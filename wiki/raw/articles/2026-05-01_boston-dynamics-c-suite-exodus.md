# C-suite Exodus at Boston Dynamics

Source: Semafor, Rachyl Jones, May 1 2026

## Departures
- **CEO Robert Playter** — retired Feb 2026 after 30+ years
- **COO** and **CSO** — also departed
- **CTO Aaron Saunders** — defected to Google DeepMind
- Additional robotics researchers and senior engineers left

## Context
- Hyundai (majority owner since 2021) wants "tens of thousands" of humanoids in car plants
- Board pushed out executives over narrowing lead against competitors
- Company making **4 Atlas humanoids/month** as of 2026
- Preparing IPO and new manufacturing facility
- Switching from prototype to production version of Atlas

## Quote from BD Spokesperson
"These changes are designed to help us prepare for the next chapter of Boston Dynamics, where we will need a structure that supports our ability to mass manufacture robots and rapidly drive scale in this emerging industry."

# Hebbia Acquires FlashDocs to Bring Generative Slide Creation to Finance

**Source**: [https://www.hebbia.com/blog/acquiring-flashdocs-streamlining-financial-workflows-with-ai-slide-deck](https://www.hebbia.com/blog/acquiring-flashdocs-streamlining-financial-workflows-with-ai-slide-deck)

Company
By George Sivulka
06.26.25
Hebbia Acquires FlashDocs to Bring Generative Slide Creation to Finance
Hebbia acquired FlashDocs, the leader in generative slide creation, so customers can turn AI-powered research and analysis directly into fully branded PowerPoint decks in seconds.
We’re thrilled to announce our acquisition of FlashDocs, the leading company in generative slide creation.
Adam Khakhar (CTO - FlashDocs), George Sivulka (CEO, Founder - Hebbia), Morten Bruun (CEO - FlashDocs)
With this acquisition, Hebbia becomes the leading AI platform for both information retrieval, agentic workflows, and now content generation, with the best AI slide creation capabilities on the market.
The new functionality enables Hebbia’s customers, including asset managers, investment banks, and professional services firms, to instantly turn powerful AI-powered insights into full slide decks in PowerPoint and Google Slides.
Slide Deck Generation was an Unsolved Problem in GenAI
For humans, even simple slide decks can take hours to build, while more complex ones typically require days of work.
While generative AI has greatly increased our ability to create text content, approaches to programmatically generate slides are limited. Without FlashDocs, generative AI solutions are (at best) limited. Creating decks requires teams to:
Wrestle with model generated XML and Google Slides APIs
Overhaul inconsistent branding and templates
Manipulate Python or JS libraries without export capabilities for PowerPoint or Google Slides
Manually copy and paste LLM content into slide decks
Even Andréj Karpathy, one of the world’s best AI researchers,
feels the pain
.
FlashDocs Unlocks State of the Art Slide Generation with GenAI
FlashDocs developed an industry leaning algorithm that turns prompts of unstructured and structured data into enterprise-quality presentations in seconds.
We’ve tested every solution on the market – they are the obvious leaders in the space. In less than a year of going to market, they’re already generating over 10,000+ AI created slides per day for enterprise use cases.
Using FlashDocs, companies convert markdown, JSON, or LLM output into fully branded slide decks instantly. Investors, bankers, lawyers, and consultants build the deck once, and export the asset everywhere. Some other key features of FlashDocs include:
Automation of tables, charts, images, and rich content
Easily upload custom templates and branding without any manual work
Markdown-to-Slides for LLMs & AI agent
One API for both PowerPoint and Google Slides
Hebbia + FlashDocs: Extending AI Workflow Automation
The acquisition of FlashDocs expands Hebbia’s technical edge beyond best-in-class information retrieval and agentic workflows, and into content generation. This reinforces Hebbia’s commitment to streamlining financial workflows end-to-end.
FlashDocs automates the final mile of AI workflows: turning structured LLM reasoning into client-ready decks. What once took hours of formatting and design, FlashDocs completes in seconds — fast, consistent, and fully on-brand.
With FlashDocs, Hebbia solves the problem of slide deck generation at the infrastructure level. FlashDocs combined with Hebbia provides the output layer that turns insight into business-ready deliverables.
More than anything, we’re incredibly excited to work with FlashDocs founding team, Morten Bruun and Adam Khakhar, who will spearhead the API business and artifact generation at Hebbia.
Together we are building the next generation of AI platforms.  Products that don’t just answer questions - they complete work.

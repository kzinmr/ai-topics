:Harvey: Opens in Singapore
by
Harvey Team
•
Jun 2, 2026
Today, we’re opening our Singapore office, the latest milestone in our commitment to customers across Asia Pacific.
Singapore plays a unique and growing role in the region's legal market. As Asia's leading hub for international arbitration, with a common law system and English-language courts, it is a natural bridge across jurisdictions. For firms and in-house teams handling work in M&A, capital markets, trade, technology, and finance, Singapore is a critical center for legal activity. As that work grows in complexity and volume, so does the demand for AI that can keep pace.
We entered the market with WongPartnership LLP as our first customer in Southeast Asia. Since then, our presence has expanded to include Rajah & Tann Singapore LLP, Drew & Napier LLC, Braddell Brothers LLP, Lee & Lee, Oon & Bazul LLP, TSMP Law Corporation, Attorney-General’s Chambers, Sreenivasan Chambers, organisations such as Airwallex, our alliance with PwC NewLaw and strategic partnership with
the Singapore Judiciary
.
As part of our investment in the region, we’re proud to
partner
with the National University of Singapore Faculty of Law, our first law school partnership in Singapore, to advance legal AI education and empower the next generation of legal talent. This partnership builds on our law school partnerships across APAC including the University of Sydney Law School and the University of Technology Sydney (UTS) Faculty of Law.
“Singapore’s role as a center for international business, legal services, and technology innovation makes it a natural fit for Harvey’s continued expansion in APAC,” said John Haddock, Chief Business Officer at Harvey. “We’re seeing growing demand from customers adopting AI at scale, and opening an office here strengthens our ability to partner with legal teams navigating increasingly complex and cross-border work.”
As AI adoption among APAC law firms and in-house teams moves from experimentation to everyday use, being on the ground matters. Our local team spans legal engineering, customer success, sales, and G&A. They will support implementation directly, deepen relationships, and help customers scale Harvey across their offices and practice groups.
Singapore joins Bengaluru and Sydney in our growing APAC presence. We are hiring across sales, legal engineering, and customer success.
If you’re interested in joining us, explore open roles on
our careers page

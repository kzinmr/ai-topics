Cameron MacGregor
Applied Legal Researcher
at
Harvey
Cameron MacGregor is on the Applied Legal Research team at Harvey. He was previously an Associate at Jones Day, where he represented clients in state and federal courts practicing complex commercial litigation across matters including breach of contract, trade secrets, products liability, and government investigations. Cam earned his J.D. from USC Gould School of Law, where he served in the Immigration Clinic, and his B.A. in Spanish Language and Literature from the University of Notre Dame.

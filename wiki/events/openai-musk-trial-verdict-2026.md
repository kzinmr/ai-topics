# OpenAI vs Musk Trial Verdict (May 2026)

## Overview

On May 18, 2026, a jury delivered a **unanimous verdict rejecting Elon Musk's claims against Sam Altman** in what was billed as "the AI trial of the century."

## Verdict Details

The jury agreed that **Musk was too late** — the statute of limitations had expired on his claims. Critically, the jury **never weighed in on the substantive question** of whether OpenAI's transformation from non-profit to for-profit structure was legitimate.

## Significance

As [[entities/gary-marcus|Gary Marcus]] noted:

> "We will never know what the world might be like had OpenAI been forced to fully follow its original mission."

The trial ended with a **procedural whimper rather than a bang**, leaving unresolved the fundamental tension between OpenAI's original non-profit mission and its current corporate structure.

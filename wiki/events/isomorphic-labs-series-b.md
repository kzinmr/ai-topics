# Isomorphic Labs $2.1B Series B

**Date**: May 20, 2026
**Company**: Isomorphic Labs
**Founder/CEO**: [[entities/demis-hassabis]] (also CEO of Google DeepMind)

## Funding Details

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Round** | Series B |
| **Amount** | $2.1B |
| **Lead Investor** | Thrive Capital |
| **Participants** | Alphabet, GV, Temasek, UK Sovereign AI Fund |
| **Use of Funds** | AI drug discovery R&D, clinical trials |

## Significance

Isomorphic Labs uses AI to discover and develop new drugs. The $2.1B Series B — one of the largest AI biotech rounds — signals strong investor confidence in AI-driven drug discovery:

- **Clinical milestone**: Targeting human clinical trials by end of 2026
- **AI approach**: Leverages DeepMind's protein folding (AlphaFold) and generative AI for molecular design
- **Market context**: Part of a wave of AI biotech funding as the industry moves from research to clinical validation
